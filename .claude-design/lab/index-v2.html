[21] Wordmark — Stencil Cut
The Stencil
Military stencil-style "GG" with horizontal cut lines through each letter. Rugged, utilitarian, road-worn. The kind of mark you'd spray on a crate.
[22] Icon + Wordmark — Graph
The Grid
Graph paper background with "GG" plotted on the grid. Data coordinates feel. The analytical backbone of the brand made visual. Axis labels in mono.
[23] Icon — Directional
The Compass
Angular compass rose with sharp diamond points. "GG" at center. N/S/E/W implied by the geometry. Navigate the gravel. Find your race.
[24] Wordmark — Rotated
The Vertical
Each letter stacked vertically: G-R-A-V-E-L on left, G-O-D on right (bold gold). Reads like a spine. Magazine editorial influence.
[25] Icon — Mechanical
The Chainring
Square chainring teeth pattern around a frame. "GG" at center. The drivetrain that powers everything. Sharp rectangular teeth — brutalist gears.
[26] Badge — Postal
The Stamp
Postmark/rubber stamp aesthetic. Double-line rectangular border with "GRAVEL GOD CYCLING" around the perimeter. Date stamp feel. Approved. Certified.
[27] Wordmark — Scoreboard
The Ticker
Scoreboard/stock ticker style. Each letter in its own cell. Horizontal strip of bordered boxes. "G·G·C" in segmented display. Live race data energy.
[28] Icon + Wordmark
The Terrain
Cross-section geological layers beneath text. Stacked horizontal strata in brand browns. "GRAVEL GOD" sits above the earth. Dig into the data.
[29] Icon — Instrument
The Gauge
Difficulty gauge with angular tick marks. "GG" in center. Needle pointing high. Measures suffering. The score instrument of gravel racing.
[30] Monogram — Connected
The Ligature
Custom GG where the two G's share a vertical stroke. Typographic ligature — the letters are fused. One mark, inseparable. "CYCLING" below in mono.
[31] Badge — Banner
The Pennant
Vertical pennant/banner shape with notched bottom. "GRAVEL GOD" reading downward. Racing pennant flag. Hang it from the handlebars.
[32] Wordmark — Blueprint
The Wire
Blueprint/technical drawing style. "GRAVEL GOD" with dimension lines, construction marks, and annotation callouts. The engineering behind the score.
[33] Badge — Angular Seal
The Seal
Octagonal seal frame (no circles — brutalist). "GRAVEL GOD" on top, "CYCLING" on bottom, "GG" at center. Official certification mark.
[34] Monogram — Tall
The Monolith
Tall narrow vertical rectangle with stacked G over G. Monolithic. Like a standing stone or mile marker. The golden ratio in proportions.
[35] Icon — Target
The Crosshair
Target reticle/crosshair with "GG" at the intersection. Four quadrant gaps. Lock onto your race. Precision scoring. Scope in on the data.
[36] Badge — Tier System
The Tier Badge
Diamond/rhombus badge shape with "T1" at top, "GG" at center. Represents the tier classification system. Each tier gets its own color. Shown as T1.
[37] Abstract — Racing Stripe
The Stripe
Three diagonal racing stripes cutting across the frame. "GG" overlaid at center. Speed, motion, road markings. The racing DNA of gravel events.
[38] Monogram — Corner Cut
The Notch
Rectangle with corner notches cut out — like a ticket or race plate. "GG" at center with gold kicker above. Race number plate aesthetic.
[39] Wordmark — Negative Space
The Negative
Dark filled bar with "GRAVEL GOD" knocked out in negative space. The text IS the absence. Clean horizontal strip. Header bar ready.
[40] Icon + Wordmark — Dual Accent
The Teal Accent
First logo to use teal as a primary accent. "GRAVEL" in teal mono, "GOD" in dark serif. Teal top border, gold bottom. Both brand accents in harmony.
