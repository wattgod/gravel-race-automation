[DESIGN LAB] 20 Logo Concepts

Gravel God Cycling

20 distinct logo concepts exploring wordmarks, monograms, icon+wordmark combos, and abstract marks. All follow the Desert Editorial neo-brutalist brand system: sharp corners, no border-radius, no shadows, no gradients, flat design. Each shown on warm-paper (#f5efe6) and dark-brown (#3a2e25) backgrounds.

[01] Wordmark — Editorial Serif
The Masthead
Newspaper-style editorial wordmark. Source Serif for "GRAVEL GOD", monospace "CYCLING" below. Gold 2px rule separator. Classic editorial authority.
[02] Wordmark — Monospace Stack
The Data Type
All-monospace stacked wordmark. "GRAVEL" light weight, "GOD" bold in gold. Ultra-tight letter-spacing. Data-driven, analytical feel.
[03] Monogram — Framed
The Block
Bold GG monogram inside a thick-bordered square. Gold inner border. Works as favicon, jersey patch, social avatar. Heavy, iconic presence.
[04] Monogram — Topographic
The Contour
GG monogram with topographic contour lines behind. Maps meet type. Terrain elevation data rendered as branding. Gold accent rule below.
[05] Icon + Wordmark
The Crown
Angular minimalist crown icon above "GRAVEL GOD". Sharp triangular points, no curves. Royal authority meets gravel grit. Gold crown, dark text.
[06] Icon + Wordmark
The Ridge
Mountain ridge silhouette as a base. Text sits above the terrain. Angular peaks, no smooth curves. The gravel landscape beneath the name.
[07] Icon — Abstract
The Spoke
Wheel spoke pattern radiating from center with "GG" at hub. Square outer frame instead of circle. Cycling meets brutalism.
[08] Abstract Badge
The Tread
Tire tread chevron pattern flanking a central wordmark badge. Angular chevrons suggest forward motion and gravel texture. Heavy bordered container.
[09] Monogram — Layered
The Overlap
Two G letterforms overlapping. First G in dark brown, second in gold. The intersection creates visual tension and depth. Tight, compact mark.
[10] Wordmark — Code
The Bracket
Monospace wordmark with code-style brackets. "[GRAVEL GOD]" — directly references the [01] kicker numbering system used across the site.
[11] Icon — Topographic
The Topo
Angular topographic contour lines in a square frame. Gold summit marker at center. Pure icon mark — terrain difficulty, elevation, the suffering ahead.
[12] Icon + Wordmark
The Profile
Elevation profile line graph integrated with wordmark. The data-viz aesthetic of the site carried into the logo. Gold elevation fill area below the line.
[13] Monogram — Dual Font
The Split
G | G with a vertical gold divider. Left G in serif (editorial), right G in mono (data). The brand's dual personality — storytelling meets analytics.
[14] Monogram — Inverted
The Slab
Solid dark rectangle with reversed-out GG. Maximum impact, minimal detail. Gold accent line near bottom. Jersey pocket, helmet sticker territory.
[15] Abstract Mark
The Fork
Two diverging paths forming a Y — the classic gravel fork in the road. One path dark brown, one gold. Choose your suffering. "GRAVEL GOD" below.
[16] Icon + Wordmark
The Score
Score-card aesthetic. Large gold "GG" (like the race scores on the site) with "Gravel God" as the editorial label. Mirrors the masthead hero layout.
[17] Abstract — Data Viz
The Bars
Horizontal bars of varying lengths — like a bar chart — with "GG" text overlaid. The analytics DNA of the brand expressed as a visual mark.
[18] Icon + Wordmark
The Crest
Shield/crest shape with angular crown at top, "GG" at center, "EST. 2025" at base. Cycling club heritage meets neo-brutalist design. Jersey-ready.
[19] Wordmark — Score Layout
The Editorial
Italic serif "Gravel God" in editorial style with a bold score number (72). Mirrors the masthead/score layout of the actual race profile pages.
[20] Abstract — Texture
The Gravel
Scattered angular particles forming a loose GG shape. Gravel as a medium. The roughness and texture of the sport expressed in pure geometry. Label below.
