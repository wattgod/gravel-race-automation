# Back Forty Highlander - Synthesis Brief

## Core Identity
Regional gravel race in rural Eastern Ontario, Canada. Grassroots community event with rolling farmland and forest roads. Tier 3 regional race with modest field size and volunteer-run organization.

## Key Facts
- **Location:** Mississippi Station, Ontario (near Ottawa)
- **Distance:** ~100km
- **Elevation:** ~800-1200m
- **Terrain:** Mixed gravel roads, farmland and forest
- **Field Size:** 100-300 riders
- **Registration:** $50-80 CAD, first-come first-served
- **Season:** Late spring/early summer

## Course Character
Rolling Eastern Ontario terrain with mix of maintained and unmaintained gravel roads. Moderate elevation gain. Open farmland sections with wind exposure. Technical forested sections. Weather-dependent conditions (mud when wet).

## Logistics
- Ottawa ~1 hour drive
- Rural location with basic amenities
- Limited aid stations
- Small field, grassroots organization
- Post-race community gathering
