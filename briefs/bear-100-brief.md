# Bear 100 - Synthesis Brief

## Core Identity
Northern Wisconsin 100-mile gravel race in remote Northwoods. Grassroots event with rolling terrain. Tier 3 regional event.

## Key Facts
- **Location:** Laona, Wisconsin
- **Distance:** 100 miles (plus shorter options)
- **Elevation:** Moderate (3000-4000ft)
- **Terrain:** Northwoods gravel roads
- **Field Size:** 100-200 riders
- **Registration:** $60-100
- **Season:** Summer

## Course Character
Rolling northern Wisconsin terrain through Northwoods. Gravel roads with moderate elevation. Forest and rural scenery.

- Grassroots organization
- Basic support
