# Burnt Bridge Classic - Synthesis Brief

## Core Identity
Cowichan Valley gravel race on Vancouver Island. Wine country setting with rolling terrain. Tier 3 regional event.

## Key Facts
- **Location:** Cowichan Valley, Vancouver Island, BC
- **Distance:** 50km, 100km options
- **Elevation:** Moderate (2000-3500ft for 100km)
- **Terrain:** Mixed gravel and paved roads
- **Field Size:** 100-300 riders
- **Registration:** $60-100 CAD
- **Season:** Spring or fall

## Course Character
Rolling wine country terrain. Mix of gravel and paved. Scenic agricultural and forest land. Moderate technical challenge.

## Logistics
- 1 hour north of Victoria
- Ferry access from mainland
- Limited local lodging
- Regional organization
