# BWR California — Synthesis Brief

## VITALS
- **Name:** Belgian Waffle Ride California
- **Location:** San Diego / Del Mar, California
- **Date:** Late April (April 25-26, 2026)
- **Distances:** Wafer (136mi) / Waffle (100mi) / Stroopwafel (60mi)
- **Elevation:** 8,500+ ft (Wafer)
- **Field:** 2,800+ across series, 800+ at California
- **Entry:** $150-$250 (first-come, first-served)
- **Founded:** 2011 by Michael Marckx

## COURSE
136 miles, 8,500 ft. Mixed surfaces: 40% paved, 30% gravel, 20% singletrack, 10% dirt. Belgian-style—technical. Named sections: Elfin Forest singletrack (mile 30-40, technical MTB, crashes common), Double Peak climb (mile 60, 1,000+ ft paved), Dirt roads (mile 80-100, chunky/loose). Technical rating: 4/5. MTB skills required.

## WEATHER
Late April San Diego: 55-70°F, 10% rain. Historical: 2019 (perfect), 2022 (rain/mud, high DNF), 2023 (dry/fast).

## EQUIPMENT
**Tires:** 38-40mm tubeless (WTB Resolute, Panaracer GK SK). **Gearing:** Compact double (34/50) for paved climbs. **Bike:** Drop-bar MTB or gravel with MTB skills. **Other:** Tubeless, spare tube, CO2.

## DNF FACTORS
10-15% (dry), 25%+ (wet). Causes: crashes (singletrack—Elfin Forest), mechanicals (mixed surfaces), fatigue (136mi, 8,500ft, technical), mud (if wet).

## COMMUNITY
Cult following. Belgian-style mixed-surface devotees. San Diego support strong. Technical course self-selects MTB-skilled riders.
