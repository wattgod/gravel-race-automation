# Coast to Coast - Synthesis Brief

## Core Identity
Michigan ultra-distance gravel race crossing from Lake Huron to Lake Michigan. 204 miles or 100-mile loop. Tier 3 regional ultra-distance event.

## Key Facts
- **Location:** Au Gres to Ludington, Michigan
- **Distance:** 204 miles (full) or 100-mile loop
- **Elevation:** Moderate (varies, relatively flat Michigan)
- **Terrain:** Michigan gravel roads
- **Field Size:** 100-300 riders
- **Registration:** $100-150
- **Season:** June

## Course Character
Relatively flat to rolling Michigan terrain crossing the state. Gravel roads with moderate elevation. Ultra-distance challenge.

## Logistics
- Crosses Michigan (logistics for 204-mile)
- Or 100-mile loop option
- Regional organization
- Ultra-distance support
