# Cowichan Crusher Gravel Fondo - Synthesis Brief

## Core Identity
Lake Cowichan gravel fondo on Vancouver Island. Forest and lake scenery with rolling terrain. Tier 3 regional BC event.

## Key Facts
- **Location:** Lake Cowichan, Vancouver Island, BC
- **Distance:** 40km, 80km, 120km options
- **Elevation:** Varies (3000-5000ft for longest)
- **Terrain:** Gravel and forest service roads
- **Field Size:** 200-400 riders
- **Registration:** $70-110 CAD
- **Season:** Spring/summer

## Course Character
Rolling to hilly Vancouver Island terrain. Forest service roads and gravel. Lake and forest scenery. Moderate technical challenge.

## Logistics
- Small town with limited amenities
- 30 min from Duncan, 1.5 hours from Victoria
- Ferry access from mainland
- Regional organization
