- **Location:** New Bern, North Carolina (Croatan National Forest)

- **Distance:** 150 miles
- **Elevation:** ~5,000 ft
- **Field:** 200-300 riders
- **Entry:** $100-$150

## COURSE
150 miles, 5,000 ft. 90% gravel, 10% dirt. Croatan National Forest. Flat coastal terrain, sandy gravel. Technical rating: 2/5. Not technical, but ultra distance + sand. 10-15 hours.

## WEATHER
Early March: 45-65°F, 30% rain. Historical: 2023 (cool/dry, 50-60°F), 2024 (variable, 45-65°F).

## EQUIPMENT
**Tires:** 38-42mm tubeless (WTB Resolute, Panaracer GK). Wider = better float in sand. **Gearing:** 1x or compact double (flat terrain). **Other:** Tubeless, 4+ bottles, layers, lights (long day).

## DNF FACTORS
15-20%. Causes: distance (150mi ultra), sandy gravel (slows pace, cumulative fatigue), mechanicals (150mi = high risk).

## COMMUNITY
East Coast ultra. 150mi coastal gravel. 200-300 riders. New Bern small coastal town support. 15-20% DNF self-selects.
