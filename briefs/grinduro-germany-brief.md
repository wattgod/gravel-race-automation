# Grinduro Germany — Synthesis Brief

## VITALS

- **Name:** Grinduro Germany
- **Location:** Hellenthal, Eifel, Germany
- **Format:** Enduro-style (4 timed descents, untimed climbs)
- **Date:** Late May (May 23-25, 2026) — Festival weekend
- **Distance:** 62 miles (~100 km)
- **Elevation Gain:** ~6,562 ft (2,000m)
- **Entry Cost:** €100-€180
- **Field Size:** 400-600 riders
- **Website:** https://www.grinduro.com/germany

## COURSE DESCRIPTION

**Unique Enduro Format:** Grinduro = "Grind" (climbing) + "Enduro" (timed descents). **Only 4 descent stages are timed**—all climbs are untimed "transport stages." Total time = sum of 4 timed descents. Rewards MTB descending skills, allows social climbing.

**Surface:** 50% gravel forest roads, 30% singletrack (timed descents), 20% paved connectors. Eifel volcanic landscape—unique German terrain.

### SUFFERING ZONES

1. **Eifel Forest Climbs (Untimed)** — Steep but no pressure, social pace OK
2. **Timed Descent Stages 1-4** — Technical singletrack, MTB skills required, crashes happen

## LOGISTICS

- **Airport:** Cologne-Bonn (CGN) — 60 km, 1-hour drive
- **Lodging:** Hellenthal tiny—book 3+ months ahead. On-site camping €30-€50
- **Aid:** Multiple stations + festival food/beer at finish

## CLIMATE

- **Mud risk:** Eifel forest holds water
