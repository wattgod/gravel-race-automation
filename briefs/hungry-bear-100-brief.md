# Hungry Bear 100 - Synthesis Brief

## Core Identity
Northern Wisconsin 100-mile gravel race in Cable (Chequamegon area). Rolling to hilly Northwoods terrain. Tier 3 regional event.

## Key Facts
- **Location:** Cable, Wisconsin
- **Distance:** 100 miles (plus shorter options)
- **Elevation:** Moderate to significant (4000-5000ft)
- **Terrain:** Northwoods gravel roads
- **Field Size:** 200-400 riders
- **Registration:** $70-110
- **Season:** Summer

## Course Character
Rolling to hilly northern Wisconsin through Chequamegon area. Gravel roads with moderate to significant elevation. Forest and lake scenery.

## Logistics
- Remote but Cable has some tourism infrastructure
- 4 hours from Milwaukee
- Regional organization
- Moderate support
