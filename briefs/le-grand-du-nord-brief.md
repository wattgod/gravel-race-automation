## COURSE DESCRIPTION

**UCI Gravel World Series** race through Minnesota's North Shore. 110 miles, 5,000 ft elevation, rolling Northwoods terrain. 70% gravel forest roads, 20% paved, 10% technical.

### SUFFERING ZONES

1. **Lake Superior Overlook Climb (Mile ~40)** - Steep, scenic
2. **Gunflint Trail Rollers (Miles 50-70)** - Constant rolling
3. **Technical Doubletrack (Miles 75-85)** - Rooty when tired
