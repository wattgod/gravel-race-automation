# MAAP Beechworth Granite Classic - Synthesis Brief

## Core Identity
Victorian high country gravel race in historic Beechworth. MAAP-sponsored event with hilly terrain. Tier 3 regional Australian event.

## Key Facts
- **Location:** Beechworth, Victoria, Australia
- **Distance:** 50km, 100km, 150km options
- **Elevation:** Significant (varies by distance, up to 8000ft+ for 150km)
- **Terrain:** Gravel roads and forest tracks
- **Field Size:** 300-600 riders
- **Registration:** $80-150 AUD
- **Season:** Spring or autumn

## Course Character
Hilly to mountainous Victorian high country. Technical climbs and descents. Historic gold rush region scenery. Challenging elevation.

## Logistics
- 3 hours from Melbourne
- Historic town with good tourism infrastructure
- Regional event with brand sponsorship
- Well-organized with aid stations
