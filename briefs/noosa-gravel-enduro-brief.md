# Noosa Gravel Enduro - Synthesis Brief

## Core Identity
Queensland hinterland gravel enduro near Noosa. Hilly terrain with enduro format. Tier 3 regional Australian event.

## Key Facts
- **Location:** Noosa Hinterland, Queensland, Australia
- **Distance:** 50km, 100km options
- **Elevation:** Significant (4000-6000ft for 100km)
- **Terrain:** Hinterland gravel and forest roads
- **Field Size:** 200-400 riders
- **Registration:** $80-130 AUD
- **Season:** Winter/spring

## Course Character
Hilly hinterland terrain with technical climbs and descents. Enduro format with timed sections. Subtropical scenery.

- Enduro format
