# Oregon Coast Gravel Epic - Synthesis Brief

## Core Identity
Central Oregon coast gravel race in Waldport. Coastal and Coast Range terrain with significant climbing. Tier 3 regional event.

## Key Facts
- **Location:** Waldport, Oregon
- **Distance:** 50km, 100km options
- **Elevation:** Significant (4000-6000ft)
- **Terrain:** Coastal and Coast Range gravel
- **Field Size:** 100-250 riders
- **Registration:** $80-100
- **Season:** Spring or Summer

## Course Character
Mix of coastal and mountain terrain with significant elevation. Forest roads with coastal views. Technical terrain.

- Moderate organization
- Moderate support
