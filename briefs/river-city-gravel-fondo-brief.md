# River City Gravel Fondo - Synthesis Brief

## Core Identity
Vancouver Island gravel fondo in Campbell River, BC. Fondo-style event with forest service roads and coastal terrain. Tier 3 regional event with BC cycling community support.

## Key Facts
- **Location:** Campbell River, Vancouver Island, BC
- **Distance:** Multiple options (50km, 100km typical)
- **Elevation:** Moderate to significant (varies by distance)
- **Terrain:** Gravel roads and forest service roads
- **Field Size:** 200-500 riders (typical BC fondo)
- **Registration:** $80-120 CAD
- **Season:** Spring/summer

## Course Character
Vancouver Island rolling to hilly terrain. Mix of maintained gravel and forest service roads. Dense forest sections with technical descents. Coastal weather influence. Wet conditions possible.

## Logistics
- Campbell River: ferry from mainland or drive from Victoria
- Mid-sized city with good amenities
- Regional airport available
- Organized event with aid stations
- Post-event festivities
