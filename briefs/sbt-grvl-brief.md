# SBT GRVL — Synthesis Brief

## VITALS
- **Name:** SBT GRVL (Steamboat Gravel)
- **Location:** Steamboat Springs, Colorado
- **Date:** Late August (August 17-18, 2026)
- **Distances:** Black (144mi) / Blue (100mi) / Green (50mi)
- **Elevation:** ~10,000 ft (Black)
- **Altitude:** 6,700-10,000 ft

- **Entry:** $200-$275 (lottery)

## COURSE
144 miles, 10,000 ft elevation, 6,700-10,000 ft altitude. 80% gravel, 20% paved. Colorado Rockies—big climbs, fast descents. Named sections: Elk River Valley climb (miles 20-40, 2,000 ft), Buffalo Pass highpoint (mile 70, 10,000 ft), Spring Creek descent (miles 70-90, fast/chunky), final rollers (miles 120-144). Aid stations at miles 40, 80, 120.

## WEATHER
Late August: 40-75°F, 20% afternoon thunderstorm risk. Altitude effects: 30% less oxygen at 10,000 ft. Historical: 2019 (perfect), 2021 (thunderstorms/cold rain), 2023 (warm, dry).

## EQUIPMENT
**Tires:** 40-42mm tubeless (WTB Resolute, Panaracer GK SK+). **Gearing:** Compact double or 1x with low gear (34/50 or 40T / 50T cassette). **Altitude prep:** Arrive 3-5 days early. **Other:** 3+ bottles, layers for 40-75°F swings.

## DNF FACTORS
5-10% (good), 15% (weather/altitude). Causes: altitude sickness (non-locals at 10,000 ft), mechanicals (chunky gravel, crashes on descents), bonking (long climbs), thunderstorms (cold rain = hypothermia risk).

## COMMUNITY
Premium event—AmGen Tour organizers, polished, professional. Steamboat resort town embrace. All levels welcome, altitude is equalizer.
