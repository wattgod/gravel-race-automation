# Sunday Creek Classic - Synthesis Brief

## Core Identity
Queensland hinterland gravel race in small Kenilworth. Rolling dairy country terrain. Tier 3 regional Australian event.

## Key Facts
- **Location:** Kenilworth, Queensland, Australia
- **Distance:** 50km, 100km options
- **Elevation:** Moderate (3000-4500ft)
- **Terrain:** Hinterland gravel roads
- **Field Size:** 100-250 riders
- **Registration:** $60-100 AUD
- **Season:** Winter/spring

## Course Character
Rolling to hilly hinterland terrain through dairy country. Gravel roads with moderate climbing. Scenic rural Queensland.

## Logistics
- 1.5-2 hours north of Brisbane
- Small town with very limited amenities
- Regional grassroots organization
- Basic support
