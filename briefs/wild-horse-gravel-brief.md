# Wild Horse Gravel - Synthesis Brief

## Core Identity
Western Colorado high desert gravel race in De Beque. Desert terrain. Tier 3 regional grassroots event.

## Key Facts
- **Location:** De Beque, Colorado
- **Distance:** 50km, 100km options
- **Elevation:** Moderate (3000-4000ft)
- **Terrain:** High desert gravel
- **Field Size:** 50-150 riders
- **Registration:** $60-90
- **Season:** Spring or Fall

## Course Character
High desert terrain through western Colorado. Gravel roads with moderate elevation. Desert and canyon scenery.

## Logistics

- Grassroots organization
- Basic support
