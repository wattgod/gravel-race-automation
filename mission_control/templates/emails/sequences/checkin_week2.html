2. Does the schedule fit your actual week? If Tuesday keeps not happening, Tuesday is wrong, not you. I'll restructure. A plan you can follow at 90% beats a perfect plan you follow at 60% — every time, by a lot.

3. Anything hurt? Knee on climbs, back on the trainer, saddle on long rides. These are signals, not weakness, and they're cheap to fix in week 2 and expensive to fix in week 10.
