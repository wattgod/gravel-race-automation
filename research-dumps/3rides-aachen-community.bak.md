## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Elite race dynamics [ELITE]:** "The men elite had a hard battle with a large group staying together for most of the race, entering the final climb with 7 riders" - the winning move came on the final climb where Vandeputte created a 4-second gap (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/3rides-gravel-race-for-marianne-vos-and-niels-vandeputte/)

**Recreational pacing reality [RECREATIONAL]:** One first-time gravel racer reported being passed by approximately 150 riders in the first 19 minutes despite starting near the front of their age group block, suggesting very fast early pace for those aiming to stay competitive (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bYZ7gBcgcyg)

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**2025 Course:** "A fast but sometimes challenging 58km track in the woods and fields south of the city of Aachen" (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/3rides-gravel-race-for-marianne-vos-and-niels-vandeputte/)

**Gravel course characteristics:** Described as having "Highspeed-Kurven, verschiedene Untergründe, Gegenhänge, die man mit Schwung fahren kann" (high-speed corners, various surfaces, counter-slopes you can take with momentum) (https://radclub.de/3rides-aachen/)

**Forest section:** The section "direkt nach dem Start im Aachener Wald" (directly after the start in the Aachen forest) was described as "like a rollercoaster ride" (https://radclub.de/3rides-aachen/)

**Scenic highlights:** Views of the Rursee (Rur lake) mentioned as one of the scenic highlights (https://radclub.de/3rides-aachen/)

**Course evolution:** The 2025 edition had "beautiful new courses" and moved to start from southern Aachen rather than the previous CHIO venue, with new routing through the Eifel region (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/3rides-gravel-race-for-marianne-vos-and-niels-vandeputte/)

## DNF Risk Factors

**Weather cancellations:** The 2025 Gran Fondo race was cancelled due to severe storms: "Some outdoor events had to be canceled at short notice, including an open-air concert on Münsterplatz in Ulm... and the 3RIDES Gran Fondo race in Aachen" due to "strong thunderstorms, heavy rain and hail" (https://www.dw.com/en/germany-updates-severe-storms-hit-several-states/live-72749136)

**Organizational chaos [RECREATIONAL perspective]:** "Mixed startboxes, late start, cars on the track" reported as issues affecting the race experience (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ec3QNcm-M5g)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Event scope:** "3RIDES is a Radsport- and Lifestyle-Festival" combining racing, expo, workshops, kids' races, and concerts over three days (https://radclub.de/3rides-aachen/)

**UCI qualification significance:** The event serves as a qualifier for both UCI Gravel World Championships and UCI Gran Fondo World Championships - riders finishing in the top 25% of their age group secure World Championship qualification (https://radclub.de/3rides-aachen/)

**Professional presence:** The 2024 race featured "Paul Voß and the Unbound-Sieger Ivar Slik" in the start list, and the 2025 edition attracted WorldTour pros including Jonas Koch (Red Bull-BORA-hansgrohe) (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-bike-festival-aachen/, https://www.tour-magazin.de/event/jedermann-rennen/3rides-aachen-2025-hochkaratige-besetzung-und-neue-strecke/)

**Region promotion:** Léon van Bon (photographer): "Die Strecken sind einfach super schön. Der Kurs des 3RIDES Gravel Race ist phantastisch. Es ist spannend, so eine Strecke in Mitteleuropa zu finden. Und die ganze Region ist viel zu wenig bekannt. Dabei ist sie super zu erreichen für viele Leute. Ich glaube, sie wird bisher komplett unterschätzt." (https://radclub.de/3rides-aachen/)

**Crowd support [RECREATIONAL]:** Despite organizational issues, one rider noted "the crowd was amazing" (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ec3QNcm-M5g)

**Multiple event options:** Beyond the elite UCI races, the festival offers non-timed "Rides" on both gravel (35km, 65km, 90km, 110km options) and road (60km, 100km, 130km, 160km options) that explore the tri-border region of Germany, Belgium, and Netherlands (https://www.radsport-rennrad.de/event/3rides-aachen-rennrad-gravel-rides/)

**Kids programming:** VeloTiade and Kids-Rad-Diplom programs introduce children to cycling "sportlich und altersgerecht" (athletically and age-appropriately) (https://www.tour-magazin.de/event/jedermann-rennen/3rides-aachen-2025-hochkaratige-besetzung-und-neue-strecke/)

## Weather Experienced

**June 1, 2025:** "Strong thunderstorms, heavy rain and hail, and gusty winds caused chaos in parts of Germany over the weekend... Within a few hours, there were hundreds of emergency calls" in North Rhine-Westphalia where Aachen is located. "Hail as big as ping pong balls hit many parts of Germany." The Gran Fondo race was cancelled due to these conditions. (https://www.dw.com/en/germany-updates-severe-storms-hit-several-states/live-72749136)
