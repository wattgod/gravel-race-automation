## Equipment & Gear Recommendations

**Jens Claussen [ELITE]:** Ran 2.4 bar front / 2.6 bar rear tire pressure WITH TUBES on Continental Terra Speed 40mm tires in dry conditions. Notes this was "etwas risky" but necessary. Gearing: 31/34 (48T chainring). (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

**Jens Claussen [ELITE]:** "Nahezu alle fuhren Tubeless – Alle außer ich... 'Old school' mit Schlauch ausgestattet" - observed nearly all other riders running tubeless setups (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

**Moritz [UNKNOWN]:** Experienced a side-cut tire failure after 100km that held but significantly impacted performance. Already had WM qualification from Valkenburg. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/06/03/3-rides-aachen-uci-gravel-world-series-rennbericht/)

## Weather Experienced

**2024 Edition:** "nahezu hochsommerlichen Temperaturen" - near midsummer temperatures. Jens Claussen's [ELITE] Garmin showed 35°C in the sun at noon start. (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

**2024 Edition:** Light rain at start: "Nachdem die Profis... aus der Priority Box auf nimmer wieder entschwunden waren, wurden in 3 Minuten Abständen die einzelnen Blocks auf die Strecke geschickt" - race started in light drizzle (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

**2025 Gran Fondo Cancellation:** Event canceled for safety reasons due to continuing unstable weather conditions and thunderstorms. Timeline: 7:00 AM "waiting for course clearance", 7:10 AM still waiting, 7:23 AM start delayed 30 minutes (light drizzle, riders standing under trees), 7:25 AM complete cancellation. (https://3rides.de/en/category/homepage-en/ and https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/3rides-gran-fondo-aachen-ein-satz-mit-x.200172/)

**2025 Gravel Race (Saturday):** Marianne Vos won women's race; event proceeded successfully day before Gran Fondo cancellation (https://3rides.de/en/category/homepage-en/)

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course Composition:** "21 km Runde mit knapp 300 hm" in 2023, changed to "41,3 km Runde mit ca. 415 hm" in 2024 - significantly longer laps with more elevation (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

**Surface Mix:** "Schotter, feiner Kies, übles Kopfsteinpflaster, giftige Anstiege und technische Wald-Passagen" - gravel, fine gravel, brutal cobblestones, punchy climbs, and technical forest passages (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

**Jens Claussen [ELITE]:** "Es ging auf der Runde von Beginn an ca. 15 min nur bergauf" - ~15 minutes of climbing right from the start of each lap (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

**2024 Course:** 124km total distance, 65% gravel, 1,580m elevation gain for the full race (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/06/03/3-rides-aachen-uci-gravel-world-series-rennbericht/)

**2023 Course Description:** "A group of 6 quickly split up from the rest... with a first part of the course combining road and cobbles and a second half with more technical sections in the wood" (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/3rides-gravel-race-aachen-with-close-sprint-finishes/)

**Jens Claussen [ELITE]:** "Die Taktik sollte sich auszahlen. Kein Einbruch, immer in einer guten Gruppe, mit Auge gefahren und nach drei Runden glücklich im Ziel" - describes riding "with awareness" through three laps (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

**Course Markings Issue:** "Als die Profis im Ziel waren, standen aus einmal an vielen Abzweigungen keine Streckenposten mehr. Ein NO GO... Zudem war die Strecke im Ganzen miserabel bis gar nicht ausgeschildert." - course marshals disappeared after elite finishers, poor signage (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Jens Claussen [ELITE] - Strategy:** "Da hinter meinem Startblock kaum noch was kam, was uns hätte aufrollen können, hieß es von Runde 1 an: ab in eine Gruppe und möglichst lange dort drin sitzen bleiben" - get into a group from lap 1 and stay there as long as possible (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

**Jens Claussen [ELITE] - Power Management:** Initially wanted to ride hills power-based but saw "immer wieder vorne ein 4 auf meiner Wattanzeige" (400+ watts repeatedly) and decided to ignore power data for rest of race. Relied on "Diesel und meine Rennhärte aus knapp 30 Jahren" - diesel engine and 30 years of race experience. Final time: 4:56h at 25.4 km/h average for 124km. (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

**Lukas [UNKNOWN] - Elite Start Pace:** "Von Anfang an Feuer unter den Reifen – ein 40er Schnitt auf Schotter" - 40 km/h average on gravel from the start, described as "very borderline." Finished 13th overall, earning WM qualification. Cramped badly at the end. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/06/03/3-rides-aachen-uci-gravel-world-series-rennbericht/)

**Moritz [UNKNOWN] - Elite Pace:** "1,5 Stunden Volldampf" - 1.5 hours full gas before tire issues forced pace reduction. Finished 56th. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/06/03/3-rides-aachen-uci-gravel-world-series-rennbericht/)

**Jens Claussen [ELITE] - Nutrition:** "1 Porridge Riegel und 5 Gels40 (ohne Koffein) von MNSTRY. Dazu zwei 0,75 Flaschen mit MNSTRY PowerCarb und zwei 0,75 Flaschen mit Wasser (hätte bei der Wärme etwas mehr sein können)" - 1 porridge bar, 5 gels (no caffeine), two 750ml bottles with carb drink, two 750ml bottles with water. Notes water could have been more in the heat. (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

**Alexander [UNKNOWN]:** Noted that without support he would have been "verdurstet" (dehydrated to death) in the sun/heat (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/06/03/3-rides-aachen-uci-gravel-world-series-rennbericht/)

**Time Cut Confusion:** Initially communicated as 4:30h for 124km (27.6 km/h average required), later extended to 5:30h on race day. This created significant uncertainty for age-group riders. (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

## DNF Risk Factors

**Crashes:** Gerrit [UNKNOWN] suffered "schwerer Sturz in Runde zwei" (heavy crash in round two) resulting in broken collarbone and three cracked ribs, DNF. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/06/03/3-rides-aachen-uci-gravel-world-series-rennbericht/)

**Mechanical Failures:** 2023 race saw World Champion Gianni Vermeersch DNF with "mechanical problem and a flat tyre" while in lead group (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/3rides-gravel-race-aachen-with-close-sprint-finishes/)

**Tire Failures:** Moritz [UNKNOWN] experienced side-cut after 100km that "held" but destroyed his pacing for final 24km (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/06/03/3-rides-aachen-uci-gravel-world-series-rennbericht/)

**Course Crowding/Safety:** Jens Claussen [ELITE] noted "die Strecke, zumindest auf der ersten Runde, grenzwertig voll. Stürze, besonders bei engeren Passagen, sind da vorprogrammiert" - course dangerously crowded especially on first lap, crashes inevitable at narrow passages (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

**Heat/Dehydration:** Alexander [UNKNOWN] emphasized critical need for support/hydration in the heat (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/06/03/3-rides-aachen-uci-gravel-world-series-rennbericht/)

**Bee Stings:** Renée [UNKNOWN] was stung by a bee in the thigh mid-race, causing circulation issues and nearly forcing abandonment (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/06/03/3-rides-aachen-uci-gravel-world-series-rennbericht/)

**Reverse Start Order Issue:** Loretta69 [UNKNOWN] criticized dangerous start order (oldest/slowest first, then progressively faster age groups with only 5min gaps): "niemand, der noch alle Beisammen hat lässt die leistungsschwächsten Athleten als Erste starten!" Created situation where faster riders would plow through slower groups. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/3rides-gran-fondo-aachen-ein-satz-mit-x.200172/)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Scale & Growth:** 2024 edition had 2,200 riders from 56 countries - biggest UCI Gravel World Series event to date. 2025 Gran Fondo had reduced participation (estimated half the starters in some age categories compared to prior year). (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/3rides-gravel-in-aachen-biggest-ever-uci-gravel-world-series-event/ and https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/3rides-gran-fondo-aachen-ein-satz-mit-x.200172/)

**Festival Atmosphere:** "Überall Leute, überall Absperrungen, einfach kein Durchkommen" - everywhere people, everywhere barriers, simply no getting through. Large expo with test bikes, workshops, talks, challenges. (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/ and https://3rides.de/en/category/homepage-en/)

**Elite Field Quality:** 2025 featured World Champion Marianne Vos; 2024 included Greg van Avermaet, Lucinda Brand, Lorena Wiebes, Daan Soete, Cameron Mason, Alban Lakata, Carolin Schiff, Judith Krahl (https://3rides.de/en/category/homepage-en/)

**Support Team Culture:** well:fair cycling team described "Support auf World-Class-Niveau" - world-class support level with "Wasserflaschen, Carbs, Notfall-Haribo oder Kinderbespaßung" (water bottles, carbs, emergency gummy bears, child entertainment). "Jede Verpflegungsübergabe saß – fast wie ein Formel-1-Boxenstopp, nur mit mehr Anfeuerungsgebrüll!" - every handoff worked like F1 pit stop but with more cheering. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/06/03/3-rides-aachen-uci-gravel-world-series-rennbericht/)

**Post-Race Washing:** "Ach ja, auch Olympiasieger wie Greg van Avermaet müssen bei Gravel Rennen ihr Bike selbst waschen..." - even Olympic champions like Greg van Avermaet have to wash their own bikes at gravel races (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

**Mixed Reviews:** Jens Claussen [ELITE]: "Ich bin nach wie vor total angefixt Gravel Rennen dieser Art zu fahren und die Veranstalter des 3Rides haben wieder unglaublich viel richtig gemacht. Aber es war nicht alles Gold was glänzt." Overall positive but with organizational concerns. (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)

**Organizational Criticism:** Multiple riders criticized disappearing course marshals after elite finishers, poor course marking, dangerous start order, and lack of communication after 2025 Gran Fondo cancellation with no refunds or compensation offered. Loretta69 [UNKNOWN]: "Aachen 3Rides? Nie mehr." - Never again. (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/ and https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/3rides-gran-fondo-aachen-ein-satz-mit-x.200172/)

**Venue:** CHIO Gelände in Aachen (World Equestrian Festival complex) - prestigious venue in tri-border region (Germany/Belgium/Netherlands) (https://speed-ville.de/3rides-aachen-gravel/)
