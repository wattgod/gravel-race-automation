# ALBIA HOLY COW — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course Description [UNKNOWN]:** "When I first rode West out of Albia, I didn't know what to expect. I knew there were a number of Class B roads I wanted to travel along the way, but I didn't expect to meet acres of grass-fed cattle, beautiful views, river crossings, and even a short ride along the Historic Mormon Trail. It was spectacular and the opportunity to explore Monroe county left me thinking, 'Holy Cow this is cool!'" (https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/16026/albia-holy-cow)

**Elevation Profile [UNKNOWN]:** "Albia is a fairly hill route, but there are a lot of long descents and nice flats to help you recharge and get ready for the next Class B challenge." (https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/16026/albia-holy-cow)

**Difficulty Classification:** Listed as "Moderate terrain with rolling short steep hills or moderate, sustained climbs" (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10062/albia-holy-cow)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Personal Goal [UNKNOWN]:** "I set a personal goal to complete the race in 4:30 and came in just late of the goal." - Video description for 100km distance in 2024 (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dnzANTYWAvk)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Local Support:** "We're working closely with the Albia Chamber of Commerce on this one and they are eager to show you around downtown and their picturesque square. You'll want to plan to stick around after this adventure!" (https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/16026/albia-holy-cow)

**Start Location (2024 update):** "For 2024 we're moving the start location to the Monroe County Fairgrounds where they have plenty of RV camping sites available!" (https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/16026/albia-holy-cow)

**Registration Tiers:** The race offers grassroots registration at $40 with chip timing, marked course, pro photography and aid stations. A $60 option adds lunch and shirt. Full season pass for all 7 Iowa Gravel Series races available for $399 including jersey. (https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/16026/albia-holy-cow)

**Part of Series:** The Albia Holy Cow is part of the Iowa Gravel Series, which includes 7 races total (https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/16026/albia-holy-cow)
