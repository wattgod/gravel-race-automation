## Race Overview & Key Details

**Location:** Back Forty Artisan Cheese Farm, 1406 Gulley Rd, Mississippi Station, ON, K0H 2C0

**Course Distances:**
- EPIC: 108.5-115 km (sources vary slightly by year)
- MEDIO: 70 km
- PETIT: 35 km

**Start Times:**
- EPIC: 9:00 AM
- MEDIO: 9:30 AM
- PETIT: 9:45 AM

**Key Features:** Part of the Gravel Cup Canada series. Routes use single-lane gravel roads through North Frontenac and Lanark Highlands. Roads have a distinctive white hue from marble of the nearby Tatlock Quarry, compared to Italy's Stradda Bianche. Post-race food and drink by the Mississippi River with waterfall sounds in background.

**Highlight Sections:** Ranger Camp, North Shore, Clarendon

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Road Surface:** "The marble of the nearby Tatlock Quarry has given many of the roads a distinctly white hue, similar to the Italian cycling classic Strada Bianche" (https://gravelcup.com/gravel-cup-events/back-forty-highlander-2024)

**Difficulty Rating:** Listed as "Moderate terrain with rolling short steep hills or moderate, sustained climbs" (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12531/back-forty-highlander-)

**Technical Aspects:** "Lots of single lane treed gravel roads" through areas where "maple syrup is the most abundant natural resource." The old logging roads are described as "quiet" (https://gravelcup.com/gravel-cup-events/back-forty-highlander-2024)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Post-Race:** "Afterwards, relax and reminisce with food and drink on the shore of the Mississippi River with the calming distant sound of waterfalls" (https://gravelcup.com/gravel-cup-events/back-forty-highlander-2024)

**Setting:** "From the picturesque location of the Back Forty Artisan Cheese Farm" with "views of the Mississippi River, Dalhousie Lake & the rolling hills of forests that dot the landscape" (https://gravelcup.com/gravel-cup-events/back-forty-highlander-2024)

**Historical Context:** "Logging and colonization has shaped the history of this once more populated area. Today the old logging roads are quiet" (https://gravelcup.com/gravel-cup-events/back-forty-highlander-2024)
