- **Surface breakdown**: Mixed paved & gravel roads; includes dirt roads, seasonal/primitive paths in related events; Washington County gravel described as "smoother than the best Pennsylvania paved roads"[2][3][4].
- **Key course features, named climbs/sectors**: Many rollers, several steep gravel climbs to 18% grade; passes scenic villages, hamlets, covered bridges; circles Battenkill River and Valley; challenging hilly terrain with long climbs[1][2][4].
- **Technical difficulty level**: Challenging with steep pitches (18%), primitive routes, and mixed surfaces pushing endurance and skills[1][3].
