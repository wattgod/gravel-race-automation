# BIG SKY GRAVEL — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Course Profile & Route Details

**Course Distances:**
- 100 Mile: 6,000-6,500 ft elevation gain (sources vary slightly)
- 50 Mile: Cuts out portions of the 100 mile route
- 22 Mile: Can be ridden as tour/e-bike (non-timed) or race

**Route Characteristics:**
"Rolling hills and mostly smooth gravel roads. Pedal through historic and rural Montana ranching country with an epic backdrop of the rugged Beartooth Mountains." (https://www.redlodgeevents.com/bigskygravel)

The 92-mile version (2022) went "through foothills from Red Lodge to Fishtail and back through Roscoe & Luther" with 6,103 ft of climbing. (https://www.tetongravity.com/forums/showthread.php/348092-Big-Sky-Gravel/)

**Aid Stations:**
- Mile 8, 31, 86: Drop Bag #1
- Mile 60: Drop Bag #2
(https://bigskygravel.com/)

## Race Logistics

**Start Times:**
- 7:00 AM: 100 Mile
- 8:30 AM: 50 Mile
- 9:30 AM: 22 Mile
(https://bigskygravel.com/)

**Check-in:** Saturday 3-8 PM and Sunday 6-6:30 AM at Roosevelt Center (519 Broadway Avenue South, Red Lodge, MT)

**Drop Bags:** Must be collected Saturday at check-in by 8 PM or by 6:30 AM Sunday morning (https://bigskygravel.com/)

**Cut-offs:** "While cut offs will be strictly enforced, they are generous and manageable." (https://www.redlodgeevents.com/bigskygravel)

## Registration & Pricing

**Early Registration Pricing (increases after February 28):**
- 22 Mile: starts at $50
- 50 Mile: starts at $70
- 100 Mile: starts at $90

"We offer age-based pricing in an effort to get more young riders on the starting line! Discounts applied automatically when registering." (https://www.redlodgeevents.com/bigskygravel)

**Volunteer Discounts:** "We also have a generous volunteer program. Volunteer before/after the race (or at one of our other races during the summer) for free/discounted race fees!" (https://www.redlodgeevents.com/bigskygravel)
