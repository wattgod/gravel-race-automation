## TERRAIN & COURSE
- **Surface breakdown**: 60-70% gravel (varies by distance), remainder paved; ~30 miles total paved on main course per rider report[1]. No singletrack or dirt breakdown available.
- **Key course features, named climbs/sectors**: Crosses GA/AL border (50% each state); traverses rural roads with scenic remote views; sectors connect ridgelines (GA smooth/meandering) vs. straight steep drops/creek crossings (AL rougher); "Rock Mills, Alabama" featured; mud logjam noted[1][2]. "Quite possibly the best route I have ever created" (organizer quote)[1].
- **Technical difficulty level**: High due to vastly different gravel between states (GA ridgeline smooth, AL steep/direct/punishing); mental/physical battle; punctures, crashes, DNFs reported[2][7].
- **Elevation profile description**: Rolling with significant gain (up to 8000 ft on 100mi); twists/turns obscure state crossings[1].
