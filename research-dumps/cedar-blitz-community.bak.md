# CEDAR BLITZ — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Rider Quotes & Race Reports

**Tracy Gravlin [ELITE]:** "The start was fast and felt great. About 26 miles in, the head winds seemed to be everywhere you turned. Riding solo at that point made it hard. Beautiful farm country though, and it felt great to finish feeling good but worn out at the same time." (https://www.joinbasecamp.com/post/tracys-story-cedar-blitz)

**Tracy Gravlin [ELITE]:** "Beth and I were the only women in the 60+, but we came in ahead of the 50-59 and one of the 40-49, so we felt legit on the podium!" (https://www.joinbasecamp.com/post/tracys-story-cedar-blitz)

**Dragon Adventures Base Camp [ELITE]:** "This race isn't just about watts and finish times—it's about sharing the day with riders who love gravel as much as we do. The post-race hangout, and great local food made the finish line feel like a festival, and that's the kind of energy we love to see in cycling!" (https://dragonadventuresbasecamp.com/2025/07/18/cedar-blitz-gravel-race-2025-highlights-and-interviews/)

## Weather Experienced

**2022:** Overcast, 64 degrees, wind 7 mph (https://results.bikereg.com/race/14522)

**2025 (50-mile race):** Significant head winds beginning around mile 26 that "seemed to be everywhere you turned" (https://www.joinbasecamp.com/post/tracys-story-cedar-blitz)

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Surface & Scenery:** "Beautiful farm country" and "Fabulous gravel! Farm country for miles and miles." The course goes through "scenic backroads, forests, and farmland around Cedar Springs, Michigan" with "forest lanes, rolling hills and gravel tracks" (https://www.joinbasecamp.com/post/tracys-story-cedar-blitz, https://dragonadventuresbasecamp.com/2025/07/18/cedar-blitz-gravel-race-2025-highlights-and-interviews/, https://ilovegravelracing.com/event/cedar-blitz/)

**Critical Section [ELITE perspective]:** Around mile 26 of the 50-mile course, headwinds become a significant factor, particularly challenging for solo riders (https://www.joinbasecamp.com/post/tracys-story-cedar-blitz)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Start Intensity [ELITE perspective]:** "The start was fast and felt great" - Tracy Gravlin on the 50-mile race suggests the opening pace is aggressive (https://www.joinbasecamp.com/post/tracys-story-cedar-blitz)

**Mid-Race Challenge [ELITE perspective]:** Solo riders face difficulty beginning around mile 26 when headwinds intensify. Group riding or drafting opportunities may be critical through this section (https://www.joinbasecamp.com/post/tracys-story-cedar-blitz)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Post-Race Scene:** "The tasty Cedar Springs Ale was our reward! We both are going home with a growler." Finish is in downtown Cedar Springs with "brewery-town cheers, music and craft beer" at Cedar Springs Brewery (https://www.joinbasecamp.com/post/tracys-story-cedar-blitz, https://ilovegravelracing.com/event/cedar-blitz/)

**Community Building:** "The BaseCamp community has given me so many cycling friends from across the country. Beth came up from Kansas!" - riders travel from out of state for this event (https://www.joinbasecamp.com/post/tracys-story-cedar-blitz)

**Event Vibe:** Described as having a "community vibe" and "high-energy" atmosphere. "The post-race hangout, and great local food made the finish line feel like a festival." Multiple riders noted it's "more than a race, it's the grit-and-grin rally of the year" (https://dragonadventuresbasecamp.com/2025/07/18/cedar-blitz-gravel-race-2025-highlights-and-interviews/, https://ilovegravelracing.com/event/cedar-blitz/)

**Growth:** "2024 brought 500+ riders" and described as "One of the fastest growing gravel races in the region" (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oq5ch_rV4cA, https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rgtWKhZV2P4)

**Well-Supported:** Multiple sources note it's "well-organized" and "well supported race" (https://dragonadventuresbasecamp.com/2025/07/18/cedar-blitz-gravel-race-2025-highlights-and-interviews/, https://www.joinbasecamp.com/post/tracys-story-cedar-blitz)

**Regional Significance:** Part of the Michigan Gravel Race Series and described as "a must-do event in the regional gravel scene" (https://cedarblitz.com/, https://dragonadventuresbasecamp.com/2025/07/18/cedar-blitz-gravel-race-2025-highlights-and-interviews/)
