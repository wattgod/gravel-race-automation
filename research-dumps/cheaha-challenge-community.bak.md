## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course Profile:**
- 100-mile route: 9,714 feet (3,000m) of climbing (https://ucigranfondoworldseries.com/en/cheaha-challenge-granfondo/)
- 126-mile ULTRA: 12,919 feet of climbing with 15% grades (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GodkAOPLQVU)
- Mount Cheaha (2,407 feet / 733m) is climbed twice on the century course - at mile 42 and mile 57 (https://ucigranfondoworldseries.com/en/cheaha-challenge-granfondo/)
- 50-mile turnaround point for the century route (https://ucigranfondoworldseries.com/en/cheaha-challenge-granfondo/)

**Named Climbs:**
Major climbs include: White's Gap, Heflin Mountain, Horseblock, "Not Again," "OH SHIFT!," and Adam's Gap (the turning point) - all climbed twice on the out-and-back course (https://ucigranfondoworldseries.com/en/cheaha-challenge-granfondo/)

**Time Trial Course:**
- 12.5-mile straight shot course with only three turns, all near the finish (https://ucigranfondoworldseries.com/en/cheaha-challenge-granfondo/)
- Described as "rough in spots, so please keep your head up while riding" (https://granfondodailynews.com/2024/05/21/uci-cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo-worlds-qualifying-results/)
- Starts in Piedmont, finishes in Jacksonville (https://ucigranfondoworldseries.com/en/cheaha-challenge-granfondo/)

**Scenery:**
- Route follows Skyline Drive leading to the top of Alabama
- Described as "forested hills" that "open onto vast overlooks" (https://app.advcollective.com/travel-guides/Lineville/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo-lineville-alabama)
- Views stretch over the Talladega National Forest (https://app.advcollective.com/travel-guides/Lineville/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo-lineville-alabama)
- Course features "smooth pavement and occasional rough patches" (https://app.advcollective.com/travel-guides/Lineville/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo-lineville-alabama)

## Equipment & Gear Recommendations

**Essential Gear [ALL LEVELS]:**
- Hydration pack with capacity for at least 2 liters of water plus electrolyte supplements - "hydration points are limited along the route" (https://app.advcollective.com/travel-guides/Lineville/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo-lineville-alabama)
- Cycling shoes with "good grip and cushioning to manage rough pavement sections and varied terrain" (https://app.advcollective.com/travel-guides/Lineville/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo-lineville-alabama)
- Padded cycling shorts for "long hours in the saddle over uneven terrain" (https://app.advcollective.com/travel-guides/Lineville/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo-lineville-alabama)
- Layered cycling jersey to "adjust to fluctuating temperatures on climbs and descents" (https://app.advcollective.com/travel-guides/Lineville/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo-lineville-alabama)
- Helmet with good ventilation (https://app.advcollective.com/travel-guides/Lineville/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo-lineville-alabama)
- Lightweight waterproof jacket for "sudden showers" (https://app.advcollective.com/travel-guides/Lineville/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo-lineville-alabama)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Pacing Advice [ALL LEVELS]:**
- "Start early to avoid heat - Begin your ride in the early morning to beat the afternoon heat and capture the best light and cooler temperatures" (https://app.advcollective.com/travel-guides/Lineville/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo-lineville-alabama)
- "Maintain steady pacing - The elevation gain can tax your stamina; pace yourself to avoid burnout on steeper climbs" (https://app.advcollective.com/travel-guides/Lineville/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo-lineville-alabama)
- "Hydration is critical" - carry sufficient water from the start as "hydration stations are scarce" (https://app.advcollective.com/travel-guides/Lineville/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo-lineville-alabama)

**Mental Approach [ALL LEVELS]:**
"Navigating the Cheaha Challenge is as much about mental stamina as physical readiness; the course can be relentless and demands pacing." (https://app.advcollective.com/travel-guides/Lineville/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo-lineville-alabama)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Event Character:**
"The ride started as a recreational ride that riders would participate in with the goal of racing the course to get a fast time, while others would start with the singular goal of simply finishing." (https://runtrimag.com/race-events/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo/)

"We are blessed with hundreds of riders who participate with the goal of finishing and having a great time among the various distances." (https://runtrimag.com/race-events/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo/)

**Post-Race:**
"When it is all done, we gather around at the finish area and have great post-ride meal to tell stories of how the ride went and connect with other riders from all over the US, and the World." (https://runtrimag.com/race-events/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo/)

**Crowd Support:**
"Every time a name was called the cyclists cheered like their own kids were winning trophies" at the 2021 time trial awards (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/6093/cheaha-challenge-draws-record-numbers-this-year)

"Bicyclists are a tight-knit group, something simple to see at an international competition's time trials Saturday." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/6093/cheaha-challenge-draws-record-numbers-this-year)

**Rest Stop Culture:**
"A multitude of volunteers and sponsors have made the Cheaha Challenge ride special — it is undoubtedly one of the best-supported century rides anywhere. From the registration people to the safety folks to the smiling faces at all the different rest stops" (https://avidcyclist.com/event/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo/)

"Then, there's the crazy rest stops — one year there was one with a Tour de France theme complete with the famous Tour devil, a small Eiffel Tower and even two pretty podium girls." (https://avidcyclist.com/event/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo/)

**Growth & Participation:**
- 2019 UCI TT: ~80 registrants (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/6093/cheaha-challenge-draws-record-numbers-this-year)
- 2021 UCI TT: 154 registrants - nearly double (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/6093/cheaha-challenge-draws-record-numbers-this-year)
- 2021 Sunday Challenge: 1,300+ riders, breaking previous record of ~1,100 (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/6093/cheaha-challenge-draws-record-numbers-this-year)
- 2025: 1,000 riders from 36 US states and 6 countries (https://ucigranfondoworldseries.com/en/cheaha-challenge-granfondo-for-ricardo-rouillon-and-emma-drabbe/)

"The Cheaha Challenge is now in it's 30th year, where it has grown from the humble roots from that first edition to become the largest annual cycling event in Alabama, and one of the largest in the United States." (https://runtrimag.com/race-events/cheaha-challenge-gran-fondo/)

**Local Setting:**
"Jacksonville, population 12,548, is blessed to be the home of Jacksonville State University (JSU) the city's largest employer... The residents of Jacksonville are family-oriented and friendly, complementing the warmth and inviting atmosphere surrounding the community." (https://ucigranfondoworldseries.com/en/cheaha-challenge-granfondo/)

"Jacksonville has award-winning barbeque restaurants, along with ones that feature a variety of cuisines." (https://ucigranfondoworldseries.com/en/cheaha-challenge-granfondo/)

**Recognition:**
"A Top 10 Event in the State of Alabama as selected by the Alabama Bureau of Tourism & Travel in 2006, 2010, and 2012." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Popup/402)
