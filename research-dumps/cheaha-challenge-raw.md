**Key course features, named climbs/sectors**: White’s Gap, Heflin Mountain, Horseblock, Not Again, OH SHIFT!, Adam’s Gap, Mount Cheaha; ULTRA adds Bain’s Gap, Cottaquilla, Chimney Peak, Chinnabee (hardest cat 2 climb in state).[5][7] Passes Weaver, Jacksonville, Piedmont, Cedartown; Time Trial: 12 miles, 3 turns near finish, Piedmont to Jacksonville.[3][5] GPS maps downloadable for all routes.[6]
