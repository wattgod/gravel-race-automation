## Equipment & Gear Recommendations

**General equipment advice [RECREATIONAL/ALL LEVELS]:** "The best type of bike to use is one with fatter tires to allow for lower pressures, enhancing comfort and performance on gravel. Suitable options include gravel bikes, cyclocross bikes, mountain bikes, hybrid bikes, touring bikes, and even road bikes or e-bikes, although road bikes might be less comfortable due to high tire pressures and hard gearing." (https://strambecco.com/event/cowichan-crusher/)

## Weather Experienced

**2024 race conditions [ELITE report]:** "Better than expected weather as the rain and showers held off until much later in the day! Conditions were ideal and most of the gravel roads were in good shape." Record 300+ participants showed up for these conditions. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/RaceDashboard/BCTripleCrownOfGravelSeries/1/7954/record-300-participants-ride-the-7th-annual-cowichan-crusher-gravel-fondo-)

**2023 race conditions:** Event ran under "mainly sunny skies" according to race director Dean Stanton who commented: "We lucked out for sure. It was not supposed to be that nice. It was good weather." (https://www.cowichanvalleycitizen.com/sports/sun-shines-as-riders-crush-gravel-at-cowichan-lake-bike-race)

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course characteristics [ELITE perspective]:** Roads described as "in excellent condition" with "mixture of climbs and then descents" making it a great race. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/RaceDashboard/BCTripleCrownOfGravelSeries/1/7954/record-300-participants-ride-the-7th-annual-cowichan-crusher-gravel-fondo-)

**General terrain [RECREATIONAL]:** The event showcases "amazing gravel roads, scenic valleys and streams and a large beautiful lake" with "rolling hills, blanketed in lush green trees, a large lake and panoramic views." Features "some steep descents" on the longer routes. (https://triplecrownofgravel.ca/cowichan-crusher/ and https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/7840/register-now-for-the-cowichan-crusher-and-save)

**Technical characteristics [RECREATIONAL]:** Medio Fondo (55km) described as "a little longer and more advanced with more climbing and technical riding." (https://triplecrownofgravel.ca/cowichan-crusher/)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Elite pacing data [ELITE - Full Fondo 98km]:** 2024 winner Fabian Merino's time of 3:05:56 over nearly 100km translates to averaging approximately 34 km/hour. Race director noted: "That means he was averaging 34 km an hour so he was riding even faster than that at points with the hills. That's crazy." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/RaceDashboard/BCTripleCrownOfGravelSeries/1/7954/record-300-participants-ride-the-7th-annual-cowichan-crusher-gravel-fondo- and https://www.cowichanvalleycitizen.com/sports/sun-shines-as-riders-crush-gravel-at-cowichan-lake-bike-race)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Event growth:** Record 300+ participants showed up for the 7th edition in 2024, representing growth and positive community reception. The 2023 event had roughly 250 racers. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/RaceDashboard/BCTripleCrownOfGravelSeries/1/7954/record-300-participants-ride-the-7th-annual-cowichan-crusher-gravel-fondo- and https://www.cowichanvalleycitizen.com/sports/sun-shines-as-riders-crush-gravel-at-cowichan-lake-bike-race)

**Post-race amenities [ALL LEVELS]:** Participants receive "warm food and cold beverage" after the event, plus "a post ride dip in the lake" is available. Registration includes "a Souvenir T shirt, support on course (mechanicals / flats), several aid stations on course, results with a time and draw prizes, plus overall top 3 prizes in each event." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/7840/register-now-for-the-cowichan-crusher-and-save and https://www.granfondoguide.com/RaceDashboard/BCTripleCrownOfGravelSeries/1/7954/record-300-participants-ride-the-7th-annual-cowichan-crusher-gravel-fondo-)

**Recognition:** Event rated by Strambecco as "one of the Best Gravel Cycling Events in the Northwest and one of the Best Cycling Race Events in Canada across all categories." Described as "Vancouver Island's Premier and Oldest Gravel Fondo." (https://strambecco.com/event/cowichan-crusher/ and https://cowichancrusher.com/)

**Competition format [ALL LEVELS]:** Prize categories include "men and women, individual, teams of 2 and teams of 4 participants" with combined time determining team results. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/7840/register-now-for-the-cowichan-crusher-and-save)
