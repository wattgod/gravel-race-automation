# DIRTY HECTOR — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Race Overview & Key Details
**Location:** Trumansburg, NY (Hector area, Finger Lakes National Forest)  
**Distance:** Approximately 40 miles  
**Elevation Gain:** 3,500-3,700 feet  
**Start:** Silver Queen Farm, 5286 Stillwell Rd, Trumansburg, NY 14886  
**Start Time:** 11:00 AM mass start (all categories together)  
**Aid Stations:** One at approximately halfway point with water and Flow Formulas electrolyte/race mix

The race is described as "NYS Gravel State Championship race" and features a challenging mixed-terrain course through the Finger Lakes National Forest.

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)
The course is explicitly described as **not a gravel "road race"** across multiple sources. Key terrain features include:

- Beautiful NY dirt roads
- Technical trail sections
- Mud
- Grass
- Water crossings
- Fence crossings
- Cow fields
- Creeks
- Forests
- "Gently rolling terrain with one or two short hills" (though 3,700' gain suggests more than this)

Multiple sources characterize it as **"gravel meets cyclocross...with lots of hills"** (https://cycle-cny.com/event/dirty-hector/ and https://fingerlakescycling.org/event/dirty-hector-gravel-race-3/)

One description calls it "CHALLENGING," "HARD," and requiring "GRIT to get through" while also being "BEAUTIFUL" and "one-of-a kind." It warns it "may be MUDDY" (https://cycle-cny.com/event/dirty-hector/)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

### Elite Finishing Times [ELITE]:
From the 2025 results (https://www.webscorer.com/racedetails?raceid=391470&did=519844):
- **Winner (Jack Bernhard):** 2:17:07 (17.5 mph average)
- **2nd place (Landon Wonderly):** 2:18:54 (17.3 mph)
- **3rd place (Nathan Yost):** 2:18:57 (17.3 mph)
- **Top 10 range:** 2:17:07 - 2:29:11

### Mid-Pack Finishing Times [COMPETITIVE/RECREATIONAL]:
- **20th place:** 2:40:35 (14.9 mph)
- **30th place:** 2:49:40 (14.1 mph)
- **40th place:** 2:58:04 (13.5 mph)
- **50th place:** 3:05:23 (12.9 mph)

The wide time spread (2:17 to 3:05+ for top 50) indicates significant technical and climbing challenges that separate the field.

## Course Navigation & Logistics
**GPS highly recommended** due to poor cell reception throughout the route. Course will be marked with signage, but downloaded route is essential. (https://www.bikereg.com/dirtyhector2026)

**Strava Route:** https://www.strava.com/routes/3226938286173883882

Roads are open to traffic with marshals at busier intersections.

## Community Feel & Atmosphere
Post-race amenities include:
- Babe's Burgers food truck
- Beer
- Ice cream at the farm
- Prizes 3-deep for each category

Multiple sources emphasize the event is "totally worth it" and recommend asking "anyone who raced last year" about the experience, suggesting strong word-of-mouth and return participation. (https://cycle-cny.com/event/dirty-hector/)

## Race Categories
The 2025 race had 131 finishers across multiple categories including:
- Open Men
- Open Women
- Masters Men 40+
- Masters Men 50+
- Masters Men 60+

All categories start together in a mass start format.
