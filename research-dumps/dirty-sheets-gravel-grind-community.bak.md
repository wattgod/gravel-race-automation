## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Surface Composition:** Approximately 90% gravel and 10% paved road, with most paved sections at the beginning of the route (https://www.mtbproject.com/trail/7012793/dirty-sheets-gravel-loop)

**Gravel Variability:** Mark Hurley [UNKNOWN] noted "Some gravel packed and other loose" - conditions vary throughout the route (https://www.mtbproject.com/trail/7012793/dirty-sheets-gravel-loop)

**Key Navigation Points:**
- Paved section first: Turn right out of parking lot, travel uphill on Cochran Mill Road
- Mile 12: Turn left at South Fulton Parkway, go around gate, cut through trees, follow shoulder downhill for 0.2 miles
- After Rico Park: Gated Upper Wooten Road (public trail surrounded by private property)
- Upper Wooten Road section "can sometimes be wet"
- Bridge is out on Garretts Ferry Road but "easily passable on a bike" per Jim ONeil [UNKNOWN]
(https://www.mtbproject.com/trail/7012793/dirty-sheets-gravel-loop)

**Hills:** Kevin Akers [UNKNOWN] described "Lots of hills that are brutal when you're tired" (https://www.mtbproject.com/trail/7012793/dirty-sheets-gravel-loop)

**Wildlife:** Chaz Pattillo [UNKNOWN] reported seeing an armadillo on the route (https://www.mtbproject.com/trail/7012793/dirty-sheets-gravel-loop)

**Distance Variations:** If all out-and-back gravel roads are included, distance increases to 33 miles from the base ~20-21 mile loop (https://www.mtbproject.com/trail/7012793/dirty-sheets-gravel-loop)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Description from organizers:** "It's not polished. It's not easy. But tucked between the beatdown climbs and muddy crossings, you'll find wide-open pastures, crooked fences, and big-sky moments that make the effort worth it. If you're into Type 2 fun and courses with bite, Dirty Sheets doesn't disappoint." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12001/dirty-sheets-gravel-grind)

**Name Origin:** This area is very popular with road riders in Metro Atlanta and is known as "Silk Sheets." The gravel version is "Dirty Sheets" - the unruly counterpart. As organizers describe it: "Think of it as Silk Sheets' unruly counterpart—less refined, more rugged, and 100% unapologetic." (https://www.mtbproject.com/trail/7012793/dirty-sheets-gravel-loop and https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12001/dirty-sheets-gravel-grind)

**2026 Event Additions:**
- Start/Finish moving to Serenbe flower fields
- Camping now available at MTB Atlanta HQ near start line with "fire pits, starry skies, and campfire stories that may or may not be true"
- Friday shakeout ride (20 miles starting 2pm)
- Sunday morning coffee ride (20-ish miles at 9am)
- Post-ride entertainment and drawings
(https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12001/dirty-sheets-gravel-grind)

**Awards:** Hand-thrown ceramic trophies from Grouchy Ass Pottery Studio for first place winners in 100 and 62-mile categories. Custom medals for second and third place. Organizers note: "the real prize: Ceramic Glory!!! Sure, cash is nice. But the real flex? A hand-thrown ceramic trophy" (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12001/dirty-sheets-gravel-grind)

**Post-Race:** Includes "enough to make you happy and then some" - post-ride meal (feast with vegetarian and meat options), drinks, entertainment. Each registered rider gets two trips through food line and three through beverage line. Kick-ass photography and video uploaded to MTB Atlanta site for free download (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12001/dirty-sheets-gravel-grind)

**Location Charm:** Mark Odendahl [UNKNOWN] captured the rural character: "Nobody around but me and the cows!" Harold Solomon [UNKNOWN] noted: "Rolling gravel in farm and wooded area. Remarkable to have this so close to Atlanta." (https://www.mtbproject.com/trail/7012793/dirty-sheets-gravel-loop)

**Side Trip Option:** Can make short side trip to Serenbe for lunch or coffee at the Blue Eyed Daisy (https://www.mtbproject.com/trail/7012793/dirty-sheets-gravel-loop)

**Proceeds:** Registration proceeds go to MTB Atlanta to build and maintain trails (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12001/dirty-sheets-gravel-grind)
