# DUNOON DIRT DASH - RAW RESEARCH DUMP

## OFFICIAL DATA

**2025 & 2026 Race Dates**

The Dunoon Dirt Dash runs as a two-day overnight bikepacking event:[1]
- **2025:** Saturday 27 to Sunday 28 September 2025[1]
- **2026:** Saturday 26 to Sunday 27 September 2026[2][3][4]

**Registration & Entry Fees**

For the 2026 event:[4]
- Early bird (until 23 Sep 2026 23:59): £94.00
- Standard: £99.00
- Registration opened: 1 November 2025
- Total field size: 100 places[4]

For the 2025 event, pricing started from £80[6], with prices ranging from £90-£99 across different Dirt Dash events that year[6].

Entry fee includes:[4]
- Route as GPX for navigation
- Electronic rider briefing
- Campsite accommodation
- Evening meal
- Breakfast
- Broom wagon support

**Start Location & Time**

Start: Saturday 9:00 AM at Argyll Hotel, Dunoon[1][4]
Finish: Sunday afternoon/evening at Argyll Hotel[1]
Registration is open Saturday from 7:30 AM – 8:45 AM at Argyll Hotel, with mandatory rider briefing at 8:45 AM[1].

**Distance & Elevation**

- **Day 1:** 65km with approximately 1,300m of climbing[1][4]
- **Day 2:** 70km with approximately 1,000m of climbing[1][4]
- **Total:** 135km over two days with approximately 2,300m elevation gain[1][4]

Note: The user's known info indicates 45 miles and 3,000 ft elevation. The actual event data shows approximately 84 miles (135km) and 7,500 ft elevation gain—significantly longer and more demanding than the stated known info.

**Nearest Airport**

**Lodging & Getting There**

**By Train:** Dunoon has no train station. Riders can take a train to Gourock and connect via ferry to Dunoon. Check Scotrail website for timetables and Calmac website for ferry times[4].

**Event Accommodation:** The entry fee includes campsite accommodation with meals (dinner and breakfast)[4].

**Additional Note:** The search results reference that for other Dirt Dash events (Cateran), accommodation options include staying at Forillon, the home of Bikepacking Scotland, and bike rentals are available from Alyth Cyclery[6].
