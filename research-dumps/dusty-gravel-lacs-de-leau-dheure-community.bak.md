# DUSTY GRAVEL - LACS DE L'EAU D'HEURE — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Event Overview & Logistics

The Dusty Gravel des Lacs de l'Eau d'Heure takes place on **Saturday, April 19, 2025**, as part of the Gran Fondo Belgium weekend (with the Gran Fondo occurring on April 20). The event is the **second round of the Dusty Gravel Series**, a gravel challenge series running from April to September across Belgium.

**Distances offered:**
- Long distance: 110km with 1,500m elevation gain
- Short distance: 70km with 1,000m elevation gain

**Start location:** Route de la Plate Taille 99, 6440 Froidchapelle, Belgium (Lacs de l'Eau d'Heure area)

**Start times:**
- 70km: 9:00 AM
- 110km: 9:30 AM

The event is described as "suitable for all enthusiasts" and promises "a challenge that will live up to your expectations, whether you're looking for performance or simply pleasure on two wheels." Participants join approximately 3,000 cyclists taking part over the Gran Fondo Belgium weekend. (https://www.dustygravelseries.be/en/grandfondo, https://www.godare.events/en/events/gravel-ride-calendar/dusty-gravel-lacs-de-l-eau-d-heure)

**Registration:** Opened January 23, 2025, with pre-registration available starting January 22. (https://www.godare.events/en/events/gravel-ride-calendar/dusty-gravel-lacs-de-l-eau-d-heure)

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

The race takes place around **Les Lacs de l'Eau d'Heure**, which is described as Belgium's largest lake system and "the longest coastline in Belgium." The area is particularly well-known among motorcyclists as a scenic destination in the Entre Sambre-Et-Meuse region. (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dVD1uN8EmsI, https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lhQGkfycugI)

The terrain is characterized as **"moderate terrain with rolling short steep hills or moderate, sustained climbs"** according to the Gran Fondo Guide difficulty rating. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12726/dusty-les-lacs-de-leau-dheure)

One rider video description notes the 75km route explores "chemins et sentiers cachés" (hidden paths and trails), suggesting the course ventures onto less-traveled gravel roads and trails around the lake area. (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XTQ8R__4rNY)

The region features "très beaux points de vue sur la région" (very beautiful viewpoints over the region), indicating scenic overlooks are part of the route experience. (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7BFBtdEOkVE)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

The event is positioned as having a **"friendly atmosphere"** where cyclists share "unique moments" during the Gran Fondo Belgium weekend. (https://www.dustygravelseries.be/en/grandfondo)

As part of the **Dusty Gravel Series**, riders can participate in multiple events throughout the season (April to September), accumulate points, and climb an official leaderboard. The series is described as "open to all gravel enthusiasts, whether novices or experienced." (https://www.godare.events/en/events/gravel-ride-calendar/dusty-gravel-lacs-de-l-eau-d-heure)

The weekend format combines both gravel and Gran Fondo road cycling events, creating a larger cycling festival atmosphere with approximately 3,000 total participants across both days.
