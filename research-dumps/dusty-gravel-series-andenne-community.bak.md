## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Opening climb:** Race starts with approximately 1km through town, then immediately hits an 8km climb that is very steep in the first 2km. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

**Technical difficulty:** The course features surfaces that "teilweise nichts mehr mit einem Gravelparcours zu tun hatten" (partially had nothing to do with a gravel course anymore). Includes singletrack sections where riders needed to be careful not to go off course or risk falling 1 meter into a ditch. Belgian course design described as "schmerzfreier" (more pain-tolerant/extreme) than typical gravel courses. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

**Course location:** The route takes riders through the Condroz Namurois and the Mosane valley. (https://www.dustygravelseries.be/ and https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OEbIUS7eOIU)

**Distance options:** Two route choices available - 75km with 1000m elevation and 100km with 1400m elevation. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

## DNF Risk Factors

**Mechanical issues:** "Das Rennen war von zahlreichen Defekten übersät. Immer wieder hatten Fahrer durch die sehr raue Strecke mit Reifenpannen zu kämpfen." (The race was riddled with numerous mechanicals. Riders repeatedly struggled with tire punctures due to the very rough course.) (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

**Technical skill requirements:** Riders lacking experience on technical courses may struggle significantly. The course requires MTB-like handling skills on singletrack sections. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Belgian event culture:** "Es war alles wie immer: belgische Gelassenheit herrschte vor. Die Startunterlagen gab es gut eine Stunde vor dem Start und die Hektik die man von deutschen Jedermannrennen kennt kam nicht ansatzweise auf." (Everything was as always: Belgian calmness prevailed. Race packets were available a good hour before the start and the hectic nature known from German amateur races didn't come up at all.) (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

**Event context:** Part of the Belgium Bike Festival, which includes MTB, cyclo, gravel, demonstrations, exhibitions, and tests - described as "a 100% bike event for lovers of two wheels." (https://www.dustygravelseries.be/)

**Participation:** Approximately 250 participants at the start, divided between the two distance options. Only 5 German riders participated. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

**Race results (2025):** Won by Lukas Malezsewski, followed by Adne Koster and Michiel van Vliet. (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OEbIUS7eOIU)

**Series format:** Part of a 3-race series across Wallonia (April, July, August) with points-based rankings for those participating in multiple events. Described as "made for riders who are looking for the ultimate in quality." (https://www.dustygravelseries.be/)

**Travel recommendation:** "If you're local to Belgium/Europe—yes, it's solid. If you're traveling internationally—skip it." (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OEbIUS7eOIU)
