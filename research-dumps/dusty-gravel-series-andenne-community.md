## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course Profile (100km):** 1,400m elevation gain over 100km distance. 75km option has 1,000m elevation. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

**Opening Climb:** Approximately 1km through town, then 8km climb with the first 2km being very steep. This happens immediately from a cold start. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

**Technical Sections:** Course includes singletrack with narrow lines and 1-meter drops into ditches if riders go off course. These sections require focus and technical skill. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

**Surface Variety:** Mixed surfaces that go beyond typical gravel, described as difficult to ride even on a gravel bike. Belgian course design is noted as being "pain-free" (schmerzfrei) in approach — meaning they don't shy away from challenging terrain. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

**Location:** Course takes riders through the Condroz Namurois and Mosane valley regions. (https://www.dustygravelseries.be/)

## DNF Risk Factors

**Mechanical Issues — Frequent:** "Das Rennen war von zahlreichen Defekten übersät. Immer wieder hatten Fahrer durch die sehr raue Strecke mit Reifenpannen zu kämpfen." [Race was riddled with mechanicals. Riders repeatedly struggled with tire punctures due to the very rough course.] (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

**Technical Skill Requirements:** Lack of technical experience on challenging terrain was noted as limiting factor for going fast. Course requires skill to navigate safely and efficiently. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Belgian Relaxed Vibe:** "Es war alles wie immer: belgische Gelassenheit herrschte vor. Die Startunterlagen gab es gut eine Stunde vor dem Start und die Hektik die man von deutschen Jedermannrennen kennt kam nicht ansatzweise auf." [Belgian calmness prevailed. Race materials available just an hour before start. None of the hectic stress typical of German amateur races.] (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

**Part of Larger Festival:** The Dusty Andenne is integrated into the Belgium Bike Festival, which includes MTB, cyclo, gravel, demonstrations, exhibitions, and bike tests — a 100% bike event for two-wheel enthusiasts. (https://www.dustygravelseries.be/)

**Field Size (2025 Andenne):** Approximately 250 participants across both distance options. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

**Limited German Participation:** Only 5 German riders participated in the 2025 Andenne race. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

**Winners (2025 Andenne):** Lukas Malezsewski won, followed by Adne Koster and Michiel van Vliet. (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OEbIUS7eOIU)

**Series Format:** Part of a three-race series across Wallonia (April-August), with points accumulation for overall series rankings. Riders compete for both individual race results and series challenge rankings. (https://www.dustygravelseries.be/)

## Race Logistics & Practical Details

**Start Time Reliability:** Scheduled 9:00 AM start actually went at 9:30 AM with no clear explanation for the delay. Plan for Belgian flexibility. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

**Registration Timing:** Race materials/numbers available about one hour before scheduled start. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/04/15/dusty-gravel-series-belgien-rennbericht/)

**Travel Recommendation:** "If you're local to Belgium/Europe—yes, it's solid. If you're traveling internationally—skip it." (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OEbIUS7eOIU)
