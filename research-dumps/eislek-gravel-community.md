## Equipment & Gear Recommendations

(https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/eislek-gravel-luxoland-2025.200322/)

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course Profile:** The long distance route is 115-117km with approximately 1,820-2,000m of elevation gain. The course consists of a 46.8km circuit made up of two parts: the Luxembourg loop (23.5-25km) and the German loop. Younger categories do 2.5 laps while older age groups (W50+, M60+) complete 1.5 laps on a 70km course with 1,100m+ elevation. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/vianden/)

**Opening Climb:** "Right from the start in Vianden, you'll feel the history and the challenge. The loop kicks off with an amazing uphill ride over cobblestones that takes you to Vianden Castle." The first 8-10km climbs from 200m to 510m elevation (highest point), with sections exceeding 10% gradient, especially in the first kilometer. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/vianden/)

**Technical Descent:** Loretta69 describes a "grobes Geröll" (coarse gravel/scree) descent of 400-500m marked as "Descent dangereuse!" with a rideable right-hand line and a rougher left "Express-Line" for skilled riders with wider tires. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/eislek-gravel-luxoland-2025.200322/)

**River Section:** Final 10km of each 24km lap runs flat along the Our River with single-track sections where overtaking is impossible, potentially problematic when faster riders catch slower ones. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/eislek-gravel-luxoland-2025.200322/)

**Surface Composition:** Course terrain is "40% forest roads, 40% gravel roads, and 20% paved roads" according to the Wish One Millau race description in the UCI series. (https://www.cyclingnews.com/races/uci-gravel-world-series-events-2025/)

**German Loop:** Described as having "softer landscapes, less demanding gradients, and a smoother rhythm" with long sections under tree canopies. After completing the German Loop, riders cross back into Luxembourg along a greenway and elevated path overlooking the Our River. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12352/eislek-gravel-luxembourg)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**eins4eins [UNKNOWN]:** "Muss nur aufpassen am ersten Anstieg nicht zu überpacen und mein Ding zu machen. Da kann man leicht zu tief gehen und dann wird das ein richtig langer, harter Tag." (Must be careful not to overpace on the first climb and do my own thing. You can easily go too deep there and then it becomes a really long, hard day.) (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/eislek-gravel-luxoland-2025.200322/)

**Elite Results [ELITE]:** Men's winner Mathijs Loman finished 115km in 3:27:40 (approximately 33 km/h average) in a three-way sprint with Petr Vakoc and Mees Hendrikx. Women's winner Rosa Kloser rode solo to finish in 3:49:50, with second place Larissa Hartog finishing 5:29 back at 3:55:19. (https://granfondodailynews.com/2025/06/27/luxembourg-hosts-uci-gravel-worlds-qualifying-eislek-race-results/)
