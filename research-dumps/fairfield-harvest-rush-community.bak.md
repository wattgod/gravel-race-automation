# FAIRFIELD HARVEST RUSH — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Race Overview & Key Facts

The Fairfield Harvest Rush is part of the Iowa Gravel Series and serves as **the championship race for the series**. The race is held in early October (October 4, 2025) in Fairfield, Iowa, starting at 6:30-7:30 AM.

**Distance Options:**
- 30 mile fun ride
- 50K/60 mile fun ride  
- 100K (62 miles)
- 100 mile race (120 miles mentioned in some sources)

**Prize Purse:** $1,000 cash prize for top male AND top female rider in the 120-mile race, plus prizes for fun ride winners and cash purse for podium finishers (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10605/harvest-rush-gravel-grinder, https://iowabicyclecoalition.org/event/fairfield-harvest-rush-iowa-gravel-series/)

**Start/Finish Location:** Fishback and Stephenson Cider House, 1949 Pleasant Plain Road, Fairfield, IA

**Registration Pricing:**
- $40 - Grassroots registration (includes chip timing, marked course, pro photography, aid stations)
- $60 - Lunch & shirt option (same benefits plus lunch and Iowa Gravel Series shirt)
- $399 - Full season pass for all 7 Iowa Gravel Series races (includes t-shirt and jersey) (https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/16029/fairfield-harvest-rush)

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

Jefferson County in SE Iowa has **"more dirt roads than any neighboring county"** according to race organizers (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10605/harvest-rush-gravel-grinder)

The terrain is described as **"Difficult terrain with longer, steeper hills"** (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10605/harvest-rush-gravel-grinder)

Race features "backroads of Jefferson County in SE Iowa" with "lots of gravel to grind on" (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10605/harvest-rush-gravel-grinder)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Gabe Hobbie [ELITE]** took second place in the 100k at the 2024 edition and was featured on the Iowa Gravel Series Podium Podcast discussing his race experience (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y9mzF5TYcZE, https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tLcgr-94hTg)

The race is described as **"friendly to both new and experienced racers"** with emphasis that "you can invite your family or buddy that's new to gravel grinding or group riding and know the route will be well marked and the people friendly" (https://www.fairfieldiowa.com/calendar/harvest_rush_gravel_grinder/)

The community is noted as being "so excited to invite gravel adventurers from all over the midwest and the world" with "lots of support from the community" (https://iowabicyclecoalition.org/event/fairfield-harvest-rush-iowa-gravel-series/)

Fairfield is described as "unique" by race organizers, who emphasize they "plan to make it a big deal" as the series championship race (https://www.racecenter.com/cycling/fairfield-harvest-rush)

The finish venue at Cider House is described as a "top-notch venue" and "premier place to host" (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10605/harvest-rush-gravel-grinder)

## Race Requirements & Support

**Mandatory Equipment:** Helmets and lights/flashers are mandatory and will be checked at the start (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10605/harvest-rush-gravel-grinder)

**Support Provided:**
- SAG support - "if you break down or just can't finish the route call our SAG team and they will come rescue you"
- Water & snack stations along each route
- Swag bag for all registered riders
- Chip timing
- Pro photography
- Well-marked course (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10605/harvest-rush-gravel-grinder, https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/16029/fairfield-harvest-rush)

**Day-of Registration:** Available with cash or card (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10605/harvest-rush-gravel-grinder)

## Iowa Gravel Series Context

The Harvest Rush is part of a 7-race Iowa Gravel Series that includes:
1. Kalona Horseshoe - May 10th
2. Silver City Century - May 17th
3. Waukon 100 - June 21st
4. Albia Holy Cow - July 12th
5. Glenwood Superbloom - August 9th
6. Soldier Cutoff Hillduro in Pisgah - September 20th
7. Fairfield Harvest Rush - Sept. 28th (listed as championship race)

Full season pass includes limited edition jersey designed by Rad Custom Cycling and all meals at every race (https://www.fairfieldiowa.com/calendar/harvest_rush_gravel_grinder/)
