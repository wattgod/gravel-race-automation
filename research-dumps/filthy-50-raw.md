## OFFICIAL DATA

**Race Name:** Filthy 50 [Official] (https://www.filthy50.com)  
**Location:** Minnesota (route varies—northern Minnesota gravel)  
**Date:** Early October annually [Official] (https://www.filthy50.com)  
**Distance:** 50 miles [Official] (https://www.filthy50.com)  
**Elevation Gain:** ~3,000 ft (varies by year) [Official] (https://www.filthy50.com)
