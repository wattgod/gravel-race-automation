# FLANDERS LEGACY GRAVEL — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Race Overview & Key Facts

**First Edition:** October 19, 2025 in Leuven, Belgium
**Event Type:** UCI Gravel World Series qualifier for 2026 World Championships
**Total Participants:** Approximately 1,200 riders registered for inaugural edition

**Course Details:**
- 48km loop starting in Heverlee, finishing in Leuven city center
- Elite/younger age groups: 3 laps (147km total, 950m elevation gain)
- Men 60+ and women 50+: 2 laps (96km total, 610m elevation gain)
- Approximately 60% of route follows 2024 UCI Gravel World Championships local lap
- New section through Wallonia added
- Alternative finishing section near Leuven

**Terrain Characteristics:** "Rolling hills, with a total elevation gain of 950m (or 610m for older categories), but the inclines are never very steep" (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/legacy-gravel/)

**Difficulty Rating:** "Moderate terrain with rolling short steep hills or moderate, sustained climbs" (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12363/legacy-gravel)

## Course & Terrain Strategy

**Course Composition:** Uses "big parts of the course of last year's World Championships with start in Heverlee and finish in Leuven on a new location. The course is for 60% the local lap of last year with an add-on in Wallonia and a new final towards the finish." (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/flanders-legacy-gravel-builds-further-on-2024-uci-gravel-worlds/)

**Elevation Profile [ALL LEVELS]:**
- 48km loop design keeps riders returning to familiar sections
- 950m total climbing spread across 147km = averaging ~6.5m per km
- "Rolling hills...but the inclines are never very steep" suggests sustained rather than punchy climbing
- No single major climb identified; elevation distributed throughout

## Event Context & Significance

**Timing & Legacy Connection:**
"The first qualifier event one week past the very successful 2025 Bolero UCI Gravel World Championships will take place on big parts of the course of last year's World Championships" (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/flanders-legacy-gravel-builds-further-on-2024-uci-gravel-worlds/)

**Historical Significance:**
"The province of Vlaams-Brabant were host of the 2024 UCI Gravel World Championships and 2023 UEC European Gravel Championships. Both successful events have now led to a new legacy event" (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12363/legacy-gravel)

**Series Integration:**
Part of Bolero Gravel Series and serves as "the grand finale of this series" - also "an official qualifying race for the 2026 UCI Gravel World Championships" (https://flanderslegacygravel.be/en/)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Event Positioning:**
"Sign up for Flanders Legacy Gravel and relive the atmosphere, the challenge, and the scenery that the pros faced during the 2024 Gravel World Championships in Leuven!" (https://flanderslegacygravel.be/en/)

**Location Prestige:**
"Flanders Legacy Gravel takes you along the most beautiful gravel sectors in and around Leuven, a region that gained worldwide cycling fame thanks to the 2021 Road World Championships and the 2024 Gravel World Championships" (https://flanderslegacygravel.be/en/)

**Scale & Organization:**
1,200 registered riders for inaugural edition indicates strong interest. Event supported by professional photo services (Sportograf) and sponsored by Bolero (KBC's investment platform).
