# GLENGARRY GAMES GRAVEL RUN — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Event Overview & Unique Features

The Glengarry Games Gravel Run is held in partnership with the historic Glengarry Highland Games in Maxville, Ontario. The event features a distinctive finish: "a 3/4 lap of an oval track in front of the giant covered grandstands in front of hundreds of spectators" (https://gravelcup.com/gravel-cup-events/glengarry-gravel-run). This spectator-friendly finish sets it apart from most gravel events.

The event runs three distance categories:
- **Epic Course**: 114km (2025) / 112km (2024)
- **Medio Course**: 83km
- **Petit Course**: 42km

Start times are staggered: Epic at 9:00 AM, Medio at 9:30 AM, and Petit at 9:45 AM.

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

The courses use "rolling gravel roads around Maxville in the United Counties of Stormont, Dundas & Glengarry" (https://gravelcup.com/gravel-cup-events/glengarry-gravel-run).

Described as "moderate terrain with rolling short steep hills or moderate, sustained climbs" (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12530/glengarry-games-gravel-run).

The Epic course includes "over 750 m of elevation gain" based on comparison with similar regional events (https://northernontario.travel/cycling/ontario-gravel-bike-events).

## Race Logistics & Support

**Services Provided:**
- Route signposted
- First aid available
- Emergency contact support
- Roving mechanical support
- Course timing with results within 24 hours
- Photos available
- Downloadable GPS files (Strava, GPX, RWGPS formats)
- Finish line chute
- Nutrition feed/aid stations
- Post-event meal provided as part of entry
- After party

**NOT provided:**
- SAG wagon
- Outriders escort/police escort

The course is "not closed to traffic" (https://northernontario.travel/cycling/ontario-gravel-bike-events).

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

The event finishes "in front of the giant covered grandstands in front of hundreds of spectators" at the Glengarry Highland Games, providing a unique atmosphere for a gravel event (https://gravelcup.com/gravel-cup-events/glengarry-gravel-run).

The event is part of the Gravel Cup Canada series, indicating it's part of a recognized regional gravel racing circuit (https://gravelcup.com/gravel-cup-events/glengarry-gravel-run).

Local cycling clubs are well-represented, with strong participation from Ottawa Bicycle Club, Nine2FivePro Cycling Team, Ride with Rendall p/b Biemme, and Cornwall Cycle Club, suggesting a strong local community following.
