# GLENWOOD GRAVEL — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

The 2020 course featured **freshly graded dirt roads** according to a pre-race preview video (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UJK4f8LKmtM)

The race is described as **"moderate terrain with rolling short steep hills or moderate, sustained climbs"** (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/13166/glenwood-gravel)

The 2025 edition was marketed with the concept of **"taking it home" and rethinking what a gravel race might be if you decided to race or ride around your own home**, suggesting a more local, community-focused course design with "surprise" features (https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/16028/glenwood-gravel)

## Race Details & Format

**Distance:** 100k / 62 miles (also mentioned: 50km option available)

**Race conditions (2020):** Clear, 67 degrees, wind 17 mph (https://results.bikereg.com/?results/12609)

**Winning time (2020) [ELITE]:** Lucas Barloon (BIKEIOWA Racing Team) won in 2:41:34

**Mid-pack times (2020) [UNKNOWN]:** Times ranged widely from the 2:41 winner to 7:26+ for back-of-pack finishers, with the median finisher around 4 hours

**Start time:** 7:30 AM (https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/16028/glenwood-gravel)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

The race is described as **"the one that started it all"** for the Iowa Gravel Series, suggesting it has foundational status in the local gravel scene (https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/16028/glenwood-gravel)

The event aims to **"make this a great day in the saddle for everyone"** with an inclusive philosophy (https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/16028/glenwood-gravel)

**Team participation was strong:** Multiple team entries visible in 2020 results including Team Struggle Bus (5 riders finishing together around 3:53), Team Rocket (4 riders at 4:10:55), Cycling Sloths (6 riders), and numerous other team entries suggesting a social/group ride culture (https://results.bikereg.com/?results/12609)

Creative team names suggest a **fun, grassroots atmosphere**: "Perineum Punishers," "Team Crowman," "Randonneuring," "Stray Dogs," "#gotheextramile" (https://results.bikereg.com/?results/12609)

The race is part of the **Iowa Gravel Series** (7-race series founded by Chris McQueen after the success of Glenwood Gravel Grinder), with full season passes available (https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/16028/glenwood-gravel and https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nefz6ymJKSw)
