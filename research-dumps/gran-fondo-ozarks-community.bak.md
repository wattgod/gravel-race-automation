## Related Ozarks Gravel Event Found: Randonneuring Gravel Rally of the Ozarks

The sources contain information about a **different** event in the Ozarks region:

### Event Overview [UNKNOWN LEVEL]
**Randonneuring Gravel Rally of the Ozarks** - March 7, 2026, Mountain Home, Arkansas. This is a team-based navigation event, NOT a traditional gran fondo race. Teams of two (or solo riders) navigate three separate ~20-mile gravel routes using one vehicle to shuttle between start points. (https://www.trifind.com/re_793140/RandonneuringGravelRallyoftheOzarks)

### Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**2025 Routes [UNKNOWN LEVEL]:**
- **Yellville route:** 21.2 miles, 2,280 ft elevation gain, 85% gravel
- **Rea Valley route:** 20 miles, 1,897 ft elevation gain, 90% gravel, described as having "one hellacious hill in the middle"
- **Gamaliel route:** 23.4 miles, 2,088 ft elevation gain, 99.9% gravel
(https://www.trifind.com/re_793140/RandonneuringGravelRallyoftheOzarks)

### Race Strategy & Pacing

**Format [UNKNOWN LEVEL]:** This is NOT a mass-start race. Teams strategically choose which of three routes to ride and in what order, shuttling bikes via vehicle between locations. 3:00 PM cutoff for starting a third route. Completion marked upon return to Rapp's Barren Brewing in Mountain Home. (https://www.trifind.com/re_793140/RandonneuringGravelRallyoftheOzarks)

### Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Venue [UNKNOWN LEVEL]:** Pre-event meet & greet and post-event festivities at Rapp's Barren Brewing Co., Mountain Home, AR. Friday evening social 7:00-9:00 PM, Saturday 6:00 AM start. (https://www.trifind.com/re_793140/RandonneuringGravelRallyoftheOzarks)
