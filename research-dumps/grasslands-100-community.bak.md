# GRASSLANDS 100 — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

The race features **flat, non-technical trail course** through the Caddo and Lyndon B. Johnson National Grasslands near Decatur, Texas. The terrain includes:

- **Challenging trail running conditions** requiring runners to cross streams, run through muddy bogs, sand, and underbrush (https://www.halfmarathons.net/races/grasslands-trail-run-100-mile-50-mile-marathon-half-marathon-15k/)

- **Rolling grasslands with moderate elevation** — described as "100-mile gravel race in Decatur, Texas—rolling grasslands with moderate elevation" (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D7mBGtpYxu4)

- **Course layout**: Out-and-back loop course starting/finishing at TADRA Point Camping Area (intersection of FS Road 900 and FS 904), divided into four loop sections marked by colors: Yellow, Blue, Red, and White (https://www.halfmarathons.net/races/grasslands-trail-run-100-mile-50-mile-marathon-half-marathon-15k/)

- The course is noted as having **"potential" for fast times "with the right conditions and athletes"** and has historically been **"a fast race"** where "many runners have earned a PR on the flat, non-technical course" (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D7mBGtpYxu4)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

- **Remote Texas backcountry setting**: Over 20,000 acres in the Caddo and Lyndon B. Johnson National Grasslands, an area popular for wildlife viewing, hunting, fishing, mountain biking, camping, and horseback riding (https://www.halfmarathons.net/races/grasslands-trail-run-100-mile-50-mile-marathon-half-marathon-15k/)

- **No amenities on course**: "No water sources or electricity available in the grasslands" — organizers note participants need to bring water for start and finish line refreshment, though water and sports drinks are provided at aid stations (https://www.halfmarathons.net/races/grasslands-trail-run-100-mile-50-mile-marathon-half-marathon-15k/)

- **Regional field size**: 100-500 participants, with "a regional field" competing (https://www.halfmarathons.net/races/grasslands-trail-run-100-mile-50-mile-marathon-half-marathon-15k/ and https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D7mBGtpYxu4)

- **Multi-distance event**: Includes 100-mile, 50-mile, marathon, half marathon, and 15K options with staggered starts (https://www.halfmarathons.net/races/grasslands-trail-run-100-mile-50-mile-marathon-half-marathon-15k/)

- Race organized by **Blaze Trails** (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D7mBGtpYxu4)

- The 100-mile distance was added after 25 years of the race existing, with organizers noting "The course had potential with the right conditions and athletes" (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D7mBGtpYxu4)
