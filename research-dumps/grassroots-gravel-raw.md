## Race History & Community

**Founded & Founder**
- **Founder:** Adam Davidson (founder & promoter)[5]
- **Family-owned, Pueblo, Colorado-based organization**[4]
- **Launch:** Grassroots Gravel was created following the departure of Gravel Locos Pueblo[5]

**Predecessor Event**
Gravel Locos Pueblo ran in 2022-2023 with Grassroots Gravel taking over in Pueblo starting in 2024. Per Adam Davidson: "Gravel Locos inspired the region with a world-class gravel event and highlighted just how good this landscape is for gravel cycling. We worked closely with Fabian and the Gravel Locos team in bringing their 2022 Pueblo event to life."[5]

**2025 Performance**
"2025 was our biggest and best year yet," per the official website[1].

**Community Focus**
The race emphasizes inclusivity with:[1]
- Women, Gender Expansive, Men, Tandem categories

- **Ride Together Grant:** Provides 10 free registrations, travel expense support, and equipment assistance to support accessibility[6]
- Grant partners: Hammerhead, Flow Formulas, Twisted Spoke CBD, Squirt Cycling Products[6]
