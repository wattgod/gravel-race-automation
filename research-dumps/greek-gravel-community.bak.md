## Rider Quotes & Race Reports

### Rally Car Racing (Not Cycling)

**Kalle Rovanperä [ELITE - WRC Driver]:** "It was really enjoyable for us on the first loop of stages today. They were four difficult stages and I was a bit surprised that the time gaps were so big, but I also felt that we did a good job in the car... There is still a long way to go to the end of the rally, so let's see what we can do tomorrow." (https://speedcafe.com/rovanpera-surprised-by-dominance-on-greek-gravel/)

**Jari-Matti Latvala [ELITE - Team Principal]:** "Greece can suit our drivers well, as the roads there are a bit wider and faster than in Sardinia, and opening the road is usually not quite as tough. Still, there will be some cleaning to do for Elfyn, Seb and Kalle, and they will need a very good Friday to keep themselves in the fight for the rest of the rally." (https://tgr-wrt.com/greek-gravel-classic-the-next-test-for-toyota-gazoo-racing/)

**Elfyn Evans [ELITE - WRC Driver]:** "The Acropolis is another of the tougher gravel rallies on the calendar with a hard and rocky base and a lot of loose rocks. We're going there at a different time of year to usual, so it has the potential to be very hot and demanding for the cars and the tyres." (https://media.toyota.co.uk/greek-gravel-classic-the-next-test-for-toyota-gazoo-racing/)

**Sébastien Ogier [ELITE - WRC Driver]:** "The challenges in Greece are not so different, with high temperatures and a rough and rocky surface, and hopefully we can be strong once more in these conditions." (https://www.gr-zoo.com/threads/greek-gravel-classic-the-next-test-for-toyota-gazoo-racing.8838/)

## Weather Experienced

**2025 Acropolis Rally:** The rally moved to June 26-29 (from its traditional September date), bringing "the possibility of harsher conditions for cars, tyres and drivers" with "high temperatures" and "searing heat" during "the middle of summer." (https://tgr-wrt.com/greek-gravel-classic-the-next-test-for-toyota-gazoo-racing/)

**2025 EHRC Round:** Race took place in early September with "trademark mix of searing heat, rocky gravel, and legendary stages." (https://www.fia.com/news/jari-matti-latvala-claims-victory-greek-gravel)

**August Test Session:** Katerina Bratsou noted "The last day was really exhausting for all, as the temperature was high enough" during Toyota's pre-event testing in August. (https://rallydiaries.eu/en/toyota-gazoo-racing-wrc-test-greek-gravel)

## Terrain Details (Rally Driver Perspective)

**Surface Characteristics:** "A hard and rocky base and a lot of loose rocks" with "high temperatures and a rough and rocky surface." The Greek gravel is described as "demanding" with roads that are "a bit wider and faster than in Sardinia." (https://media.toyota.co.uk/greek-gravel-classic-the-next-test-for-toyota-gazoo-racing/)

**Technical Sections:** The Caroutes area features "lot of technical sections" and is "really close to nature" with no communication networks available. (https://rallydiaries.eu/en/toyota-gazoo-racing-wrc-test-greek-gravel)

**Classic Stages:** The Eleftherohori stage is described as "a classic Acropolis Rally special stage." (https://www.reddit.com/r/rally/comments/14ovffv/today_was_rally_day/)

**Stage Details (2025 Route):**
- Friday includes the Stiri stage, "last driven in 1997"
- Saturday is "the rally's longest day"
- Sunday features "the brand-new Smokovo and well-known Tarzan" stages
- Route includes stages around Loutraki, south of Lamia service park
(https://www.miamicars.com/news/syndicated-news/acropolis-rally-greece-previewgreek-gravel-classic-the-next-test-for-toyota-gazoo-racing/)

## Race Strategy & Pacing (Rally Car Racing)

**[ELITE - WRC Level]** Rovanperä's tire strategy: "For the second loop we made some set-up changes to gain a bit of protection for the rough conditions, and we lost quite a bit of traction, but it was still quite good, and we were being wise and not doing anything stupid." He also noted having "a bit of a different tyre choice to the others and that was good for us: overall I just had a bit more confidence on the hard tyres." (https://speedcafe.com/rovanpera-surprised-by-dominance-on-greek-gravel/)

**Road Position Strategy [ELITE]:** "Opening the road is usually not quite as tough" in Greece compared to other rallies, but "there will be some cleaning to do" for early starters who "will need a very good Friday to keep themselves in the fight for the rest of the rally." (https://www.gr-zoo.com/threads/greek-gravel-classic-the-next-test-for-toyota-gazoo-racing.8820/)

## DNF Risk Factors

**Mechanical Issues:** Belgian driver Ghislain de Mevius "looked set for" a podium position "until their Mazda 323 GTX succumbed to mechanical issues on day two." (https://www.fia.com/news/jari-matti-latvala-claims-victory-greek-gravel)

**Mid-Rally Scares:** Latvala and Hussi experienced "a scare on SS5 when a sudden misfire threatened to derail their charge. A swift fix by the JML-WRT service crew ensured their rally stayed on track." (https://www.fia.com/news/jari-matti-latvala-claims-victory-greek-gravel)

**Transmission Failures:** German crew Siegfried and Renate Mayr "overcame a transmission failure thanks to spare parts generously loaned overnight by fellow competitor Pasi Hapulahti." (https://www.fia.com/news/jari-matti-latvala-claims-victory-greek-gravel)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Spectator Enthusiasm:** Katerina Bratsou observed: "Spectators got their 'dose' four days in row. Their presence in a month which most people spend on beach, declares the lack of international races which our country used to host." (https://rallydiaries.eu/en/toyota-gazoo-racing-wrc-test-greek-gravel)

**Local Support:** "Greek duo Marios Stafilopatis and George Chatzirigas delighted home fans with second overall" and "Local pairing Alexandrou Christodoulou and Athanassios Samaras also earned plaudits for their spirited run." (https://www.fia.com/news/jari-matti-latvala-claims-victory-greek-gravel)

**Historic Rally Spirit:** The Category 3 drama showed "true to the spirit of historic rallying" when competitors shared spare parts overnight to help others continue. (https://www.fia.com/news/jari-matti-latvala-claims-victory-greek-gravel)

**Cultural Significance:** The 2025 rally "returns to the Greek capital Athens for a start ceremony and opening super special in the heart of the city on Thursday evening" with Sunday finish "in the historic town of Delphi." (https://tgr-wrt.com/greek-gravel-classic-the-next-test-for-toyota-gazoo-racing/)
