# Grinduro France — Research Dump

## OFFICIAL DATA

**Race Name:** Grinduro France [Official] (https://www.grinduro.com/france)  
**Organizer:** Grinduro Series  
**Location:** Xonrupt-Longemer, Vosges Mountains, France  
**Date:** Late June (June 28-30, 2026) — Festival weekend [Official] (https://www.grinduro.com/france)  
**Distance:** 62 miles (~100 km) [Official] (https://www.grinduro.com/france)  
**Elevation Gain:** ~6,562 ft (2,000m) [Official] (https://www.grinduro.com/france)  
**Format:** Enduro-style (4 timed descent stages, untimed climbs) [Official] (https://www.grinduro.com)  
**Registration:** €100-€180 [Official] (https://www.grinduro.com/france)  
**Field Size:** 400-600 riders [Official] (https://www.grinduro.com/france)  
**New Venue:** Moved to Vosges in 2024 (previously Monts du Lyonnais) [Official] (https://www.grinduro.com/france)  
**Website:** https://www.grinduro.com/france  

## TERRAIN & COURSE

**Format:** Same as Grinduro Germany—only 4 timed descents, untimed climbs [Official] (https://www.grinduro.com)  
**Surface:** 50% gravel, 30% singletrack (timed stages), 20% paved [Official] (https://www.grinduro.com/france)  
**Vosges Mountains:** Lower altitude than Alps but steep, technical, forested terrain [Official] (https://www.grinduro.com/france)  

## WEATHER & CLIMATE

**Late June Vosges:** 55-75°F, 20% rain chance [Weather.com] (https://weather.com)  
**Historical:** 2024 (new venue): Warm, dry (65-75°F) [Reddit] (https://reddit.com/r/gravelcycling)
