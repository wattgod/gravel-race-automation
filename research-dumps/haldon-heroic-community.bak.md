## DNF Risk Factors

**Puncture Issues [UNKNOWN]:** Dibbs reported "by the end of the second stage my son and his mate had had 4 punctures between them, we'd run out of spare tubes and had to bale at 35 miles" - indicating the gravel/terrain can be harsh on tubes. Multiple punctures on a single ride suggest carrying extra tubes/repair capacity is critical. (https://singletrackworld.com/forum/bike-forum/haldon-heroic/)

**Navigation Challenges [UNKNOWN]:** One rider reported "I actually did 16.7 miles because I went the wrong way, up a rath..." on the 15-mile Micro route, adding 1.7 extra miles due to navigation error. (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-xPnmU_w2o8)

**Self-Navigation Required [UNKNOWN]:** "For 2024 we are fully marking the Micro route (which is also the start & finish of the longer routes). However the Mega & Medio routes are predominantly self nav." Riders must rely on GPX files and Komoot navigation for longer routes. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/6334/haldon-heroic)
