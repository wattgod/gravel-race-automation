## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course surface:** "It showcases the best gravel trails & bridleways in the Haldon Forest Area, Devon, UK" (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aMSegvkedW8)

**Navigation difficulty:** One rider went wrong on the Micro route despite it being "fully marked," adding 1.7 miles to what should have been a 15-mile course. (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-xPnmU_w2o8)

**Terrain classification:** Event is described as "Difficult terrain with longer, steeper hills" and characterized by community reviewers as "Hilly" (81% of reviews). (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/6334/haldon-heroic)

**Terrain suitability:** Described as "genuinely suitable for the average rider on a gravel bike without segueing too far into MTB territory" — suggesting technical difficulty is manageable for non-mountain bikers. (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aMSegvkedW8)

**Elevation profiles:**
- Mega route: 81km/50mi with 1170m elevation
- Medio route: 54km/34mi with 810m elevation  
- Micro route: 24km/15mi with 330m elevation
(https://tortugo.run/race/haldon-heroic-gravel-sportive-devon-united-kingdom-e4)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Navigation approach:** The Mega & Medio routes are "predominantly self nav" with accurate downloadable GPX files provided. Free Komoot navigation is included with entry. The Micro route is fully marked and serves as the start/finish for longer routes. (https://sportivaevents.clubtrac.co.uk/e/haldon-heroic-7628)

## DNF Risk Factors

**Navigation errors:** At least one documented case of a rider getting lost on the "fully marked" Micro route, going "the wrong way, up a rath..." and adding extra mileage. (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-xPnmU_w2o8)

**Course navigation rating:** Community ratings show 3.88/5 for "Course Navigation" with the question "How well marked was the course or was good quality self-navigation material provided?" — the lowest-rated category among all event aspects. (https://racecheck.com/races/haldon-heroic-gravel-sportive/)

**Event size:** Limited to 800 participants (across all categories), with 248 total racers timing out in 2025. Characterized by 72% of reviews as "Small & Intimate" atmosphere. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/6334/haldon-heroic, https://www.webscorer.com/race?raceid=386389)
