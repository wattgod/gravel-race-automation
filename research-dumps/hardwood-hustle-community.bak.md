# HARDWOOD HUSTLE — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Event Overview & Basic Facts

**The Hardwood Hustle** is a gravel cycling event in northern Wisconsin, described as "a gravel cycling ride on beautiful forest roads" that riders "will find both challenging and scenic." (https://www.thenxrth.com/events/the-hardwood-hustle)

**Location:** Departs from Buchanan Memorial Park, County Hwy A, Nelma, WI 54542 (some sources also list Long Lake, WI as the location) (https://www.thenxrth.com/events/the-hardwood-hustle)

**Distance Options:** 
- Century/100 miles/85 miles (sources vary on exact distance)
- 100K/50 miles (medium option)
- 39 miles/26 miles (shortest option)

**Start Times:**
- Long route: 8:00am or 9:00am CDT (sources vary)
- Medium and short routes: 9:00am CDT

**End Times:**
- Long route: 5:00pm CDT
- Medium route: 4:00pm CDT
- Short route: 4:00pm CDT

**Event Type:** Self-supported ride. "Riders should carry enough water, nutrition, and tools to provide for themselves!" (https://www.thenxrth.com/events/the-hardwood-hustle)

**Fundraiser Component:** "Fundraiser for the Alvin Volunteer Fire Department. Requiring a $30 minimum donation." All proceeds donated directly to the Town of Alvin Volunteer Fire and Rescue Department. (https://www.thenxrth.com/events/the-hardwood-hustle)

**Date History:** The event ran on September 9, 2022 and September 30, 2023 based on registration sources.

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

The event features "some of the best gravel the Northwoods has to offer" and "beautiful forest roads." (https://www.bikesignup.com/Race/WI/LongLake/TheHardwoodHustle)

The shortest route (39 miles/26 miles) "will still feature great gravel and some challenging climbs" despite being the easiest option. (https://www.thenxrth.com/events/the-hardwood-hustle)

**On-Route Services:** "There is a gas station on route at mile 55 in Hiles, WI" for the long route (85-mile version). (https://www.bikesignup.com/Race/Events/WI/LongLake/TheHardwoodHustle)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**[UNKNOWN LEVEL]** - The event description emphasizes flexibility: "Ride at your own pace. Enjoy some slow gravel or get your hustle on!" This language appears consistently across all distance options, suggesting the event accommodates various rider abilities. (https://www.thenxrth.com/events/the-hardwood-hustle)

**Self-Support Requirements:** All riders must be self-sufficient. The only mentioned on-route resupply is a gas station at mile 55 on the long course. Riders need to plan nutrition, hydration, and mechanical support accordingly. (https://www.bikesignup.com/Race/WI/LongLake/TheHardwoodHustle)

**Training Context:** The event is described as a "great opportunity to train for upcoming events such as The Hibernator and Red Granite Grinder," suggesting it's positioned as an early-season or training event. (https://www.thenxrth.com/events/the-hardwood-hustle)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

The event supports local emergency services, with all proceeds going to the Town of Alvin Volunteer Fire and Rescue Department, giving it a community fundraiser atmosphere. (https://www.bikesignup.com/Race/WI/LongLake/TheHardwoodHustle)

The messaging emphasizes inclusivity with phrases like "New to gravel or looking for something a little shorter?" for the shortest distance option, suggesting a welcoming environment for newer gravel riders. (https://www.thenxrth.com/events/the-hardwood-hustle)
