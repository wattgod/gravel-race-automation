## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Surface Mix - spessartpantani [UNKNOWN]:** "Untergrund ist viel gemischt, aber nichts Grobes, nur Asphalt, Schotter und etwas Wiese, keine wurzeligen Trailpassagen, alles fahrbar." [Surface is very mixed but nothing rough, only asphalt, gravel and some grass, no rooty trail sections, everything rideable] (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

**Course Character - CmdT [UNKNOWN]:** "Den Kurs fand ich für'n Gravelrennen fast perfekt. Viele Belagwechsel, viele Richtungsänderungen, keine besonderern schwierigkeiten oder Gefahrenpunkte, schöne Schotterabschnitte." [Found the course almost perfect for a gravel race. Many surface changes, many directional changes, no particular difficulties or danger points, nice gravel sections] (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

**Climbs - Official data:** First climb after 2km is 6% average with peak up to 9.2%, 1.5km long. Climb towards Magdeberg comes after 15km: 2.5km at 3.6% average with gradients up to 8%, starting offroad then finishing on road. Toughest climb after 23km: 3km at 5.2% average with peaks up to 13% towards Hohenstoffeln, starting offroad on hard soil before top. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/singen/)

**Elevation Detail:** 52km lap has 1067m elevation; full course (2x 52km laps) totals 2134m. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/singen/)

**Race Selection - spessartpantani [UNKNOWN]:** "Es war auch nicht so gefährlich wie es in Aachen berichtete wurde, da die Strecke sehr schnell selektiert." [Not as dangerous as reported from Aachen, as the course separates riders very quickly] (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

**Key Attack Point - Rosa Klöser [ELITE]:** Selection of 3 riders happened after a climb at 10km, then Klöser attacked solo on a grass climb at 20km. (https://www.cyclingnews.com/races/uci-gravel-world-series-events-2025/hegau-gravel-festival/results/)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Elite Men's Race Dynamics [ELITE]:** "The first lap saw 16 riders massed together and marking any moves. Three riders escaped on the second half of the race, Loman accelerating with last year's winner Jonas Koch and Felix Stehli." (https://www.cyclingnews.com/races/uci-gravel-world-series-events-2025/hegau-gravel-festival/results/)

**Pacing Reality - spessartpantani [UNKNOWN, ~4 W/kg FTP]:** "Bin den Bike Marathon mal mit 2h26min gefahren (74kg, NP252W ca. FTP 275W). Die entschärfte Gravelrunde müsste vielleicht nochmal 10 Minuten schneller gehen, aber halt nicht 3 mal hintereinander..." [Rode the bike marathon in 2h26min at 74kg, NP 252W, FTP ~275W. The easier gravel lap might go 10 minutes faster, but not 3 times in a row...] Note: This rider estimated they couldn't sustain this for the full UCI distance. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

**Power Requirements - CmdT [UNKNOWN]:** "Ich befürchte, unter 4.3W/kg FTP kommst du nicht mal in die Nähe der Qualifikationsplätze, wobei eben auch vor allem die Renndauer von 5h eine wesentliche Rolle spielt sowie die wiederholbare 1-5min Leistung." [Fear that below 4.3W/kg FTP you won't get close to qualification places, with race duration of 5h playing a key role plus repeatable 1-5min power] (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

**Bike Weight Advantage - spessartpantani [UNKNOWN]:** "Im Vergleich mit dem MTB bin ich die Anstiege mit dem 3kg leichteren Gravelbike bei gleichen Watt schon deutlich schneller hoch gefahren (z.B. an einem 7 min Anstieg ca. 30s schneller)." [Compared to MTB, climbed significantly faster on the 3kg lighter gravel bike at same watts - e.g., 30s faster on a 7-minute climb] (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)
