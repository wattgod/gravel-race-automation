# HEGAU GRAVEL RACE — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Rider Quotes & Race Reports

**Rosa Klöser [ELITE]:** "Happy to take the win today at the UCI Gravel Series in Singen. I set a hard pace pretty much from the start to thin out the group and after 10km in the race, together with Nicole Louis and Marielle Trouwborst we managed to get a selection of 3 riders after a climb. After 20km on a grass climb I managed to get away and pushed hard all the way till the finish" (https://www.granfondoguide.com/RaceDashboard/UCIGravelWorldSeries/1/8617/kloser-and-lowman-take-victory-at-uci-hegau-gravel-race)

**Jonas Koch [ELITE]:** "Had a blast racing at Gravel World Series Singen. Couldn't repeat my win from last year, but happy with my second second place in this year's gravel campaign, especially after a crash early in the race." (https://granfondodailynews.com/2025/07/08/uci-hegau-gravel-results-kloser-loman-finish-on-top-again/)

**spessartpantani [COMPETITIVE]:** "Ich bin nur 1 Runde mitgefahren. Auch das hat voll gereicht. Mir hat es mega Spaß gemacht. Mehr als beim Hegau Bike Marathon. Die Strecke war fürs MTB echt langweilig und fürs Gravelbike aber genau richtig, aber anspruchsvoll... Die eigentlich angedachte lange Strecke mit 136km wäre für mich nicht ein Kampf gegen die Mitstreiter, sondern ums Überleben geworden. Dafür sind die Anstiege zu steil, so dass ich meist über oder an der Schwelle hätte fahren müssen" [Translation: "I only rode 1 lap. That was more than enough. I had a blast. More than at the Hegau Bike Marathon. The course was really boring for MTB but just right for gravel bike, but challenging... The originally planned long distance of 136km would not have been a fight against competitors for me, but for survival. The climbs are too steep, so I would have had to ride mostly over or at threshold"] (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

**CmdT [COMPETITIVE]:** "Den Kurs fand ich für'n Gravelrennen fast perfekt. Viele Belagwechsel, viele Richtungsänderungen, keine besonderern schwierigkeiten oder Gefahrenpunkte, schöne Schotterabschnitte. Anspruchsvoll wurde das Rennen durch die vielen, steilen Anstiege. Auch über die verkürzte Renndauer, hat das ganz schön Körner gezogen, mehr als es das Topo hätte vermuten lassen!" [Translation: "I found the course almost perfect for a gravel race. Lots of surface changes, lots of direction changes, no particular difficulties or danger points, nice gravel sections. The race became challenging due to the many steep climbs. Even over the shortened race duration, it really took its toll, more than the topo would have suggested!"] (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

## Equipment & Gear Recommendations

**spessartpantani [COMPETITIVE]:** "Ich fahre einen 42er Specialized Pathfinder und glaub, dass der gut passt." [Translation: "I'm running a 42mm Specialized Pathfinder and think it fits well."] Noted the surface is "viel gemischt, aber nichts Grobes, nur Asphalt, Schotter und etwas Wiese, keine wurzeligen Trailpassagen, alles fahrbar" [Translation: "very mixed, but nothing coarse, just asphalt, gravel and some grass, no rooty trail passages, everything rideable"] (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

**CmdT [COMPETITIVE]:** "Ich hatte mich dann doch für den G-One RS vorne und hinten entschieden, im Nachhinein wäre ein wenig mehr Grip am Vorderrad besser gewesen. Ich blieb zwar auf dem Rad, musste mich aber teilweise echt konzentrieren." [Translation: "I decided on the G-One RS front and rear, in hindsight a little more grip on the front wheel would have been better. I stayed on the bike, but sometimes I really had to concentrate."] Note: This was in muddy conditions after rain. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

**CmdT [COMPETITIVE]:** On gearing for the course: "Als Normalo sollte man keine zu dicke Übersetzung haben" [Translation: "As a normal person you shouldn't have too thick/big a gear ratio"] due to steep climbs (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

## Weather Experienced

**2024 Edition:** Heavy rain and flooding on parts of the originally planned course forced organizers to shorten the route from 136km to 108km (two large 54km loops instead of two 54km loops plus a shorter third loop). The course was described as muddy, especially in forest sections. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/hegau-gravel-festival-distances-updated-for-the-weekend/)

**2025 Edition:** Course was shortened from planned 108km to approximately 104km due to weather conditions, but specific details not provided in sources.

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**spessartpantani [COMPETITIVE]:** "Sind bisher auch schon ein paar bekannte Namen gemeldet. Ist ne anspruchsvolle Runde mit viel Hoch und Runter. Mehrere steile Stiche und sehr verwinkelter Kurs. Landschaftlich sehr schöne Strecke... Im letzten 1/3 wird es auch etwas flacher." [Translation: "There are already a few well-known names registered. It's a challenging route with lots of ups and downs. Several steep climbs and a very winding course. Very beautiful landscape... In the last 1/3 it also gets a bit flatter."] Notes the course uses sections from the 2017 UCI Mountain Bike Marathon World Championships. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

**CmdT [COMPETITIVE]:** "Der Boden war, vor allem in den Waldabschnitten noch recht feucht, demensprechend haben wir uns schon eine Schlammpackung abgeholt." [Translation: "The ground was still quite wet, especially in the forest sections, so we got a mud pack."] Course features: "Viele Belagwechsel, viele Richtungsänderungen, keine besonderern schwierigkeiten oder Gefahrenpunkte" [Translation: "Lots of surface changes, lots of direction changes, no particular difficulties or danger points"] (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

Course composition: 66% pure gravel, mix of asphalt, gravel/schotter, and some grass sections. No rooty technical trail passages. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/hegau-gravel-festival-distances-updated-for-the-weekend/)

**Rosa Klöser [ELITE]:** Selection happened "after 10km in the race... after a climb" and she made her decisive attack "after 20km on a grass climb" (https://www.granfondoguide.com/RaceDashboard/UCIGravelWorldSeries/1/8617/kloser-and-lowman-take-victory-at-uci-hegau-gravel-race)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Rosa Klöser [ELITE - 2025 winner, 3:33:18 for 108km]:** Set a hard pace from the start to thin out the group. Made selection of 3 riders after 10km on a climb, then attacked solo at 20km on a grass climb and "pushed hard all the way till the finish" with over 7 minutes gap by finish. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/RaceDashboard/UCIGravelWorldSeries/1/8617/kloser-and-lowman-take-victory-at-uci-hegau-gravel-race)

**Mathijs Loman [ELITE - 2025 winner, 3:10:45 for 108km]:** In the men's race, the first lap saw 16 riders massed together marking moves. Three riders escaped in the second half, with Loman saving enough to out-sprint Jonas Koch at the finish. (https://www.cyclingnews.com/races/uci-gravel-world-series-events-2025/hegau-gravel-festival/results/)

**spessartpantani [COMPETITIVE - ~4 W/kg FTP, 74kg, ran 2:26 on MTB version previously]:** "Die eigentlich angedachte lange Strecke mit 136km wäre für mich nicht ein Kampf gegen die Mitstreiter, sondern ums Überleben geworden. Dafür sind die Anstiege zu steil, so dass ich meist über oder an der Schwelle hätte fahren müssen und das wäre dann auf die Menge an Höhenmeter echt eine Aufgabe geworden" [Translation: Would have had to ride mostly over or at threshold on the steep climbs, making the full distance a survival challenge rather than a race] (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

**CmdT** [COMPETITIVE] **(~4 W/kg FTP, 74kg, 250W NP in similar races):** "Für mich war es ein echt hartes Rennen" over the shortened 104km distance. Notes that difficulty was greater than the topo suggested. Recommends at least 4.3 W/kg FTP to be competitive for qualification places based on experience at similar UCI races. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

## DNF Risk Factors

**spessartpantani [COMPETITIVE]:** Notes that the course "sehr schnell selektiert" [Translation: "selects very quickly"] meaning slower riders get dropped early but this also reduces crash risk compared to packed groups. "Wenn man auf die Schnauze fällt, dann vermutlich nur selbstverschuldet" [Translation: "If you fall on your face, it's probably only your own fault"] (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

**Jonas Koch [ELITE]:** Mentioned "a crash early in the race" but still finished 2nd, suggesting crashes can happen but aren't necessarily race-ending. (https://granfondodailynews.com/2025/07/08/uci-hegau-gravel-results-kloser-loman-finish-on-top-again/)

Pacing: Multiple riders noted the steep, repeated climbs are the key challenge. Going too hard early on these climbs can lead to blowing up over the 5+ hour duration for competitive riders.

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**spessartpantani [COMPETITIVE]:** "Organisation war Top, wie immer." [Translation: "Organization was top, as always."] Notes this continues the tradition from the previous Hegau Bike Marathon organization. Appreciated that it was "sehr international" with strong riders, though felt the "echte Hobbyfraktion war etwas unterrepräsentiert" [Translation: "real hobby faction was somewhat underrepresented"]. Compared favorably to Aachen: "War aber vielleicht sogar das bessere Event. (Oder halt anders...)" [Translation: "But was perhaps even the better event. (Or just different...)"] due to less chaos and fewer crashes. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

**CmdT [COMPETITIVE]:** "Das Rennen an sich war schon geil und die Landschaft auch echt schön, ein gelungenes Rennen." [Translation: "The race itself was really cool and the landscape really beautiful, a successful race."] However noted that for World Championship qualification, larger events like Aachen offer better odds due to more participants. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)

**Field size:** Over 900 riders in 2025 from 37 different countries; 725 competitors in 2024; nearly 1,000 expected participants for future editions. (https://granfondodailynews.com/2025/07/08/uci-hegau-gravel-results-kloser-loman-finish-on-top-again/ and https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/hegau-gravel-race-with-a-very-international-line-up/)

Start/finish location: "mitten in Singen" [Translation: "in the middle of Singen"] providing town-center atmosphere. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/hegau-gravel.194216/)
