# HOT SPRINGS GRAVEL GAUNTLET — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Event Overview & Logistics

**Location & Venue:** Hot Springs Resort and Spa in downtown Hot Springs, NC, situated on the NC/TN border. The resort features natural hot springs, lodging, cabins, and a campground. (https://www.southeastgravel.com/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet)

**Full Weekend Format:** This is designed as a weekend-long event, not just a race-day experience. Friday includes a shakedown ride pre-riding the River Road section (5pm start, followed by Q&A), Friday night number pickup and pre-race party at Big Pillow Brewery with guest pros, Saturday race, and Saturday night camping and festivities. (https://www.southeastgravel.com/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet)

**Course Distances:**
- Long Course: 61 miles
- Short Course: 28-30 miles (varies by year)
- Fun Course: 13 miles
(https://www.southeastgravel.com/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet)

**Series Context:** Event 3 of 4 (2026) or Event 2 of 6 (2024) in the Southeast Gravel Series, with omnium points available for series competitors. (https://www.southeastgravel.com/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet, https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/11689/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet)

**Aid Stations:**
- Long Course: Mile 11.5, 45, 53.5
- Short Course: Mile 11.5, 20
- Stocked with water, carb drink mix, gels, pickle juice, coke, and various snacks
(https://www.southeastgravel.com/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet)

**Post-Race:** Free food and free beer at the finish line. (https://www.southeastgravel.com/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet, https://happeningnext.com/event/southeast-gravel-hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet-eid3a0b1qeh0n)

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**The Defining Characteristic:** "Hilliest Course Yet! Situated on the NC/TN border, Hot Springs has one flat road along the French Broad River, everything else is climbing on some amazing scenic roads." (https://www.southeastgravel.com/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet)

**Course Terrain Description:** "Moderate terrain with rolling short steep hills or moderate, sustained climbs" (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/11689/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet)

**Why Hot Springs Works:** "The gravel, terrain, and lack of traffic around Hot Springs made it a no brainer to have an event there." (https://www.southeastgravel.com/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet)

**Scenic Setting:** "Hots Springs is a gravel and trail hotspot worth checking out, it not only has trail town charm but cool off bike offerings, coffee shops, brewery art and old buildings." [COMPETITIVE] (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LeDysq8VeCw)

**2024 Long Course Elite Results [ELITE]:**
- Winner Dylan Johnson: 3:12:30 (61 miles) = 19.0 mph average
- 2nd place Fergus Arthur: 3:16:33 (+4:03)
- 3rd place Elliott Baring: 3:19:06 (+6:35)
(https://www.webscorer.com/racedetails?raceid=347784&embed=2)

**Time Spreads Show Climbing Impact:** In 2025, the top 10 men finished within 12 minutes of each other (3:11:42 to 3:23:33), but by 40th place the time was 3:57:22—a massive spread indicating the climbing takes its toll as rider power decreases. (https://www.webscorer.com/racedetails?raceid=384812&did=508707)

**Tougher Course Strategy:** "Maybe the tougher courses suit you and you'll have a chance to claim those valuable omnium points with the mountains of Hot Springs" - suggesting that strong climbers can make up ground in the series here. (https://www.southeastgravel.com/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Weekend Destination Focus:** "Don't just ride your bike and leave town. Stick around on Saturday for some more camping, food, and hang out." (https://www.southeastgravel.com/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet)

**Small Town Charm:** "Hot Springs has a tiny and vibrant downtown. With restaurants and breweries, people come for the weekend or stop as they're thru hiking the Appalachian Trail. Plan on spending a weekend downtown and fall in love." (https://www.southeastgravel.com/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet)

**Pro Interaction:** Friday night pre-race party includes Q&A with guest pros at Big Pillow Brewery. The 2024 event featured Jeremiah Bishop and Rocket from Rocket Revolution leading a Friday shakeout ride. (https://www.southeastgravel.com/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet, https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/11689/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet)

**Post-Race Social:** "Go as hard as you want, and know that a hot meal and a cold refreshing beer will be awaiting you at the finish line. Stick around and cheer on fellow riders who are finishing." (https://www.southeastgravel.com/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet)

**Natural Hot Springs Recovery:** After the race, participants can "soak in the rejuvenating hot springs to soothe those muscles" at the resort venue. (https://happeningnext.com/event/southeast-gravel-hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet-eid3a0b1qeh0n)

**Family-Friendly Weekend:** "Bring the family and stay at the Hot Springs Resort and Spa. They offer tent camping, RV sites and cabins." (https://happeningnext.com/event/southeast-gravel-hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet-eid3a0b1qeh0n)

**"Trail Town" Atmosphere:** Hot Springs functions as an Appalachian Trail resupply point, giving it a unique outdoor community vibe with thru-hikers mixing with cyclists. (https://www.southeastgravel.com/hot-springs-gravel-gauntlet)
