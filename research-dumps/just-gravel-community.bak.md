# JUST.GRAVEL — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Race Overview & Context

**Just Gravel** is described as a beginner-friendly UK gravel event based in Grizedale, with routes that offer safe, family-oriented activities. It's positioned as an accessible entry point to gravel racing.

The event is consistently characterized as welcoming to newcomers and families. It's listed among Europe's "Top 5 First Gravel Races" for 2025, selected based on inclusivity, cost, location, and size criteria.
