## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**YouTube race footage [ELITE perspective]:** The race description references "Iowa City's rolling hills gravel" and emphasizes that "Iowa is not FLAT!!" - contradicting common perceptions of Iowa terrain (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_7VS-l6yPqA)

**Course characteristics [UNKNOWN level]:** Described as "Moderate terrain with rolling short steep hills or moderate, sustained climbs" according to the official race listing (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/11788/kalona-horseshoe)

**Road surface [UNKNOWN level]:** The Amish and Mennonite population keeps "these roads beautiful and rustic without the complexity of asphalt" - indicating predominantly gravel/unpaved surfaces (https://www.racecenter.com/cycling/kalona-horseshoe)

**Course topology [UNKNOWN level]:** Described as "windy and rolly" with "captivating and quiet" scenery (https://icorrmtb.org/event/kalona-horseshoe/)

**Regional context [UNKNOWN level]:** The race provides "a bit of a preview of the terrain in Southeastern Iowa" for the series championship race in Fairfield later in the season (https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/15673/kalona-horseshoe)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Post-race food [UNKNOWN level]:** Multiple sources emphasize the local bakeries and specifically pie: "Come for the race, stay for the amazing bakeries!" (https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/15673/kalona-horseshoe)

**Post-race mandatory experience [UNKNOWN level]:** "the post-race pie is mandatory. (Seriously, don't skip the pie.)" along with "food, drinks, and as much pie as you can handle" (https://icorrmtb.org/event/kalona-horseshoe/)

**Cultural experience [UNKNOWN level]:** The race offers a unique opportunity to "race your bike past a horse-drawn buggy" due to the Amish/Mennonite community presence (https://icorrmtb.org/event/kalona-horseshoe/)

**Team participation:** Goosetown Racing had several members participate in the inaugural 2024 race, with at least one podium finish (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_7VS-l6yPqA)

**Event positioning:** Kalona Horseshoe was new to the Iowa Gravel Series in 2024 and serves as the series opener, providing early-season racing opportunities (https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/15673/kalona-horseshoe)

**Venue:** Starting location is the Kalona Historical Village at 715 D Avenue, Kalona, IA (https://icorrmtb.org/event/kalona-horseshoe/)

**Registration tiers [UNKNOWN level]:** 
- $40 "Grassroots Registration" - includes chip timing, marked course, pro photography and aid stations
- $60 "Race Pace" - adds Iowa Gravel Series t-shirt and finish line raffle entry
- $400 "Full Season" - all 6 races in series plus Superbloom Series jersey
(https://www.bikeiowa.com/Event/15673/kalona-horseshoe)
