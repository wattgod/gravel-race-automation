## TERRAIN & COURSE
- **Surface breakdown (% gravel, pavement, singletrack, dirt)**: Unavailable; primarily on **Ruta Vicuña-Yendegaia**, a gravel corridor built by **Cuerpo Militar del Trabajo (CMT)** army engineers (~1 km built per year)[1][2][4].
- **Key course features, named climbs/sectors**: Traverses **pampa plains**, **native forests**, **lakes** (Lago Lynch, Lago Escondido, **Lago Blanco**, Lago Fagnano/Kami), **rivers**; **two southernmost mountain passes on Earth**; **Sierra Carmen Silva**; **llanura del río Grande** with guanacos; segments include **Villa Cameron to Lago Blanco**, **Lago Blanco to Caleta María**, **Caleta María via Campamento CMT to Pampa Guanaco**, return to **Villa Cameron** (out-and-back on single road)[1][2][4]. Passes through **Karukinka Park** (mentioned in related events)[3].
- **Technical difficulty level**: Described as **grueling** stage race in **remote wilderness**, **inhóspitas (inhospitable) lands**, **difficult terrain with longer, steeper hills**; least explored paths in virgin environment[1][2][4][6][9].
- **Elevation profile description**: **5,500 meters** total ascent over 440 km; features **steppe**, **pampa magallánica**, early forests; Stage 1 through deserted steppe landscape[1][2][4].
