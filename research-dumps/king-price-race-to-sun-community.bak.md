## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course Profile:**
- 161km (100 miles) distance with only 1,000m elevation gain - described as "flat and fast" (https://racetothesun.co.za/honeycomb226ers_dominate/)
- 50-mile option starts at halfway point at Beestekraal Kerk, Roodekoppies (https://racetothesun.co.za/)

**Route Character - Tale of Two Halves:**

"This year's route was a story of two distinct halves. The first section followed the picturesque Hartbeestpoort Dam Wall and the canals, offering fast, flowing sections where riders could settle into their rhythm. The second half transformed into a rugged 'bush' experience, with challenging gravel sections, sand and the new Sediba Kwele testing riders' endurance and technical skills." (https://racetothesun.co.za/honeycomb226ers_dominate/)

**Specific Terrain Challenges:**

**Corrugations:** Multiple riders mentioned "quite a bit of corrugation" with "lots of short sections parts with corrugation" throughout the course (https://forum.bikehub.co.za/topic/195470-event-king-price-race-to-the-sun/)

**Loose Sand:** Described as prevalent, particularly on climbs in the second half (https://forum.bikehub.co.za/topic/195470-event-king-price-race-to-the-sun/)

**2025 Route Changes:** "Your feedback from last year's race was invaluable, and we've used it to redesign key sections of the course. While the first half remains unchanged, we've c[hanged the second half]" - added the standout Sediba Kwele Reserve (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FYSA5w9weX0 and https://racetothesun.co.za/honeycomb226ers_dominate/)

**Finish Section Drama:** "Willeit, who had led into the Sun City approach, missed a critical turn along with Sanders, allowing Preen to claim the win" - navigation matters in final kilometers (https://racetothesun.co.za/honeycomb226ers_dominate/)

**The Final Kick:** "I saw the Pawpaw and I saw the Fan... 700M to go the legs seized up... I walked a bit to the top of that little kick 500 m before the finish" - there's a short, sharp climb near the finish (https://forum.bikehub.co.za/topic/195470-event-king-price-race-to-the-sun/page/4/)

**Course Markings:** One rider praised "The markings of the course, the marshals along the way, very professionally done" (https://racetothesun.co.za/)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Critical Pacing Advice:**

**JJDT [UNKNOWN]:** "As already mentioned the first half is easy, mostly flat with some downhill and no climbs and with this one tends to going all out. The second half do have more climbs and uphill drags and if you have wasted most of the energy on the first half the second half will be a long ride with no power." (https://forum.bikehub.co.za/topic/195470-event-king-price-race-to-the-sun/)

**Pandatron [UNKNOWN]:** "Dont get carried away on the first half, second half has some sting" (https://forum.bikehub.co.za/topic/195470-event-king-price-race-to-the-sun/)

**RearWheelDrive [UNKNOWN]:** Describes the classic mistake: "Proper rookie mistake, went out too hard tried to keep up too much, at about 145 I realised this race is going to be 10km too far" (finished in 7:20) (https://forum.bikehub.co.za/topic/195470-event-king-price-race-to-the-sun/page/4/)

**Mountain Bru [UNKNOWN]:** "Had a really lekker first 100km and paced myself well for a sub 6, but then the wheels came off a bit and I ended up doing 6:34 after dawdling the last 10km" (averaged 28km/h for first 100km) (https://forum.bikehub.co.za/topic/195470-event-king-price-race-to-the-sun/page/4/)

**Elite Race Tactics:**

**Marc Pritzen [ELITE]:** Launched solo breakaway at 70km mark and held it to finish, winning by 7 minutes in 4:30:11. Credited team disruption of chase as critical to success. (https://racetothesun.co.za/honeycomb226ers_dominate/)

**Elite Women's finish [ELITE]:** "The trio worked cohesively to establish a decisive gap around the 130km mark" - the decisive move came late in the race (https://racetothesun.co.za/honeycomb226ers_dominate/)

**Nutrition:**

**Pandatron [UNKNOWN]:** "If you dont have a nutrition plan now, then its too late. Just try to take in what you currently do on a race with regularity and hydrate." (https://forum.bikehub.co.za/topic/195470-event-king-price-race-to-the-sun/)

**Op1 [UNKNOWN]:** "Nothing major to have 'special' tyres, your normal tyre choice will be just fine with your normal riding pressures. Nutrition; as mentioned to late to change. Use what you normal ride with." (https://forum.bikehub.co.za/topic/195470-event-king-price-race-to-the-sun/)
