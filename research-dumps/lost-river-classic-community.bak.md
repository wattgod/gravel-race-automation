## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course Composition [UNKNOWN]:** "The premier Lost River Classic route has something for everyone: gravel, pavement, singletrack, doubletrack logging roads, and private farm tracks we have been granted special permission to use." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10501/lost-river-classic)

**Long Route (85 miles) [UNKNOWN]:** "73 mile, mostly gravel route primarily in Hardy County, WV, with some segments in Virginia. There are four challenging gravel climbs and 9,000 total feet of elevation." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10501/lost-river-classic)

**Medium Route (60 miles) [UNKNOWN]:** "The Two Ridge Loop joins the 73 mile bike race for the first half of the ride and returns through Lost River State Park to the Mathias Community Center from the top of South Branch Mountain at the Lost River Barn... about 45 miles long." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10501/lost-river-classic)

**Terrain Mix [UNKNOWN]:** "Route is 90 percent gravel roads, crosses over two lane light traffic highway, route is not closed to any traffic." (https://moorefieldexaminer.com/2025/08/19/lost-river-classic-traverses-gravel-terrain/)

**Howard's Lick Climb [UNKNOWN]:** "Hardy County is home to a number of significant climbs, but none more infamous than the paved ascent on the State Park Road / backside of Howard&#8217;s Lick up and over the Moorefield side of South Branch Mountain. This nearly 5 mile climb covers 2,000 feet of elevation at an average grade of over 8%." (https://lostrivertrailscoalition.org/blog/)

**Surface Quality Note [UNKNOWN]:** "The 60k course is half paved and half gravel. The 106k course adds the challenging ascents of both Rich Mountain Road and Cascade Lake Road." (https://www.roadbikerider.com/gravel-ride-race-list/)

**Difficulty Assessment [UNKNOWN]:** "This is a very challenging event." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10501/lost-river-classic)

**Reputation [UNKNOWN]:** "the Lost River Classic has developed a reputation for being one of the most challenging and technical gravel races in the East." (https://lostrivertrailscoalition.org/blog/)

**Water Crossing [UNKNOWN]:** "There is one river crossing, and two super cool tunnel underpasses going under I-26... The water crossing is very smooth and a paved surface." (https://www.southeastgravel.com/events)

**Support Availability [UNKNOWN]:** "There will be a minimum of one aid station along the course" for the long routes, with "3" aid stations total listed. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10501/lost-river-classic)
