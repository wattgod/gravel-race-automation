# LOWELL CLASSIC — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Race Overview & History
The Lowell Classic is a well-established gravel road cycling race **founded in 2013** in Lowell, Michigan. Multiple sources describe it as "a classic old world style gravel road race" that has become a signature event in the region. The race is set along the banks of the Flat River and through rural Ionia County, featuring **approximately 85-95% unpaved roads**. It's been described as "the crown jewel of Lowell County" and serves as **the final stop of the Michigan Gravel Race Series** (https://www.ohiogravelgrinders.com/event-listings/2025/10/25/lowell-classic-gravel-race, https://lowellclassic.com/about-2/).

The race venue is **Fallasburg County Park**, which first opened in 1929 on 56 acres and now encompasses over 180 acres including two spring-fed streams, an island on the Flat River, and a portion of the 4000-mile North Country Trail (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/5267/lowell-classic).

## Course Options & Distances
The race offers multiple distance options:
- **15 mile (Mini)** route
- **34 mile (Short)** route - approximately 2000+ feet elevation gain
- **50 mile (Long)** route
- **100 mile (Ultra/Rogue 100)** route

All routes are **single loops** that are marked and supported. The courses feature **two historic wooden covered bridges dating back to the mid-1800s** (https://www.stellafly.com/events/lowell-classic-gravel-road-race/).

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)
Multiple sources describe the courses as "challenging, scenic, and fun" with **"rolling short steep hills or moderate, sustained climbs"** (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/5267/lowell-classic).

**[ELITE] Race report notes:** One rider achieved **8th place overall, 2nd place age group** in the 15-mile race and noted it was their "last gravel race of the year" with a **3rd place overall finish for the Michigan Gravel Race Series (MGRS) mini series** (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ngfV_0F8tYs).

The **38-mile race** is documented to have **2000+ feet elevation gain with 70% gravel** surfaces (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pKJHVh7MruM).

One rider [UNKNOWN] described weather conditions at their race: **"Beautiful sunny skies and 45 degrees at start. 38 miles with 2200ft elevation gain"** (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kXnLUXU6LHM).

## Race Logistics & Format
**Start times:**
- Rogue 100: TBA
- 50 Mile: 10:00 AM (Mass Start)
- 34 Mile: 10:05 AM (Mass Start)

**Race day packet pickup:** Saturday morning between 8:00 and 9:30 AM at the Fallasburg Park lodge.

**Timing:** RFID chip timing with live results and high-speed video.

**Parking:** Convenient parking directly across from the lodge (600-700 vehicles) (https://www.stellafly.com/events/lowell-classic-gravel-road-race/).

## Prize Purse & Categories
**Cash prizes for 50 & 34 mile races:**
- First overall (male & female): $250.00
- Second overall (male & female): $150.00
- Third overall (male & female): $100.00

Medals are awarded to top three overall finishers in the 50 & 34 mile races, and medals to all top three category finishers in all races.

**Categories include:** Athena, Clydesdale, Fat Bike, Junior, Long, Mini, Short, Single Speed, Tandem, Ultra, Vintage (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/5267/lowell-classic, https://ilovegravelracing.com/event/lowell-classic/).

## Equipment Flexibility
The race explicitly welcomes diverse bike choices: **"Road, cross, mountain, fat, or tandem, your choice. Any bike will work, bring your favorite."** The race is described as suitable for "elite road, cyclocross, mountain bike racer, to those of you just looking for a great ride" (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/5267/lowell-classic).

## Community Feel & Atmosphere
**[RECREATIONAL] From race website:** "Our team is dedicated to making your day on the gravel worth every second. If you're a competitive cyclist or just a casual rider, this is the perfect race for you!" The organizers emphasize they are "constantly finding ways to improve experiences for our racers – whether that's by implementing industry leading technology for tracking race times, or through great customer service" (https://lowellclassic.com/about-2/).

The venue is described as family-friendly: **"Bring the whole family; Fallasburg Park is a real gem and a great venue for this bike race."** The park features paved and unpaved walking paths, several playgrounds, ball fields, and a disc golf course (https://www.stellafly.com/events/lowell-classic-gravel-road-race/).

The race is positioned as **"fall's epic gravel finale"** and the **"season's grand gravel curtain call"** as it typically occurs in late October (https://ilovegravelracing.com/event/lowell-classic/).

## Series Affiliation
The Lowell Classic is part of the **Michigan Gravel Race Series** and is designated as a **"Premier Race"** with **"Double Points"** in the I Love Gravel Racing series (https://ilovegravelracing.com/event/lowell-classic/, https://www.stellafly.com/events/lowell-classic-gravel-road-race/).
