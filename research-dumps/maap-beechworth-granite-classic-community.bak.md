## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

The event features up to **60km of gravel roads on the 115km route** alone as of 2025. The terrain includes varied surfaces: undulating private farm lanes, bush tracks, fire roads, and the famous **'Champagne de Pilot'** gravel. (https://flowmountainbike.com/post-all/beechworth-granite-classic-2025/)

Specific terrain characteristics include:
- **Mount Stanley**: winding red-clay-hard-pack and sub-alpine rainforest
- **Pilot and Barambogie forests**: granitic roads described as "faster than bitumen"
- **Mount Pilot area**: immaculate champagne gravel
- **Grassy sections**: local farmers have allowed the course to cut through farmland, breaking up long stretches of gravel with grass sections (https://flowmountainbike.com/post-all/beechworth-granite-classic-2025/)

**Course flexibility:** The 90km and 115km routes head off in the same direction in the morning before splitting around Chiltern, allowing riders to switch from the longer to shorter route mid-ride if needed. All routes converge for the final 15km. (https://flowmountainbike.com/post-all/beechworth-granite-classic-2025/)

The official description notes: "A tremendous variety of surfaces keeping riders on their toes and in adventure mode at every turn." (https://events.humanitix.com/maap-beechworth-granite-classic-2024)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Course flexibility strategy [ALL LEVELS]:** Routes are designed to interconnect, with the 90km and 115km splitting at Chiltern. Riders feeling fatigued can drop from the 115km to 90km route at this junction. All routes converge for the final 15km, meaning riders on different distances will be riding together. (https://flowmountainbike.com/post-all/beechworth-granite-classic-2025/)

**Nutrition strategy [ALL LEVELS]:** Unlike typical gravel events with gel and mix tables, the Granite Classic provides substantial on-course food including hot quesadillas, coffee, and donuts. Previous years featured peanut butter quesadillas, Bridge Road Brewers non-alcoholic beers, and gin shots provided by Barking Owl Distilling Co. Organizers increased the number of feed stations for 2025 after some riders missed food in 2024. (https://flowmountainbike.com/post-all/beechworth-granite-classic-2025/)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

The event is explicitly **not a competition** — there are **no podiums**, just "good riding and good people." The organizers emphasize it's a "ride, not a race." (https://flowmountainbike.com/post-all/beechworth-granite-classic-2025/)

**Origins:** Started as a small local ride called "Tour de Beechworth" where founder Ben Kraus and locals would ride in mid-Winter, then watch the Tour de France live at the brewery with a French dinner. It has grown from this social ride into the current 1,300+ rider event while maintaining its non-competitive ethos. (https://flowmountainbike.com/post-all/beechworth-granite-classic-2025/)

**Event atmosphere:** Described as having an "inclusive, anything goes, party atmosphere" with "on-course food and drinks" and entertainment including:
- Roving bands and DJ setups on course
- Musicians playing guitar solos from atop roadside boulders
- Dueling drummers
- Hilltop DJ sets with views (https://flowmountainbike.com/post-all/beechworth-granite-classic-2025/)

**Weekend schedule highlights:**
- **Friday:** Nighttime 15-20km shakedown ride (social pace), Spoken handmade bicycle show, Crumpler Film Festival (shortened version from the States)
- **Saturday:** Main ride with four distance options (15km, 45km, 90km, 115km), three-course dinner at Beechworth Memorial Hall with guest speakers including Mitch Docker and Grace Brown (Tokyo Olympics 2020), followed by **Beechworth's first-ever nightclub** with DJs
- **Sunday:** Optional 35km recovery spin with coffee and treats at Stanley General Store (https://flowmountainbike.com/post-all/beechworth-granite-classic-2025/)

**Family-friendly:** The 15km option and event structure allow riders to bring family members and partners. Described as "a riding weekender for anyone and everyone" with people fitting it into larger family holidays. (https://flowmountainbike.com/post-all/beechworth-granite-classic-2025/)

Run by **Bridge Road Brewers**, a local brewery, emphasizing local ownership and community connection. (https://flowmountainbike.com/post-all/beechworth-granite-classic-2025/)
