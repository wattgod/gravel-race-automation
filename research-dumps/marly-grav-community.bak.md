# MARLY GRAV — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Event Overview & Scale

Marly Grav (formerly Gravel Fondo Limburg) is Europe's largest one-day gravel event. The 2025 edition sold out with **2,550 riders from 26 nations** competing (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/europes-biggest-gravel-race-returns-as-marly-grav-race/). It's a UCI Gravel World Series qualifier, with top 25% finishers in each age/gender category earning spots at UCI Gravel World Championships.

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

The course features **South Limburg's "white roads"** (marl/limestone gravel roads), which give the event its name. A unique feature: **passage through the Valkenburg marl cave during the final lap** (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/europes-biggest-gravel-race-returns-as-marly-grav-race/).

**Course distances:**
- 155km for men 19-59 and women 19-49 (approximately 1,000-1,600m climbing)
- 100km for men 60+ and women 50+ (https://allevents.in/valkenburg/marly-grav-race/200029568876449)

The race starts on the **iconic Cauberg climb**, famous from the Amstel Gold Race (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/europes-biggest-gravel-race-returns-as-marly-grav-race/).

The course is characterized by **rolling hills and loose soil** in the Netherlands' most beautiful cycling area (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/valkenburg/).

One YouTube video title describes the experience as **"CHAOS XXL"** suggesting a challenging, fast-paced race environment (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MPjpAjfhtRE).

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Tim Wellens [ELITE]** completed the 155km course in **4:21:17** for the win, finishing a minute ahead of second place (https://granfondodailynews.com/2025/05/14/wellens-punches-ticket-to-gravel-worlds-wins-uci-marly-grav-race/).

**Lorena Wiebes [ELITE]** won the women's elite race in **5:19:46**, though fastest woman overall was **Malou Eisen [ELITE amateur]** at **5:13:46** (https://granfondodailynews.com/2025/05/14/wellens-punches-ticket-to-gravel-worlds-wins-uci-marly-grav-race/).

For the 100km medio distance, **Guyon De Garde [UNKNOWN]** won in **3:20:22**, with women's winner **Marijke De Smedt [ELITE]** at **3:44:57** (https://granfondodailynews.com/2025/05/14/wellens-punches-ticket-to-gravel-worlds-wins-uci-marly-grav-race/).

*Note: Elite/pro times listed above. Recreational riders should expect significantly longer finish times based on individual fitness.*

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

The event takes place at the **Shimano Experience Center** (Home of Gravel), which features changing rooms, showers, secure lockers, technical support, and a coffee corner with barista service (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/valkenburg/).

The event spans **three days (May 8-10, 2026)** with multiple components:
- UCI gravel race
- Bikepacking trip
- Social rides
- Workshops
- Bike expo
- "Limburg festival atmosphere" (https://www.marlygrav.com/en/)

Valkenburg itself is described as a city that "seems to encourage visitors to just have a good time," known for its tourist atmosphere with hotels, bars, and restaurants. The town has deep cycling history, having hosted UCI Road World Championships (1948, 1998) and UCI Cyclocross World Championships (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/valkenburg/).

The event attracts **legendary cycling figures** including Johan Museeuw, Sven Nys, Greg Van Avermaet, Niki Terpstra, and Dirk De Wolf (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/europes-biggest-gravel-race-returns-as-marly-grav-race/).
