# MARLY GRAV — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Rider Quotes & Race Reports

**Tim Wellens [ELITE]:** Won the 2025 race in 4:21:17 for the 155km route, "Fresh off a 3rd place finish on the white gravel roads of Strade Bianche" - secured a front row start at the UCI Gravel World Championships with the victory (https://granfondodailynews.com/2025/05/14/wellens-punches-ticket-to-gravel-worlds-wins-uci-marly-grav-race/)

**Lorena Wiebes [ELITE]:** Won the women's elite race in 5:19:46, though notably "results show the fastest woman on the day was young Dutch amateur Malou Eisen (LIV GRASSROOTS) who finished six minutes faster in 5:13:46" (https://granfondodailynews.com/2025/05/14/wellens-punches-ticket-to-gravel-worlds-wins-uci-marly-grav-race/)

**Dutch blog perspective [UNKNOWN]:** "Stel je voor: je klikt in, ademt diep in, en boem: direct de Cauberg op. Geen tijd om rustig warm te draaien." (Translation: "Imagine: you clip in, take a deep breath, and boom: straight up the Cauberg. No time to warm up gradually.") - emphasizing the brutal start (https://www.bicycling.nl/nieuws/1295327/alles-wat-je-moet-weten-over-marly-grav-race-2025.html)

**Course description [UNKNOWN]:** "Verwacht geen eindeloze rechte gravelwegen zoals in de Achterhoek of Drenthe. Hier is alles korter, steiler en intenser. Klimmen, sturen, bijten." (Translation: "Don't expect endless straight gravel roads like in the Achterhoek or Drenthe. Here everything is shorter, steeper and more intense. Climbing, steering, biting down.") (https://www.bicycling.nl/nieuws/1295327/alles-wat-je-moet-weten-over-marly-grav-race-2025.html)

## Equipment & Gear Recommendations

**General recommendation [UNKNOWN]:** From reconnaissance video description: "Bij droge omstandigheden zijn semi slick banden echt wel top" (Translation: "In dry conditions, semi-slick tires are really excellent") - noting that reconnaissance was done in dry conditions one month before the race (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jb_0v__2YTY)

**Course terrain mix [UNKNOWN]:** 
- 100km route: 46.6km unpaved, 13km paved gravel, 57km total offroad, 34km asphalt, 1.87km cobbles
- 155km route: 74.8km unpaved, 19.3km paved gravel, 52km asphalt, ~5km singletracks, plus cobble sections
(https://www.bicycling.nl/nieuws/1295327/alles-wat-je-moet-weten-over-marly-grav-race-2025.html)

**Note:** Equipment recommendations are limited in sources - most content focuses on course description rather than specific rider setups.

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**The cave section [UNKNOWN]:** "In de eerste lus duik je ondergronds: de Gemeentegrot van Valkenburg wacht. Met fiets en al rijd je door een eeuwenoude mergelgrot, waar de temperatuur constant fris blijft en de sfeer haast surrealistisch is." (Translation: "In the first loop you dive underground: the Valkenburg Municipal Cave awaits. You ride through a centuries-old marl cave with your bike, where the temperature stays constantly cool and the atmosphere is almost surreal.") - This unique underground passage through the marl cave is a race highlight (https://www.bicycling.nl/nieuws/1295327/alles-wat-je-moet-weten-over-marly-grav-race-2025.html)

**The Cauberg start:** Race begins immediately on the Cauberg climb, "the famous climb, known from the Amstel Gold Race and countless World Championships, forces you to be awake from meter one" (https://www.bicycling.nl/nieuws/1295327/alles-wat-je-moet-weten-over-marly-grav-race-2025.html)

**Course character:** "Zo'n 60 procent van het parcours bestaat uit onverharde wegen: grindpaden, veldwegen en holle wegen" (Translation: "About 60 percent of the course consists of unpaved roads: gravel paths, field roads and sunken lanes") (https://www.bicycling.nl/nieuws/1295327/alles-wat-je-moet-weten-over-marly-grav-race-2025.html)

**Elevation:** 
- 100km route: ~960m climbing
- 155km route: ~1,510m climbing
(https://www.komoot.com/collection/3449919/-marly-grav-race-2025)

**Terrain highlights from Komoot:**
- Marl cliffs: "These are old marl quarries, a high wall of marl"
- Valkenburg Municipal Cave: "Exit of the Gemeentegrot Valkenburg. And of the many mines here in the hills around Valkenburg"
- Château St. Gerlach Sculpture Park along the route
(https://www.komoot.com/collection/3449919/-marly-grav-race-2025)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Elite pacing [ELITE]:** Tim Wellens completed 155km in 4:21:17 (average ~35.6 km/h), with second place Joris Nieuwenhuis arriving "a minute later" in a three-up sprint (https://granfondodailynews.com/2025/05/14/wellens-punches-ticket-to-gravel-worlds-wins-uci-marly-grav-race/)

**Women's elite pacing [ELITE]:** Lorena Wiebes finished in 5:19:46 for 155km (average ~29.1 km/h), with second place 14 seconds back and third 52 seconds back - indicating tight racing at the front (https://cyclingflash.com/race/uci-gravel-world-series-gravel-fondo-limburg-we-2025/result)

**Feed zones [UNKNOWN]:** 
- 100km route: 3 support stations
- 155km route: 5 support stations
- "Denk aan snelle snacks, verse bidons en mechanische hulp als het écht misgaat" (Translation: "Think of quick snacks, fresh bottles and mechanical help if things really go wrong")
(https://www.bicycling.nl/nieuws/1295327/alles-wat-je-moet-weten-over-marly-grav-race-2025.html)

**Course structure:** 
- 100km = 2 loops
- 155km = 3 loops
- Both include an approximately 50km loop structure
(https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/europes-biggest-gravel-race-returns-as-marly-grav-race/)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Event scale:** "A staggering 2550 riders from 26 nations" participated in 2025, making it "the largest one-day gravel event in all of Europe" (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/europes-biggest-gravel-race-returns-as-marly-grav-race/)

**Pro participation:** The race attracts major WorldTour talent including "double vice world champion Florian Vermeersch and his UAE teammate Tim Wellens, alongside Soudal Quick Step's Yves Lampaert and Pascal Eenkhoorn" plus "legendary figures of cycling like Johan Museeuw, Sven Nys, Greg Van Avermaet, Niki Terpstra and Dirk De Wolf" (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/europes-biggest-gravel-race-returns-as-marly-grav-race/)

**Post-race scene:** "Start, finish én nagenieten doe je bij het Shimano Experience Center. Hier vind je niet alleen je rugnummer en het officiële podium, maar ook een gezellige afterparty." (Translation: "Start, finish and post-race enjoyment all happen at the Shimano Experience Center. Here you'll find not only your race number and the official podium, but also a cozy afterparty.") (https://www.bicycling.nl/nieuws/1295327/alles-wat-je-moet-weten-over-marly-grav-race-2025.html)

**Multi-day event:** Beyond the UCI race, Marly Grav is "three days with the best of the gravel world: a UCI gravel race, bikepacking trip, social rides, workshops, bike expo and a unique Limburg festival atmosphere" (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10803/marly-grav-race-and-festival)

**Valkenburg setting:** "Hidden between the moss-green hills, in the heart of the Heuvelland protected nature reserve, the center of the marl town sparkles, with its rich tourist tradition" - the town is "packed with hotels, bars and restaurants" and famous for hosting Amstel Gold Race and multiple UCI World Championships (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/valkenburg/)

**Video perspective [UNKNOWN]:** One rider titled their race video "CHAOS XXL | Marly Grav 2025" describing it as "the chaos and excitement of the Marly Grav Race" (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MPjpAjfhtRE)
