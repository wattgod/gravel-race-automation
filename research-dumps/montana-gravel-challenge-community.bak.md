## Event Structure & Format

The Montana Gravel Challenge is a **3-day, 3-stage event** with the following structure:

**Stage 1 - Friday: Time Trial**
- ~9-11 mile TT (course length has varied by year)
- 100% gravel/dirt
- 756 feet of climbing (2025 course)
- Individual starts, first rider off at 3:30pm

**Stage 2 - Saturday: Southside/Petty Road**
- ~52-56 miles depending on year
- ~3,000 feet of climbing
- Mix of 38 miles paved / 18 miles gravel (2025 course)
- Mass start format with waves (Competitive at 9:30, Recreation/Paracyclist at 9:40)

**Stage 3 - Sunday: Rocky Mountain Roubaix**
- Circuit race, typically 4 laps for ~45 miles
- 1,711 feet of climbing
- 27 miles paved / 18 miles gravel (2025 course)
- Includes a mud climb section
- Mass start with waves

**Division Structure (as of 2024-2025):**
- **Competitive:** Timed results, podium spots, omnium points
- **Recreation:** "Start at your pace and try to finish all three stages" - results posted but no podiums
- **Paracyclist:** Separate omnium
- **Virtual:** Complete courses on own schedule, submit Strava rides

**Omnium Scoring:** Points awarded per stage (1st=20, 2nd=16, 3rd=13, down to 1 point for all finishers outside top 10) across overall and age group categories (12-14, 15-16, 17-18, 19-29, 30-39, 40-49, 50-59, 60-69, 70+)

(https://www.bikesignup.com/Race/MT/Huson/MontanaHellRide, https://competitivetiming.com/montana-gravel-challenge-results/, https://bikingmt.blogspot.com/2024/04/montana-gravel-challenge-2024.html)

## Weather Experienced

**2024 race conditions:** "This weekend was the 3-day montana gravel challenge, and it's raining. Friday we were out setting up the course... Between this morning when I decided to shorten the TT a bit and when we actually raced it got a lot wetter" ... "Was a little disappointed because... dressed for cold and rain which was forecast but didn't really happen, and by the time the hard pedaling began I didn't really have an opportunity to try to take off my gloves and jacket." ... "Day 3... This mud climb is a section I cut out of the TT on friday morning because it was a little iffy and it has only gotten worse over the last few days of periodic rain and cool temps." (https://bikingmt.blogspot.com/2024/04/montana-gravel-challenge-2024.html)

**Date/Timing:** Event consistently held late April (2024: April 26-28; 2023: April 28-30; 2022: April 29-May 1) - early spring in Montana with variable conditions. (https://competitivetiming.com/montana-gravel-challenge-results/)

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Stage 1 TT specifics [COMPETITIVE perspective]:** "It's rolling and and uphill finish so having something left for that is good" ... "definitely good call to avoid the twisty descent that was already pretty fucked before it rained more" (https://bikingmt.blogspot.com/2024/04/montana-gravel-challenge-2024.html)

**Stage 2 specifics:** "Day 2 has been the long gravel stage of the race with 3 being a more road circuit. This year we didn't do the long gravel loop because it's a lot of logistics and probably kind of dangerous especially toward the end of the 1 lane gravel road where you're descending around blind corners." ... "2nd day more pavement, with a few out and backs, one pretty gradually uphill for like 10mi, the next shorter with more climbing. The 2nd section was muddy and slick" (https://bikingmt.blogspot.com/2024/04/montana-gravel-challenge-2024.html)

**Stage 3 specifics:** "Day 3 was the 'road race' with an out on dirt to a 10mi circuit that's mostly paved, with a climb on mud but thankfully the descent side was dry." ... "After the climb and descent on the circuit... it's about 2k generally uphill to the finish." (https://bikingmt.blogspot.com/2024/04/montana-gravel-challenge-2024.html)

**Course changes by year:** Event format has evolved - 2022 had different stage names (Ninemile Road TT, Southside Road, Rocky Mountain Roubaix), 2021 had different stage structure, 2018 included "Montana Hell Ride" as a stage name. Course details vary year to year. (https://competitivetiming.com/montana-gravel-challenge-results/)

## Location & Logistics

**Race HQ:** 18450 Six Mile Road, Huson, MT 59846 (near Missoula) (https://www.bikesignup.com/Race/MT/Huson/MontanaHellRide)
