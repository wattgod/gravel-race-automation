- 129 total racers across three distances
- Winner **Justin Filuk [ELITE]** completed the 40km Impulso in 2:13:10 (https://www.webscorer.com/racealldetails?raceid=394540)
- Part of the 2025 Trek BC Gravel Series presented by Bluebird Self Storage (https://cyclinggreatescapes.com/nanaimo-unpaved-gravel-fondo-results-and-photos-round-3-of-2025-trek-bc-gravel-series/)
- Hosted by Mosaic Forest Management on their private gravel roads (https://cyclinggreatescapes.com/nanaimo-unpaved-gravel-fondo-results-and-photos-round-3-of-2025-trek-bc-gravel-series/)
- Uses Nanaimo Motocross Association track as venue (https://cyclinggreatescapes.com/nanaimo-unpaved-gravel-fondo-results-and-photos-round-3-of-2025-trek-bc-gravel-series/)

**2024 Edition (June 16, 2024):**

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

The course utilizes **Mosaic Forest Management's private gravel roads**, giving access to terrain not typically available to the public (https://cyclinggreatescapes.com/nanaimo-unpaved-gravel-fondo-results-and-photos-round-3-of-2025-trek-bc-gravel-series/)

The race incorporates the **Nanaimo Motocross Association track** as part of the venue/course (https://cyclinggreatescapes.com/nanaimo-unpaved-gravel-fondo-results-and-photos-round-3-of-2025-trek-bc-gravel-series/)

## Race Format & Logistics

**Start Format:**
- 2024: Mass start for all riders (https://www.webscorer.com/race?raceid=355715)
- 2025: Manual wave start with self-seeding (fastest riders in front) at 9:30 AM (https://www.webscorer.com/racealldetails?raceid=394540 and https://www.bcgravelseries.com/nanaimounpaved)

**Race Day Schedule (2025):**
- 7:30-9:00 AM: Check-in and package pick-up
- 9:15 AM: Greetings and rider's briefing
- 9:30 AM: All riders start
- 11:00 AM-3:00 PM: Food truck and drinks available
- 3:00 PM: Awards presentations
(https://www.bcgravelseries.com/nanaimounpaved)

**Pre-Race:**
- Saturday before race (10:00 AM-5:30 PM): Check-in and race package pick-up at Arrowsmith Bikes, 4300 Wellington Rd, Nanaimo (https://www.bcgravelseries.com/nanaimounpaved)

**Location:**
Nanaimo MX Association Race Track, Weigles Rd, Nanaimo, BC V9T 3L9 (https://www.bcgravelseries.com/nanaimounpaved)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

Part of a **3-race Vancouver Island Gravel Series** alongside River City Gravel Fondo (Campbell River, April) and Burnt Bridge Classic (Duncan, May), with Nanaimo Unpaved as the finale event (https://zone4.ca/~bzKM9Z/)

The series is **presented by Trek Bicycle** with support from Bluebird Self Storage and Liquor Plus sponsorship (https://cyclinggreatescapes.com/nanaimo-unpaved-gravel-fondo-results-and-photos-round-3-of-2025-trek-bc-gravel-series/ and https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6iidX_vlUJ0)

Post-race amenities include **food trucks and drinks available from 11 AM-3 PM**, creating a festival atmosphere for finishers (https://www.bcgravelseries.com/nanaimounpaved)

**Professional race photography** by Jay Wallace documents the event (https://cyclinggreatescapes.com/nanaimo-unpaved-gravel-fondo-results-and-photos-round-3-of-2025-trek-bc-gravel-series/)
