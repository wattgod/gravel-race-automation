## Race Overview & Context

Nanaimo Unpaved is part of the Trek BC Gravel Series (formerly known as MIVA Unpaved before rebranding). The race offers three distance options: Impulso (~40-42km), Intermedio (~70-85km), and Esperto (~110-120km). The venue is at Nanaimo MX Association Race Track on Weigles Rd, with racing taking place in mid-June.

The race uses RFID chip timing (Impinj reader) and features a manual wave start grouped by distance. In 2025, there were 129 racers; in 2024, there were 107 racers.

**Course location:** The race uses gravel roads on MosaicForests land and incorporates sections of the Nanaimo Motocross Association track (https://cyclinggreatescapes.com/nanaimo-unpaved-gravel-fondo-results-and-photos-round-3-of-2025-trek-bc-gravel-series/).

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

The Impulso course features **relatively flat terrain with limited steep climbing**. Based on race results showing tight finish times and percentage differences, the course appears to reward sustained power rather than climbing ability.

**Course profile characteristics:**
- Campbell River XC course (different event, same region) had "less than 180m of elevation gain per lap made the overall course feel pan-flat" [ELITE] (https://bookofchad.ca/category/race-reports/)
- Cumberland area features "long and wide grass straights, tight corners, and mountain bike terrain" [ELITE] (https://bookofchad.ca/category/race-reports/)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Fast starts are critical [ELITE]:** From regional race experience: "The start went up a fire road, and the first minute of the ride started quickly, not xco quick, but 400 watts average for the first minute quick. I managed to sit on as the front group got strung out" - Chad Grice [ELITE] discussing similar Vancouver Island races (https://bookofchad.ca/category/race-reports/)

**Pacing context for Impulso distance [ELITE]:** The winning time of 2:13:10 for 40km suggests an average speed of ~18 km/h, with top finishers clustered within 5 minutes of the winner. The field spreads significantly after the top 10, with times ranging up to 3:46:26 for the last finisher.

## DNF Risk Factors

**2025 DNF data:** 2 DNFs recorded in the Impulso 40km race out of 32 starters:
- William Goodwin (F2C, Masters 55-64, age 61)
- Colin Darling (Masters 55-64, age 60)

Both DNFs were in the Masters 55-64 category (https://www.webscorer.com/racealldetails?raceid=394540).

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Event schedule and amenities:**
- Saturday packet pickup from 10am-5:30pm at Arrowsmith Bikes
- Sunday morning check-in from 7:30-9:00am
- 9:15am rider briefing
- 9:30am mass start (self-seeded with fastest riders in front)
- Food truck and drinks from 11am-3pm
- Awards at 3pm

(https://www.bcgravelseries.com/nanaimounpaved)

**Series context:** Nanaimo Unpaved is Round 3 of the Trek BC Gravel Series, following River City Gravel Fondo (Campbell River, April) and Burnt Bridge Classic (Duncan, May), with a final event in Cumberland in September (https://cyclinggreatescapes.com/nanaimo-unpaved-gravel-fondo-results-and-photos-round-3-of-2025-trek-bc-gravel-series/).

## Age Group Participation Patterns

The race attracts a broad age range with strong Masters participation:

**Impulso (40km) 2025 [ELITE/COMPETITIVE]:**
