# NOOSA GRAVEL ENDURO — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Equipment & Gear Recommendations

**[COMPETITIVE] Course Designer Recommendation:** "Gravel or Cyclo Cross bikes are perfect. You could do on a mountain bike if you wish but it will be very slow. E bikes allowed. Tyres – bigger is better! 34mm + knobby robust tyres are recommended. Gear ratio's – the terrain is hilly, so gear ratios should be selected on individual ability. While not super steep, is still long so it's recommended to go smaller if in doubt of where you're fitness is at." (https://noosaenduro.com.au/adventure-gravel/)

**[UNKNOWN] Real-World Test:** "I am on the road bike with 23mm Maxxis Refuse tyres and there is gravel and single trail!" — rider sneaking onto the back of the 2022 Noosa Gravel Enduro (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eKgU9fRY6-4)

**[COMPETITIVE] Footwear Note:** "Beginner riders may have to dismount on a few occasions for some very short sections and as such, gravel or mountain bike shoes are recommended. Experienced off-road riders shouldn't need to during the ride as a general rule." (https://noosaenduro.com.au/adventure-gravel/)

**[COMPETITIVE] Essential Self-Sufficiency:** "It's always recommended that riders carry their own phone, pump, spares and basic tools. Unrepairable bike problems or injuries and we can arrange a pickup shuttle if you have your phone on you to communicate with us." (https://noosaenduro.com.au/adventure-gravel/)

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Surface Mix:** "The courses are extremely varied and contain mixed surfaces like sealed, gravel and private roads, fire roads, single track, pitted fields, rocky sections, rapid downhills and some very technical sections." (https://localsintheloop.com.au/listing/noosa-enduro-2024/)

**60km Breakdown [UNKNOWN]:** "The 60km is approximately 20% single track, 50% forest road, 30% bitumen or other (concrete bike path etc)." (https://noosaenduro.com.au/adventure-gravel/)

**100km MTB Breakdown [UNKNOWN]:** "The 100km is approximately 40% is single track, 40% gravel roads, and 20% bitumen or other (concrete bike path etc)." (https://noosaenduro.com.au/adventure-gravel/)

**Gravel Course Evolution [COMPETITIVE]:** "Our new course still has the classic multi-terrain feel of the Noosa Enduro taking in all of Noosa's epic hinterland along some premium graded gravel roads, sealed roads, fire roads, farms, track and trails. While it might sound like a mountain bike ride, we can assure it's not! This course is designed 100% for gravel bikes... We have removed a few of the most difficult trail sections from last year though, making it a course that you should be able to ride around with no dismount sections required." (https://noosaenduro.com.au/adventure-gravel/)

**Gravel Specs [COMPETITIVE]:** "The Noosa Gravel Enduro course is a more widely achievable 90km in length but with approximately 1250m of climbing" (https://noosaenduro.com.au/adventure-gravel/)

**Challenge Zones [ELITE]:** "This course has everything, from tracks and trails to some of the most challenging climbs at Mt Tinbeerwah, Sheppersons Hill and Cooran Tablelands Range." (https://www.accommodationinnoosa.com.au/noosa-enduro-2025-a-challenging-riding-experience/)

**140km Elite Course [ELITE]:** "Including a 1770m vertical climb, and premium gravel tracks, this course is only for those with an above average fitness level." (https://www.accommodationinnoosa.com.au/noosa-enduro-2025-a-challenging-riding-experience/)

**100km Gravel Signature Climb [ELITE]:** "Immerse yourself in the breath-taking views as you climb 6km up Mother Mountain." (https://www.accommodationinnoosa.com.au/noosa-enduro-2025-a-challenging-riding-experience/)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Time Expectations [ELITE]:** "This course is designed for dedicated and well-seasoned bikers. It can take even the most experienced riders up to ten hours to complete." — referring to the 100km MTB course (https://www.accommodationinnoosa.com.au/noosa-enduro-2025-a-challenging-riding-experience/)

**Course Suitability [RECREATIONAL]:** "The 60km event is designed for those not ready to take on the 100km. We also have a 30km ride suitable for any ability." (https://noosaenduro.com.au/adventure-gravel/)

**Cutoff Times:** "12.00pm at Woondum National Park" — cutoff for Gravel Enduro course (https://noosaenduro.com.au/adventure-gravel/)

**Support Infrastructure [ALL LEVELS]:** "There will be three food and water stations across the course to allow for plenty of opportunity for hydration and nutrition." (https://noosaenduro.com.au/adventure-gravel/)

**Checkpoints:** "Pomona, Woondum NP, Ringtail Forest" listed as checkpoint locations on gravel course (https://noosaenduro.com.au/adventure-gravel/)
