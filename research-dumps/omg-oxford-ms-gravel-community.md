## Race Overview & Context

OMG (Oh My God!) is the first race in the Mississippi Gravel Cup series, held in Oxford, MS in the Holly Springs National Forest. The race offers 25, 50, and 100-mile distances with multiple age and category divisions. The 100-mile course features over 7,000 feet of elevation gain on rolling hills and well-packed gravel service roads. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/5842/oh-my-god-kickstarts-mississippi-gravel-cup-p/b-lauf)

The Mississippi Gravel Cup series was created by Matt Roland, Jason Shearer, and Landon Voller, with races held across four different parts of Mississippi to provide varying terrain experiences. Organizer Jason Shearer noted: "We're really proud to bring people into our Mississippi communities and experience Mississippi on two wheels." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/5842/oh-my-god-kickstarts-mississippi-gravel-cup-p/b-lauf)

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

The course runs through Holly Springs National Forest and is characterized by "rolling hills, fun descents, gorgeous forest views and well-packed gravel service roads." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/5842/oh-my-god-kickstarts-mississippi-gravel-cup-p/b-lauf)

The 100-mile route conquers over 7,000 feet of elevation gain, indicating sustained climbing challenges throughout the distance. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/5842/oh-my-god-kickstarts-mississippi-gravel-cup-p/b-lauf)

The 25 and 50-mile courses are offered with ride leaders "for the newly initiated or who wanted to explore gravel riding," suggesting these distances are more accessible for developing riders. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/5842/oh-my-god-kickstarts-mississippi-gravel-cup-p/b-lauf)

## DNF Risk Factors

The 2024 race saw notable DNF rates among competitive riders. Multiple riders from Marx-Bensdorf/BPC Cycling Team dropped out despite being in contention early, including:
- Kellan Maxwell (DNF after lap 1 in 2:42:28.9)
- Scott Rollins (DNF after lap 1 in 2:43:15.3)
- Aaron Tubergen (DNF after lap 1 in 2:48:05.1)

Several experienced riders with team affiliations also DNF'd without completing even one lap, suggesting challenging course conditions or technical issues can strike at any level. (https://www.webscorer.com/results?raceid=339078&did=429275&gender=M)

## Weather Experienced

**2021 race:** The event experienced unexpected winter conditions. Local cyclist Bart Luther noted: "Having snowflakes in Mississippi was a bonus!" indicating riders faced snow during the race. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/5842/oh-my-god-kickstarts-mississippi-gravel-cup-p/b-lauf)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

The 2021 event was limited to 160 riders due to COVID-19 protocols, with "masked riders in waves of 50" starting the race. Despite restrictions, organizers provided "Premium swag, a sick finishers patch, and fist bumps and socially distanced air hugs." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/5842/oh-my-god-kickstarts-mississippi-gravel-cup-p/b-lauf)

Local cyclist Bart Luther [ELITE] commented: "Seeing all the smiles, seeing all the exhilaration, I think sometimes competition is good for the soul." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/5842/oh-my-god-kickstarts-mississippi-gravel-cup-p/b-lauf)

The event provides aid stations stocked with water, sports drink and assorted snacks, plus a post-race meal. During COVID, aid stations used individually packaged snacks and bottled water with masked volunteers. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/5842/oh-my-god-kickstarts-mississippi-gravel-cup-p/b-lauf)

Organizer Jason Shearer noted strong demand: "What we were excited and surprised to see was that those riders signed up in minutes. Everybody was ready to get back on their bikes." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/5842/oh-my-god-kickstarts-mississippi-gravel-cup-p/b-lauf)

The race attracts regional participation, with the 2021 event drawing "riders from all around Mississippi and even people from eight other states as far as Iowa and Colorado." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/5842/oh-my-god-kickstarts-mississippi-gravel-cup-p/b-lauf)

Podium prizes are awarded three deep in each category, with multiple age group and specialty categories including Open, Master (46-59), Sexagenarian (60+), Clydesdale/Athena, Junior, and Singlespeed divisions. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/5842/oh-my-god-kickstarts-mississippi-gravel-cup-p/b-lauf)
