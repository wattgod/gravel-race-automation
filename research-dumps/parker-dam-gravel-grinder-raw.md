# PARKER DAM GRAVEL GRINDER - RAW RESEARCH DUMP

## OFFICIAL DATA

**Race Dates**

The 2026 Parker Dam Gravel Grinder is scheduled for **Saturday, May 9, 2026**[1][4][5][7]. The 2025 edition took place on **Saturday, May 10, 2025**[3][6][8].

**Location & Start Details**

The race is held in **Penfield, PA** in Clearfield County[1][4][5]. The specific venue is **S.B. Elliott State Park, Old Route 153, Penfield, PA 15849**[5]. Race start time is **9:00 AM** on race day[5][7].

**Race Day Schedule (2026)**

- Registration opens at 7:00 AM
- Registration closes at 8:30 AM[4]

**Distance Options & Elevation**

The race offers three route options[2]:

| Route | Distance | Elevation Gain |
|-------|----------|-----------------|
| Full Series | 71.7 miles | 5,707 feet |
| Half Series | 51.9 miles | 4,188 feet |
| Short Series | 23.1 miles | 1,680 feet |

*Note: The search results note that "distances may not be exact, they are based off of Ride With GPS route planner and have not been verified in person."[2]

**Aid Stations & Cutoff Times**

For the Full Series: Aid stations at Mile 25 and Mile 48, with cutoff times of 11:30 AM (Aid Station #1) and 2:30 PM (Aid Station #2)[2].

For the Half Series: One aid station at Mile 28 with a 12:00 PM cutoff time[2].

For the Short Series: No aid stations and no cutoff time[2].

**Registration & Pricing**

- Registration opened December 8, 2025 for the 2026 event[1]

- Online registration closes at 11:00 PM, **Thursday, May 7, 2026**[2]
