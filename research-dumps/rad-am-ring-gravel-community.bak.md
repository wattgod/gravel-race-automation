## Equipment & Gear Recommendations

**[UNKNOWN] Flachlandtiroler_:** Wide tires are essential for the fast descents. Ran 43mm front and 38mm rear on a Stevens Super Prestige, but noted that "breite Reifen auf den schnellen Abfahrten im Vorteil" (wide tires are advantageous on the fast descents). Despite this, experienced significant hand and back fatigue, more so than leg fatigue, suggesting even wider may be beneficial. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/rad-am-ring-2025-erstes-gravel-race-am-nürburgring.196393/page-2)

**[UNKNOWN] Multiple riders:** Many riders experienced flats in the first lap, particularly noted were glass shards on the most technical descent section. Pre-race tire sealant check recommended. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/rad-am-ring-2025-erstes-gravel-race-am-nürburgring.196393/page-2)

**Equipment notes:** Disc wheels are NOT allowed for the time trial due to crosswind concerns on the Nordschleife sections. (https://www.tour-magazin.de/event/jedermann-rennen/rad-am-ring-2025-restplatze-fur-gravel-tt-und-rtf/)

## Weather Experienced

**2025 Race Conditions [UNKNOWN riders]:** Hot and oppressive ("warmen und drückenden Wetter"), dry and dusty conditions ("staubtrockenen Bedingungen"). Multiple riders mentioned dehydration risks. The dust was so severe on the steep gravel descents that riders described it as a "Staubapokalypse" (dust apocalypse) with speeds exceeding 60 km/h on the first lap. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/rad-am-ring-2025-erstes-gravel-race-am-nürburgring.196393/page-2 and https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/07/20/rad-am-ring-gravel-race-100km-rennbericht/)

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course surfaces:** The first 3 km follow the Grand Prix track before transitioning onto the Nordschleife. After the Hatzenbach section, the route becomes predominantly off-road with loose gravel on steep, winding descents. (https://radamring.de/en/the-course-is-set-and-what-a-course-it-is/)

**Technical sections:** 
- The route includes two tight transitions from road to off-road that create bottlenecks, forcing dismounts
- One very steep, rooty section that most riders had to walk
- The most technical descent is covered in glass shards (2025 conditions)
- One section features a 17% maximum gradient between the "Karussell" and "Hohe Acht"
- A specific wurzelige (rooty) steep climb that riders report riding 3 out of 4 laps
- The surface changes from loose gravel in early laps to more packed conditions after traffic

**Course profile:** 25 km loop with approximately 300m elevation gain per lap. The main climb covers 300m over 5.5 km to the "Hohen Acht" section. Total for 100 km: ~2,000-2,300m elevation. (https://radamring.de/en/gravel-race_eng/ and https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/rad-am-ring-2025-erstes-gravel-race-am-nürburgring.196393/page-2)

**Feed zones:** Feed & tech zone is located on the Nordschleife after the GP track transition in an uphill section (area T 13). Outside assistance is permitted but activities on downhill sections and certain Nordschleife sections are prohibited. (https://radamring.de/en/gravel-race_eng/)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**[UNKNOWN] Flachlandtiroler_:** First lap was fastest despite not knowing the descents, simply by following other riders. Second lap intentionally slowed down to ride by power meter. Drank approximately 4.5L total (started with 1.3L, refilled after each lap). Notes this was likely excessive as needed to urinate at 3h mark and finished with one full bottle. Had increased hydration after previous cramping incident at another race. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/rad-am-ring-2025-erstes-gravel-race-am-nürburgring.196393/page-2)

**[UNKNOWN] borki:** Drank 1.6L during a 2-lap (50km) race and still experienced cramping in both legs in the final lap at the Döttinger Höhe bridge section. Suggests hydration needs may be higher than expected given the heat. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/rad-am-ring-2025-erstes-gravel-race-am-nürburgring.196393/page-2)

**[UNKNOWN] Renée:** Long climbs favored her riding style and allowed making up time lost on technical descents where caution was necessary due to debris. Recommends being conservative on technical descents if unsure, time can be made up on climbs. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/07/20/rad-am-ring-gravel-race-100km-rennbericht/)

**Overtaking dynamics [UNKNOWN]:** Despite nearly 200 riders and high speeds (60+ km/h) on dusty descents in lap 1, riders reported surprisingly courteous and skilled overtaking behavior. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/rad-am-ring-2025-erstes-gravel-race-am-nürburgring.196393/page-2)

**Elite pace reference [ELITE] Paul Voß:** Fastest lap was 47:XX minutes. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/rad-am-ring-2025-erstes-gravel-race-am-nürburgring.196393/page-2)

**Time limits:** For the 100 km race, the time limit for three laps is 4:30:00. Riders crossing after this time are classified as having finished, but with completed laps counted at net time rather than full distance. The 50 km race ends when the winner crosses the line. (https://radamring.de/en/gravel-race_eng/)

## DNF Risk Factors

**High DNF rate (2025):** Only 93 of 192 starters (48%) in the 100km race made it to the finish line. Many riders abandoned after 1-2 laps, with Strava data showing heat and underestimation of difficulty as primary factors. Multiple riders were seen walking/pushing bikes. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/rad-am-ring-2025-erstes-gravel-race-am-nürburgring.196393/page-2)

**Mechanical issues:** High number of flats in the first lap, particularly due to glass on the most technical descent. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/rad-am-ring-2025-erstes-gravel-race-am-nürburgring.196393/page-2)

**Dehydration/cramping:** Multiple riders reported cramping issues despite consuming significant fluids. The hot, oppressive conditions in 2025 caught many riders out. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/rad-am-ring-2025-erstes-gravel-race-am-nürburgring.196393/page-2 and https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/07/20/rad-am-ring-gravel-race-100km-rennbericht/)

**Expectations mismatch:** One rider noted that "so mancher machte den Eindruck mit ganz anderen Vorstellungen dort hin gekommen zu sein" (some gave the impression they arrived with completely different expectations). (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/rad-am-ring-2025-erstes-gravel-race-am-nürburgring.196393/page-2)

**Lapped riders:** Unlike some other events, lapped riders were NOT removed from the race, though there's a time cutoff system ("a.K." = außer Karenz/outside time limit). Elite riders were lapping mid-pack at approximately 2:40 into the race. (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/rad-am-ring-2025-erstes-gravel-race-am-nürburgring.196393/page-2)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Event organization:** Multiple riders praised the organization. "Ansonsten war es eine astreine Veranstaltung, die Nr.-Ausgabe etc. lief sehr flüssig, wir hatten ohne anzustehen unsere Nummern und auch sonst fand ich für die Anzahl an Teilnehmern sehr gut abgewickelt." (Otherwise it was a top-notch event, number distribution etc. ran very smoothly, we got our numbers without queuing and overall I found it very well executed for the number of participants.) (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/rad-am-ring-2025-erstes-gravel-race-am-nürburgring.196393/page-2)

**On-course support:** Strong trackside support noted, with specific mention of Mario, Cari, Tim & Bella providing excellent nutrition support at consistent points each lap, which was crucial in the hot conditions. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/07/20/rad-am-ring-gravel-race-100km-rennbericht/)

**Unique atmosphere:** Start and finish in the Formula 1 arena, part of the larger "Grand Depart" where over 10,000 participants across various disciplines start in succession. The route runs on/along the legendary Nordschleife ("Green Hell") without crossing public roads. (https://radamring.de/en/new-at-rad-am-ring/ and https://radamring.de/en/gravel-race_eng/)

**Course patron:** German gravel icon and multiple German Champion Paul Voß actively supported course development and won the inaugural 2025 edition, then returned as defending champion. (https://radamring.de/en/new-at-rad-am-ring/ and https://www.bike-x.de/alle-radarten/gravelbike/titelkaempfe-bei-rad-am-ring-gravelbike-dm-2026-am-nuerburgring/)

**Size concerns:** One rider noted: "Hoffe aber das die Starterfelder in Zukunft nicht viel größer werden" (Hope the starting fields don't get much bigger in the future), suggesting the ~200-rider field felt near capacity for the technical course. Another countered that it "Verdient mehr Starter" (deserves more starters). (https://www.rennrad-news.de/forum/threads/rad-am-ring-2025-erstes-gravel-race-am-nürburgring.196393/page-2)

**Prize money:** €5,200 total prize purse with separate classifications for men and women, plus age group categories. Top prize is €1,300. (https://radamring.de/en/gravel-race_eng/ and https://radamring.de/en/the-course-is-set-and-what-a-course-it-is/)

**Championship venue:** The 2026 edition will host the German Gravel Bike Championships (Gravelbike-DM) on July 24-26, 2026, with women racing 4 laps (~100km/2,200m) and men racing 5 laps (~125km/2,750m). (https://www.bike-x.de/alle-radarten/gravelbike/titelkaempfe-bei-rad-am-ring-gravelbike-dm-2026-am-nuerburgring/)
