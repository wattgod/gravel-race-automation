## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course structure:** The 100km race consists of 4 laps of 25km each with a total of 2000m elevation gain (approximately 550m per lap). (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/07/20/rad-am-ring-gravel-race-100km-rennbericht/)

**Surface conditions:** The first lap featured loose gravel on steep and winding descents requiring sliding through corners rather than steering. Conditions improved on subsequent laps as the surface settled. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/07/20/rad-am-ring-gravel-race-100km-rennbericht/)

**Technical features:** 
- Two narrow transitions from road to terrain that create bottlenecks forcing dismounts
- At least one very steep, rooty section that required walking
- Glass shards present on the most challenging descent
- Long climbs that favor strong climbers
(https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/07/20/rad-am-ring-gravel-race-100km-rennbericht/)

**Course routing:** The route leads "on or along the legendary Nürburgring Nordschleife – i.e. directly or almost through the 'Green Hell'. Public roads are neither used nor crossed. The start and finish are in the fascinating Formula 1 Arena." The different distances are designed as lap races. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12983/rad-am-ring-gravel-race)

**Course design input:** "The newly crowned German champion and former Nürburgring winner Paul Voß assisted in the composition" of the gravel race course. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12983/rad-am-ring-gravel-race)

**Paul Voß [ELITE - German Champion]:** "Die Teilnehmenden erwartet eine abwechslungsreiche, anspruchsvolle Strecke, die aber auch für Neueinsteiger machbar ist" (Translation: Participants can expect a varied, challenging course that is also manageable for newcomers). He also noted: "Das Fahren auf der Rennstrecke ist für mich ein Highlight" (Riding on the race track is a highlight for me). After three kilometers on the modern Grand Prix track, "führt das Rennen abwechselnd über die legendäre Nordschleife oder auf Schotterwegen und Trails abseits des Asphalts." (the race alternates between the legendary Nordschleife or on gravel paths and trails off the asphalt). "Es warten klassische Schotterwege und ein paar schöne Singletrails" (Classic gravel paths and some nice singletrails await). (https://www.bike-x.de/alle-radarten/gravelbike/rad-am-ring-gravel-race-gravelbike-premiere-auf-dem-nuerburgring/)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Climbing advantage [UNKNOWN]:** One rider noted that long climbs suited them well and allowed them to make up time lost on technical descents where they rode cautiously. This suggests strong climbers can use the ascents strategically to offset time lost on sketchy descents. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/07/20/rad-am-ring-gravel-race-100km-rennbericht/)

**First lap considerations:** The first lap is the most technically challenging with loose gravel on descents. Riders should expect conditions to improve on subsequent laps as the surface settles. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/07/20/rad-am-ring-gravel-race-100km-rennbericht/)

**Support strategy [UNKNOWN]:** "Richtig tollen Support an der Strecke haben u.a. Mario, Cari, Tim & Bella gemacht – hier konnte man wirklich jede Runde darauf zählen, dass man top verpflegt wurde." (Mario, Cari, Tim & Bella provided really great support on the course - you could really count on being well supplied every lap.) On-course support at consistent points each lap was noted as "enormously important" especially in hot, humid conditions. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/07/20/rad-am-ring-gravel-race-100km-rennbericht/)

## DNF Risk Factors

**Puncture risk:** Glass shards littered the most challenging descent, creating significant flat tire risk. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/07/20/rad-am-ring-gravel-race-100km-rennbericht/)

**Bottleneck hazards:** Two narrow transitions from road to terrain create bottlenecks that force dismounts, potentially causing crashes or delays in congested conditions. (https://rsv-rheinadler.de/wordpress/2025/07/20/rad-am-ring-gravel-race-100km-rennbericht/)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Event scale:** "Rad Am Ring is a weekend-long festival of cycling with 5,500 competitors" in 2017, growing to record participation of 12,000 cycling fans in 2025. (https://www.bikeradar.com/features/routes-and-rides/heres-what-its-like-to-race-germanys-24-hour-rad-am-ring and https://nuerburgring.de/news/rad-am-ring-2025-ueberragende-stimmung-in-der-gruenen-hoelle?locale=en)

**Atmosphere:** Event director Hanns-Martin Fraas called it "a celebration on every level" and "Es war brilliant, sowohl was die Wetterbedingungen angeht als auch alles andere" (It was brilliant, both in terms of weather conditions and everything else). He noted: "I'm thrilled by the discipline of 98 percent of our participants" who adhered to rules throughout. The event had "Outstanding atmosphere in the Green Hell" with "waves of emotion erupted among athletes, spectators and the organizing team." (https://nuerburgring.de/news/rad-am-ring-2025-ueberragende-stimmung-in-der-gruenen-hoelle?locale=en)

**Venue uniqueness:** The legendary Nordschleife circuit, nicknamed "Green Hell" by Formula One legend Jackie Stewart after his 1968 win there, serves as the centerpiece. Start and finish are in the Formula 1 Arena. Public roads are neither used nor crossed. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12983/rad-am-ring-gravel-race and https://nuerburgring.de/news/rad-am-ring-2025-ueberragende-stimmung-in-der-gruenen-hoelle?locale=en)

**Multiple events:** The gravel race is "embedded" in the larger Rad am Ring cycling festival, which includes multiple race formats and distances running simultaneously throughout the weekend. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12983/rad-am-ring-gravel-race)

**Prize money:** The 100km race is "open to everyone and with tempting prize money" totaling 5200 Euro for the five fastest women and men. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12983/rad-am-ring-gravel-race and https://www.bike-x.de/alle-radarten/gravelbike/rad-am-ring-gravel-race-gravelbike-premiere-auf-dem-nuerburgring/)

**Expo and demos:** The event features "hundreds more supporting, spectating, enjoying the atmosphere and running the expo and bike demos." (https://www.bikeradar.com/features/routes-and-rides/heres-what-its-like-to-race-germanys-24-hour-rad-am-ring)
