# RADL GRVL — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

The event is described as having "a diverse and communal atmosphere that's as competitive and challenging as it is enjoyable" with professional cyclists competing alongside first-time gravel racers (https://sportivebreaks.com/en-au/gravel/radl-grvl-2025/).

**Event Format:** RADL GRVL is "a four-day cycling experience and race at the Tour Down Under" where "friends, family and top professional cyclists take on three world-class, fully supported, routes" ranging from 35km to 130km, including the 130k Oceania Gravel Championships (UCI Gravel World Championship Qualifier) (https://www.radlgrvl.com/radl-overview).

**Tiffany Cromwell [ELITE]:** "I've spent years racing professionally, and the unpaved roads around my hometown of Adelaide are the reason I fell in love with gravel cycling." (https://www.radlgrvl.com)

**Unique Social Events:**
- **Quad Lock Budgy Ride:** Hosts Tiffany Cromwell, Valtteri Bottas, Amy Charity, and Ryan Steers lead a shakeout ride to Moseley Beach Club "for a surfside party and frolic in the sea" (https://www.radlgrvl.com/radl-overview)
- **Schwalbe Pro Panel:** "A spectacle that's not to be missed" featuring "competition, shenanigans, trivia, and Q&A on the big stage with some of the biggest names in cycling… and beyond" (https://www.radlgrvl.com/radl-overview)
- **Valtteri Bottas Sausage Sizzle:** Exclusive ticketed event at Willunga Hill where "Valtteri will be manning the grill and slinging sausages" while spectators watch the Santos Tour Down Under Men's Stage 4 (https://www.radlgrvl.com/radl-overview)
- **Buffalo Bash:** Post-race party at Electra House in Downtown Adelaide celebrating "the life changing power of the bike" with World Bicycle Relief. Riders receive complimentary entry (https://www.radlgrvl.com/radl-overview)

**Event Heritage:** Founded by the creators of SBT GRVL (Steamboat, Colorado) in partnership with F1 driver Valtteri Bottas and professional cyclist Tiffany Cromwell. The GRVL brand is "associated with a premium cycling experience in unique locations" (https://sportivebreaks.com/en-au/gravel/radl-grvl-2025/).

**Integration with Santos Tour Down Under:** The event takes place at the Santos Tour Down Under Village in downtown Adelaide, with opportunities to watch professional WorldTour racing stages throughout the weekend (https://www.radlgrvl.com).

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Location:** The race takes place in McLaren Vale and surrounding areas near Adelaide, South Australia (https://sportivebreaks.com/en-au/gravel/radl-grvl-2025/).

**Course Options:** Three distance options available: 35km, mid-distance (unspecified), and 130km, with the 130km serving as the Oceania Gravel Championships (https://www.radlgrvl.com/radl-overview).

**Training Rides [ELITE/VIP package]:**
- Thursday: Tarmac ride from Adelaide to Norwood, then to Uraidla "via a combination of tarmac and gravel roads" - approximately 40-60km with 800m elevation gain (https://sportivebreaks.com/en-au/gravel/radl-grvl-2025/)
- Saturday: Guided ride to Willunga Hill - approximately 60+km with 800m elevation gain (https://sportivebreaks.com/en-au/gravel/radl-grvl-2025/)

**Notable Landmarks:** Willunga Hill is described as "iconic" and features prominently in both the RADL GRVL experience and the Santos Tour Down Under racing (https://sportivebreaks.com/en-au/gravel/radl-grvl-2025/).

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Course Support [ALL LEVELS]:** All routes are described as "fully supported" (https://www.radlgrvl.com/radl-overview).

**VIP Package Support [ELITE - for those booking premium packages]:** 
- "Mechanical support: On hand all weekend" with expert mechanics available at the hotel
- Support vehicle accompanies guided training rides
- Race day transport from Adelaide to McLaren Vale included
(https://sportivebreaks.com/en-au/gravel/radl-grvl-2025/)
