# RIVER CITY GRAVEL FONDO — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Event Overview & Logistics

**Location & Organization:** Campbell River, BC, on Vancouver Island. The event is part of the Trek BC Gravel Series (previously Mosaic Gravel Series) and uses gravel roads through City of Campbell River, Mosaic Forest Management land, BC Parks, and local First Nations territory. Start location at Pedal Your World Bike Shop (465 Merecroft Rd) or We Wai Kai Sports Field depending on year.

**Race Distances:**
- Impulso: ~32-40km (varies by year)
- Intermedio: 70-72km
- Esperto: 113-120km (varies by year)

**Participation:** Strong turnout with 178 riders in the inaugural 2024 event, growing to 216 riders in 2025, with over 250 mentioned for another year. (https://www.panachecyclingsports.com/post/river-city-gravel-fondo-a-huge-success, https://www.webscorer.com/race?raceid=386987, https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dm41yeTkcEs)

**Course Designer:** Routes designed by Canadian Masters Gravel Champion John Vanderveen. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12111/river-city-gravel-fondo)

**Event Itinerary (2026):**
- Saturday check-in: 10:00am-5:30pm at bike shop
- Sunday check-in: 7:30am-9:00am
- 9:15am: Rider's briefing
- 9:30am: All riders start (self-seeded, fastest in front)
- 11:00am-3:00pm: Food truck & drinks
- 3:00pm: Awards
(https://www.bcgravelseries.com/rivercitygravelfondo)

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course Description [UNKNOWN]:** "Fun and fast rollers through the forest to Campbell Lake and back." Terrain characterized as "moderate terrain with rolling short steep hills or moderate, sustained climbs." Surface is "mostly gravel." (https://www.thegravelcyclistsguidetobritishcolumbia.ca/ride-list/campbell-river-river-city-fondo, https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/12111/river-city-gravel-fondo)

**Intermedio (2025) Stats [UNKNOWN]:** 72km distance with 621m elevation gain. (https://www.thegravelcyclistsguidetobritishcolumbia.ca/ride-list/campbell-river-river-city-fondo)

**Major Route Change Alert [UNKNOWN]:** The original route is "no longer rideable as such due to a 6-year construction plan on the dam where the lake is crossed (i.e. presumably till about 2032)." Organizers indicate "River City Gravel Fondo course will be re-routed for 2026 because the original route is no longer rideable due to a 6-year construction plan on the dam where riders cross the lake. There will be a new route coming out that will most likely be mostly on the north side of the lake." (https://www.thegravelcyclistsguidetobritishcolumbia.ca/ride-list/campbell-river-river-city-fondo)

**Access Considerations [UNKNOWN]:** Course uses Mosaic Forest Management land which is "gated; check their website for access open/closed status." (https://www.thegravelcyclistsguidetobritishcolumbia.ca/ride-list/campbell-river-river-city-fondo)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Event Reception [UNKNOWN]:** The inaugural event in 2024 "was met with huge success and praise from participants as 178 gravel cycling enthusiasts from all over BC tackled gravel routes in the pristine area West of Campbell River." (https://www.panachecyclingsports.com/post/river-city-gravel-fondo-a-huge-success)

**Event Philosophy [UNKNOWN]:** The series "invites those who are simply looking for a challenge and to share their experiences with colleagues, friends, and family." The marketing emphasizes: "Embarking on a TREK BC Gravel Series event is no small feat; it's a test of endurance, strength, and mental fortitude. The rugged terrain and unpredictable conditions push you to your limits, demanding every ounce of determination and resilience. Yet, amidst the challenge lies an unparalleled reward: the breathtaking beauty of nature unfolding around you." (https://cyclingbc.net/event/river-city-gravel-fondo-2025/)

**Participant Demographics [ELITE/UNKNOWN]:** Strong age-group participation across multiple master's categories (35-44, 45-54, 55-64, 65+) and youth divisions, with competitive fields in all three distances. The 2025 event had 67 riders in the Esperto, 111 in the Intermedio, and 38 in the Impulso. (https://www.webscorer.com/race?raceid=386987)
