## Weather Experienced

**2025 Edition:** Described as "dry and dusty environment" during the race, with heat being a significant challenge factor. (https://www.archyde.com/safari-gravel-race-hailey-hunter-smith-wins-solo/)

**2024 Edition:** Course conditions included "brutally sandy" terrain in the first half that "cost riders precious energy early in the race" according to photo documentation. (https://velo.outsideonline.com/gravel/gravel-racing/safari-gravel-shines-a-spotlight-on-kenyas-growing-race-scene)

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Surface composition:** 85% gravel with surfaces varying from red clay, single track, and game trails. The 111km course includes initial paved roads for the first 8km before encountering gravel sections. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/naivasha/)

**Elevation profile:** Highest point reached at 43km at 2,214m altitude, followed by descent to a short mid-course paved section. Lowest point reached after 80km at 1,675m elevation. Total climbing: 2,500 meters. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/naivasha/ and https://cyclingmagazine.ca/gravel/giraffes-lions-and-zebras-oh-my-hailey-hunter-smith-wins-safari-gravel-race/)

**Course-specific challenges [ELITE perspective]:** "The first half of the 100 kilometer course was brutally sandy, costing riders precious energy early in the race." The race also featured "sharp black lava rock under tire." (https://velo.outsideonline.com/gravel/gravel-racing/safari-gravel-shines-a-spotlight-on-kenyas-growing-race-scene and https://cyclingmagazine.ca/gravel/giraffes-lions-and-zebras-oh-my-hailey-hunter-smith-wins-safari-gravel-race/)

**Wildlife encounters:** "Plains animal spotting is all but guaranteed" according to race organizers. Photo documentation shows "A herd — rather, a dazzle — of zebras thundered across the course early in the day." Course passes through terrain with giraffes, lions, zebras, eagles, antelopes, and baboons. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/naivasha/ and https://velo.outsideonline.com/gravel/gravel-racing/safari-gravel-shines-a-spotlight-on-kenyas-growing-race-scene)

**Unique features:** Course passes by "Pride Rock" (the inspiration for Disney's The Lion King) and through a large geothermal powerplant area "whose plumes could be seen throughout the second half of the race." (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/naivasha/ and https://velo.outsideonline.com/gravel/gravel-racing/safari-gravel-shines-a-spotlight-on-kenyas-growing-race-scene)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Elite pacing [ELITE perspective - Hans Becking, 2024 winner]:** "Hans Becking (NED) ultimately created a 4-minute gap over Lukas Baum (GER) with a strong second half" — suggesting the race is won in the second half after the sandy opening sections. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/safari-gravel-race-for-maddy-nutt-and-hans-becking/)

**Elite women's pacing [ELITE perspective - Maddy Nutt, 2024 winner]:** Maddy Nutt finished "2 minutes ahead of Xaveline Nirere (RWA)" with third place "11 minutes behind the leader" — indicating strong separation in elite women's field. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/safari-gravel-race-for-maddy-nutt-and-hans-becking/)

**Tactical observation [ELITE perspective, 2025]:** "Solo sorties like Smith's becoming increasingly common and strategic sprint finishes like Malezsewski's, highlighting the importance of a well-rounded skillset." (https://www.archyde.com/safari-gravel-race-hailey-hunter-smith-wins-solo/)

## DNF Risk Factors

**Mechanicals - flat tires [UNKNOWN level rider]:** One YouTube video titled "UCI Safari Gravel Race 2025 (Full Ride Uncut)" mentions "Had 2x flat front tire, even though I rode Thunder Burt..." in the description, suggesting sharp lava rock and thorny terrain present puncture risks. (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=i4tso9L5YCE)

**Physical demands:** The combination of altitude (racing between 1,675m and 2,214m), heat, sandy terrain in the first half, and "sharp black lava rock" creates challenging conditions emphasizing both physical and mental endurance. (https://cyclingmagazine.ca/gravel/giraffes-lions-and-zebras-oh-my-hailey-hunter-smith-wins-safari-gravel-race/)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Post-race atmosphere:** "Incredible vibes at the finisher's party at Carnellies" is repeatedly mentioned. The event includes a "LOOP village" for family-friendly activities, vendors, and food. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/naivasha/ and https://safarikenyaexplorer.com/hells-gate-bike-races/)

**Local participation:** "Many riders from Kenya said that Safari Gravel was their first bike race" and the 2024 edition saw "all 19 members of the East African Team Amani and Black Mamba racing teams compete in the elite race." In the 60km race, "Kenyan men dominated the top four spots." (https://velo.outsideonline.com/gravel/gravel-racing/safari-gravel-shines-a-spotlight-on-kenyas-growing-race-scene and https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/safari-gravel-race-for-maddy-nutt-and-hans-becking/)

**International/local dynamic [ELITE perspective]:** "The event drew a tight community of locals, expats and international pros" creating a unique cultural exchange atmosphere. (https://cyclingmagazine.ca/gravel/giraffes-lions-and-zebras-oh-my-hailey-hunter-smith-wins-safari-gravel-race/)

**Scale:** The 2024/2025 editions "attracted over 500 cyclists" and nearly 500 riders from around the world. (https://safarikenyaexplorer.com/hells-gate-bike-races/ and https://velo.outsideonline.com/gravel/gravel-racing/safari-gravel-shines-a-spotlight-on-kenyas-growing-race-scene)

**Aid stations:** "Riders who stopped for water were treated to perfectly ripe watermelon, bananas, and pineapple" according to photo documentation from 2024. (https://velo.outsideonline.com/gravel/gravel-racing/safari-gravel-shines-a-spotlight-on-kenyas-growing-race-scene)

**Unique character:** Race staged at "Kingfisher Flower Farm, which produces cut flowers for bouquets and vegetables for export. Farmworkers enjoyed the spectacle on the day." Riders passed through "kale fields" and "greenhouses during the early kilometers." (https://velo.outsideonline.com/gravel/gravel-racing/safari-gravel-shines-a-spotlight-on-kenyas-growing-race-scene)

**Organizational mission:** "Team AMANI, which organizes the race alongside Migration Gravel Race, focuses on equity and opportunity in East African cycling" — giving the event a social mission beyond competition. (https://cyclingmagazine.ca/gravel/giraffes-lions-and-zebras-oh-my-hailey-hunter-smith-wins-safari-gravel-race/)
