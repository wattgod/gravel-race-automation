## Overview & History

The **Sagan Fondo** is a gran fondo cycling event in Truckee, California, founded by **three-time world road racing champion Peter Sagan** in 2018[4]. The event was originally established as part of Sagan's two California gran fondo events announced in partnership with event producer Bike Monkey[1][5].

**Founder:** **Peter Sagan**, accomplished three-time world road racing champion[1][4].
