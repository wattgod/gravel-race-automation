The Spirit World 100 has been scheduled for two different dates across years:
- **2025 Event:** October 23-26, 2025[1][2]

## COMMUNITY & REPUTATION

**Race History**

The search results indicate the race is described as "a catalyst for an increase in growth" in Patagonia[3], suggesting it plays a community development role. However, the founding year and founder information are not provided in the search results.

**Race Reputation in Gravel Community**

The Spirit World 100 is characterized by strong community vibes and a grassroots atmosphere. One attendee quote states: "The Spirit World is in a class by itself. It is a gathering of people who love bikes and people. Every single person at the event was absolutely, simply goodness!"[2]

Another rider noted: "Total adult summer camp vibe, but way better! Traveled to the event solo but never felt alone."[2]

A third testimonial emphasized: "Felt like a small community of friends who happen to ride 100 miles of gravel. The vibe was amazing!"[2]

One experienced gravel racer compared it to grassroots mountain bike racing: "It reminds me of local grassroots MTB races when I was a child, but with a completely different race experience from anything I've ever done. It is simply the coolest event."[2]
