# SWAMP FOX GRAVEL FONDO — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Race Overview & Key Details

**Location:** Francis Marion National Forest, Huger, SC (near Charleston)
**Distance Options:** 27, 50, 100 miles, plus 178-mile "Moultrie Militia Gravel Raid" overnight option
**Terrain Type:** Gravel forest roads, described as "moderate terrain with rolling short steep hills or moderate, sustained climbs"
**Support Level:** Minimally supported - 1 aid station at mile 25 for 50-mile, 2 aid stations (miles 25 and 72) for 100-mile
**Cell Coverage:** Remote forest with unreliable cell phone reception
**Named After:** Revolutionary War hero Francis Marion ("The Swamp Fox"), depicted in the movie "The Patriot"

## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course Conditions from 2023 [UNKNOWN]:** "Weather conditions were proper $h!te with cold temperatures and demoralizing rain. I was able to hold the front group until a crash in a mud pit took me out." (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dvQby-ppJ00)

**Course Distance Variance [UNKNOWN]:** One rider noted the 2021 50-mile race "turned out to be 53" miles (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WtYAjRcKUIQ)

**Tire Recommendations:** Event organizers recommend "A minimum of 28c wide tires with tread is recommended" and note "Cyclocross, Gravel, Mountain and Road bikes will be allowed and can be used. The best choice would be a gravel bike." (https://www.mtpleasantvelo.org/swampfoxgravelfondo)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**2025 Winning Times [ELITE]:**
- 27 miles: Rich Redding - 1:36:30 (16.8 mph average)
- 50 miles: Shaune Huysamen - 2:32:42 (19.7 mph average)
- 100 miles: Max Abner - 4:38:36 (21.6 mph average)

**Cutoff Times (from organizer):**
- MMGR (178 miles): 16 hours to complete
- 100 miles: Must reach mile 70 aid station within 5 hours, complete course within 8 hours. "If you do not reach the aid station within 5 hours, you will be unsupported if you choose to continue the route."
- 50 miles: Must complete within 5 hours

**Pacing Spread [ELITE results from 2025]:** On the 27-mile course, the winning time was 1:36:30 while the last finisher took 2:58:12 - nearly double the winner's time, indicating significant variability in rider abilities.

## DNF Risk Factors

**Remote Location:** Event organizers explicitly warn: "The forest is remote and cell phone reception is not guaranteed. Please plan accordingly." (https://www.mtpleasantvelo.org/swampfoxgravelfondo)

**Self-Sufficiency Required:** "Riders should be prepared to fix anything that may come their way (flats, mechanicals, etc)... There is NO SAG wagon to pick you up. If you leave the course, we may not be able to find you." (https://www.mtpleasantvelo.org/swampfoxgravelfondo)

**Weather Challenges:** 2023 race featured "cold temperatures and demoralizing rain" with mud pits causing crashes (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dvQby-ppJ00)

**DNF Rate (2024 data):** 
- 100 miles: 4 DNFs out of 51 total starters (7.8% DNF rate) including riders in Cat 2, 3, 4, and 5 categories (https://www.gravel-results.com/race/18314)
- 100 miles: 4 additional DNFs in 2025 out of 39 starters (https://www.gravel-results.com/race/20364)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Post-Race Scene:** "10:30 AM - Empanada Cuisines and Roe Roe's Filipino Food trucks will be on site ready to serve post-ride food! All riders are encouraged to hangout in the finish area and share their stories of #gravelfreedom" (https://www.mtpleasantvelo.org/swampfoxgravelfondo)

**Multi-Day Event:** The race includes a Friday course preview ride, early packet pickup at Commonhouse Aleworks brewery, the overnight MMGR raid Friday night, main event Saturday, and a "Dirt Church" post-event ride Sunday sponsored by Trek Bicycle Store. (https://www.mtpleasantvelo.org/swampfoxgravelfondo)

**Environmental Responsibility:** Organizers emphasize: "Absolutely no littering on the course. Littering on course will result in disqualification. It is vital we leave the forest as we found it." (https://www.mtpleasantvelo.org/swampfoxgravelfondo)

**Historical Context:** Event emphasizes the Revolutionary War history: "The forest is named for the revolutionary war hero Francis Marion, who was known to the British as the Swamp Fox. He used these swamps, creeks and forest areas to ambush and hide from the British" (https://www.mtpleasantvelo.org/swampfoxgravelfondo)

## Logistics & Planning

**Navigation:** "We no longer mark our courses. Riders should download the course GPX file to their navigation device." (https://www.mtpleasantvelo.org/swampfoxgravelfondo)

**Parking:** "Park only in designated areas (shown) and avoid low-lying areas as you will get your vehicle stuck. This is private (borrowed) property. Please respect the owner's land." (https://www.mtpleasantvelo.org/swampfoxgravelfondo)

**Registration:** Caps at approximately 300 participants; no day-of registration available. (https://www.mtpleasantvelo.org/swampfoxgravelfondo)

**Wave Starts (2025):**
- 8:00 AM - Mens/Womens 100-mile Open
- 8:10 AM - Womens/Mens 40+ and 50+ 100-mile
- 9:00 AM - Mens/Womens 50-mile Open
- 9:10 AM - Womens/Mens 40+ and 50+ 50-mile
- 9:30 AM - 27-mile route

**What's Included:** Custom 100% cotton event T-shirt (must register by ~2 weeks before event to guarantee shirt), post-race food, finisher awards for all finishers, podium awards for top 3 in each category (50 and 100 mile only).

**Traffic Concerns:** "There will be several highway and road crossings with heavy car and truck traffic. There may be an area of the course where the racers will share the road with car and truck traffic. Please keep to the right and ride single file except where passing... Traffic in the National Forest includes horses. Horseback riders have the right of way on trails." (https://www.mtpleasantvelo.org/swampfoxgravelfondo)

**No Refund Policy:** "Mount Pleasant Velo Events, LLC does not provide refunds for event entry fees... Event credits, valid toward other Mount Pleasant Velo events, may be issued up to two (2) weeks prior to the registered event in cases of unforeseen circumstances where event insurance is not available." (https://www.mtpleasantvelo.org/swampfoxgravelfondo)
