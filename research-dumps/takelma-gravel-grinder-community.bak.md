# TAKELMA GRAVEL GRINDER — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Rider Quotes & Race Reports

I have been on long rides before, but never have I done such intense consecutive climbing. *on gravel at that* What a great experience though and will be looking for another Gravel Grinder, as it was a blast getting to surge down the mountain road that had just taken everything out of you to get up! ## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

The race starts literally inside Seven Feathers Casino Resort - riders roll through the casino doors before beginning the course. (https://www.takelmagravelgrinder.com/)

The course begins with approximately 13 miles of flat pavement before the gravel and climbing begins. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/7641/registration-opens-for-one-the-craziest-grinders-out-west-the-takelma-gravel-grinder)

The main KOM section runs from mile 15 to mile 21 - a 6-mile climb gaining 2,200 vertical feet. This section features a 1.3-mile climbing effort averaging 9% grade, with gradients reaching up to 15%. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/7641/registration-opens-for-one-the-craziest-grinders-out-west-the-takelma-gravel-grinder)

After cresting the main climb, riders are rewarded with a "spectacular valley view of Days Creek." (https://traveloregon.com/things-to-do/events/takelma-gravel-grinder-3/)

The course passes farm and ranch landscapes along the South Umpqua River before transitioning to gravel. (https://www.takelmagravelgrinder.com/)

Riders continue toward Chief Miwaleta Park with scenic views of Galesville Reservoir. (https://traveloregon.com/things-to-do/events/takelma-gravel-grinder-3/)

The timed portion is 43 miles of the total 56-57 mile course. The final 13 miles back to the casino are untimed. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/7641/registration-opens-for-one-the-craziest-grinders-out-west-the-takelma-gravel-grinder)

The course is described as 55% gravel overall. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/7641/registration-opens-for-one-the-craziest-grinders-out-west-the-takelma-gravel-grinder)

One YouTube rider noted "rapid descents" and an "eyeball-shaking descent on a rough road" is mentioned for related routes in the area. (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dt9ycvdxONU)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

The race is limited to 250 riders (some sources say 200), creating a more intimate event. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/7641/registration-opens-for-one-the-craziest-grinders-out-west-the-takelma-gravel-grinder)

Pre-race coffee from Takelma Roasting (a Native American-owned specialty coffee roaster) is served starting at 6:00 AM before the 8:30 AM start. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/7641/registration-opens-for-one-the-craziest-grinders-out-west-the-takelma-gravel-grinder)

Sasquatch mascot sees riders off at the start. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/7641/registration-opens-for-one-the-craziest-grinders-out-west-the-takelma-gravel-grinder)

Post-race celebration includes the "Squatchee Fest" (also called "Bites, Blues & Brews Festival") at the casino with food from the Seven Feathers Food Truck provided complimentary with meal vouchers. Wine Fest has also been mentioned. (https://www.takelmagravelgrinder.com/)

Awards include "Coffee Grinder Trophies for Top KOM & Finishers." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/7641/registration-opens-for-one-the-craziest-grinders-out-west-the-takelma-gravel-grinder)

All participants receive a custom "Takelma Tough" T-shirt. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/7641/registration-opens-for-one-the-craziest-grinders-out-west-the-takelma-gravel-grinder)

The event is part of the Oregon Triple Crown Series, organized by Mudslinger Events. Completing three Oregon Triple Crown events earns riders a custom Pactimo jersey. (https://strambecco.com/event/takelma-gravel-grinder/)

Age categories range from 15 to 75+ for both men and women, plus a tandem category. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/7641/registration-opens-for-one-the-craziest-grinders-out-west-the-takelma-gravel-grinder)

Aid stations with electrolyte drinks and food are provided on course, along with bike support and SAG vehicles. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/7641/registration-opens-for-one-the-craziest-grinders-out-west-the-takelma-gravel-grinder)

The casino venue offers unique post-race amenities including a spa, indoor pool, fitness center, and full accommodations for those who want to make it a weekend. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/Index/7641/registration-opens-for-one-the-craziest-grinders-out-west-the-takelma-gravel-grinder)

Group rides in the area (like the Honey Badger Rides) are described as having "a fun community vibe" and regularly draw 30+ cyclists, suggesting the regional gravel community is active and welcoming. (https://www.southernoregon.org/cycling-biking/gravel-adventure-biking/ultimate-guide-to-gravel-rides-in-southern-oregon/)
