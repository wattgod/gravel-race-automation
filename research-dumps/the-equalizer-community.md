## Course & Terrain Details (From Event Listings)

**Location:** Marthasville, MO (Warren County)

**Distance Options:**
- 50K (actually 43 miles, described as "XL")
- 100K

**Elevation Gain:**
- 50K: 1,800' (one source) / 2,700' (another source - discrepancy in listings)
- 100K: 3,786' / 4,131' (discrepancy in listings)

**Terrain Description (from iRace.ai):**
"The course takes you through roads that look untouched by time, into the beautiful and beastiful rolling hills of Warren county highland farm country. Gravel consistency ranges from fast hard packed crushed limestone, to loose gravel, to big brown Charette Creek river valley rock, to buttery smooth asphalt 3 creek crossings and dirt for the 100k." (https://irace.ai/events/the-equalizer-gravel-race)

**Course Characteristics (from BikeSignup):**
- "Fast enough for the pavement pounder, yet technical enough for the gravel grinders"
- Mix of gravel and paved sections
- 3 creek crossings (100K only)
- Both distances share the course for first 5-12 miles (sources vary)
- 50K joins 100K "through the full classic Equalizer loop through Warrenton"
- "The 100K sees all NEW gravel this year (and a beastly KOM climb the 100k get to see twice)"
- Course marked with "orange arrows on the road roadside directional stakes" plus corner marshals

(https://www.bikesignup.com/Race/MO/Marthasville/Equalizer)

## Race Logistics & Amenities

**Start Time:** 7:30 AM (with wave starts for different categories)

**Aid Stations:**

- Sag bags accepted - will be at aid station

**Camping & Facilities:**
- Free onsite camping Friday and Saturday nights at Wessel Park and KT Caboose

**Post-Race:**

- KT Caboose onsite with "discounted food and drink specials" including hamburgers, veggie burgers, chips, beers

(https://www.bikesignup.com/Race/MO/Marthasville/Equalizer)

## Race Categories & Championships

The event serves as:
- Regular 50K/100K open race
- Finale event for "SHO ME MO' GRVL SERIES"
- **Missouri State Championship** for gravel cycling

**State Championship Categories (100K only):**
- Men's 4,5
- Women's 1,2,3
- Women's 4-5
- Masters 40+
- Masters 50+
- Masters 60+

**State Championship Requirements:**
- Must have current USAC ANNUAL license
- Must be Missouri resident
- Must race 100K distance
- USAC points NOT required to register for specific category

**Licensing:**
- One-day USAC license included in registration for non-licensed riders
- Annual USAC license holders must have it for State Championship eligibility

**Rules:**
- Aerobars NOT allowed
- Centerline rule in effect
- State Championship contenders cannot work/ride with other categories/genders
- 15-minute protest period after results posted

(https://www.bikesignup.com/Race/MO/Marthasville/Equalizer)
