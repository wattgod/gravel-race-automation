## TERRAIN & COURSE

**Surface Breakdown**

The course is **over 80% gravel**, with the remainder on other surfaces[2][3][5]. Described as "firm gravel" throughout[2][5]. The route is "almost entirely off road"[2].

**Course Features & Location**

The **111km UCI race route** winds through the **Galloway Forest Park**, showcasing "its lochs, forests and rugged hillsides"[3]. The course takes place within the **Galloway and Southern Ayrshire UNESCO Biosphere**[2][3][5]. The race starts and finishes in **Garries Park** in **Gatehouse of Fleet**[3].

**Key Course Details**

Within the opening kilometre, riders are on gravel and climbing the day's first **11km ascent**[5]. Cycling Weekly described the route as **"relentless"**[5]. The terrain suits both "climbers and rouleurs alike"[5].

**Technical Difficulty**

Described as a "tough one-day gravel cycling race" with "thrilling, action packed racing"[5]. The combination of 1,761m of climbing with 80%+ gravel makes it technically and physically demanding[3].

## COMMUNITY & REPUTATION

**Race History**

The Gralloch returns for its **fourth edition in May 2026**[2]. It is organized by **RED:ON Events**, the experienced team behind the British Gravel Championships (King's Cup) and Raiders Gravel[5]. The event was founded by **RED:ON Sports**, with **Maximilian Wussler** listed as Co-Founder[1].

**Significance in Gravel Racing**

The Gralloch hosts **the first UCI Gravel World Series event ever held in Britain**[2]. It is described as **Britain's leading gravel cycling event**[1] and the **UK's first top level gravel race**[2]. The race qualifies riders for the **UCI Gravel World Championships in September 2026**[2][4].

**Reputation & Perception**

The Gralloch is **"often considered as one of the most beautiful courses in the series by the participants"**[5]. It is positioned as **"a true gravel classic in the making"**[5]. The event is described as a **"must-attend event for elite riders, amateurs, and cycling fans alike"**[1].

**Notable Participants & Winners**

The search results reference that "the world's best gravel racers" compete in the Elite category[4], but no specific names of past winners or notable riders are provided.

**2026 Festival Atmosphere**

The weekend includes **live music, local food and drink, and a vibrant expo village**[1]. The event features **shakeout rides, food, drink, camping, kids activities**[4]. It combines **"competition and community"**[1].
