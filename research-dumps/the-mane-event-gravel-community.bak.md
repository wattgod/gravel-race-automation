# THE MANE EVENT GRAVEL — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Event Overview & Mission
The Mane Event Gravel Grinder is held annually in Sheridan, Wyoming, typically in mid-September. The event serves as a fundraiser for local nonprofits, particularly the Sheridan Sundowner Lions Club, which conducts vision screenings, equips hospitals and clinics, distributes medicine, and raises awareness of eye disease through their mission of "providing vision for all" and their international SightFirst Program to eradicate blindness. (https://sheridanwyoming.com/2024/09/17/the-mane-event-gravel-grinder-racing-to-raise-funds-for-sight/)

As of 2025, the event is organized by Wild Path Racing, LLC, which took over operations to help grow the event after the Lions Club needed additional support. (https://sheridanwyoming.com/2025/09/03/grinding-gravel-and-giving-sight/)

## Course Options & Terrain Details

**Course Distances (2025-2026):**
- 25 Mile: Out-and-back route along ranch land with a climb on the way out and downhill return
  - Paved: 5.9 mi (23%) / Unpaved: 19.6 mi (77%)
  - Total Ascent: 953 feet
  - Aid station at halfway point (12.5 mile marker)
  - Start Time: 9:30 AM

- 32 Mile: Added in 2025, includes the "triple crown" climb featured in longer routes
  - Described as "a small mileage jump from our smallest route but it runs over our 'triple crown' climb just like the 54 and 72 mile routes. A truly challenging and beautiful route."
  
- 54 Mile: Loop with several shorter climbs and "a long draw of a climb that will challenge you right in the middle of the route"
  - Paved: 8 mi (15%) / Gravel: 46 mi (85%)
  - Total Ascent: 3,188 feet
  - "The long downhill in the last ~15 miles will surely have you smiling as you roll into the finish"
  - Aid stations about every 10 miles
  - Start Time: 8:30 AM

- 78 Mile: Extended loop through rolling hills east of Sheridan with some highway sections for recovery
  - Paved: 15.9 mi (20%) / Gravel: 62.4 mi (80%)
  - Total Ascent: 4,105 feet
  - "Takes east of Sheridan through the rolling hills of well-maintained county roads with a bit of highway asphalt to recover and get your average speed up"
  - Hooks into the 54-mile route
  - Aid stations about every 10 miles
  - Start Time: 7:30 AM

(https://www.sheridanwyoming.org/festivals-events/mane-event-gravel-grinder/)

**General Course Description [UNKNOWN]:**
Routes wind "through rolling ranch land and sweeping foothills north and east of Sheridan" on "mostly gravel roads" with "beautiful views." The terrain is described as "moderate terrain with rolling short steep hills or moderate, sustained climbs." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10592/the-mane-event-gravel-grinder)

## Course Support & Logistics

The event is described as "fully-supported" with:
- Aid stations every 12-15 miles with water, electrolytes, first aid, and snacks
- Sweeper vehicle to ensure riders are cared for
- Well-marked routes
- Flaggers at key points
- Restrooms at aid stations

(https://sheridanmedia.com/news/205839/the-mane-event-gravel-grinder-on-public-pulse-sept-2/)

**Start/Finish Location:**
Originally started and finished at Wyarno Bar & Grill, but moved to Koltiska Pumpkin Patch (120 Cat Creek Road, Sheridan, WY) for 2025 with a "new start/finish line setup and additional activities for families." (https://avidcyclist.com/event/the-mane-event-gravel-grinder/)

**Check-In:**
All riders must check in on Friday evening (4:00-8:00 PM) at the designated location to receive race packets and final instructions. This is also the last opportunity for late registration. (https://sheridanwyoming.com/2024/09/17/the-mane-event-gravel-grinder-racing-to-raise-funds-for-sight/)
