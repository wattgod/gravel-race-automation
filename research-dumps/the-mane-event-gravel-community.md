## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Jim Rapp [UNKNOWN]:** "The routes of the competition will take riders onto scenic roadways and rolling hills." (https://sheridanmedia.com/news/146679/lions-club-presents-the-mane-event-gravel-grinder/)

The course features "mostly gravel roads that wind through rolling ranch land and sweeping foothills north and east of Sheridan." Terrain is described as "moderate terrain with rolling short steep hills or moderate, sustained climbs." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10592/the-mane-event-gravel-grinder)

**Course marking and support:** Routes are "well-marked" with aid stations every 12-15 miles. The event is described as "fully-supported" with sweeper vehicle coverage. (https://sheridanmedia.com/news/205839/the-mane-event-gravel-grinder-on-public-pulse-sept-2/)

**Location specifics:** Starting and finishing at Koltiska Pumpkin Patch (mentioned for 2026) or Wyarno Bar & Grill (mentioned in earlier years). The event showcases "the untamed beauty of Wyoming" in the Sheridan area. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10592/the-mane-event-gravel-grinder)

## Event Context & Purpose

The Mane Event is the primary fundraiser for the Sheridan Sundowner Lions Club, supporting vision-related causes. **Bill Rathburn [UNKNOWN]:** The Sundowner Lions Club "conducts vision screenings, equipping hospitals and clinics, distributing medicine and raising awareness of eye disease." The club works toward "providing vision for all" and supports the international SightFirst Program to eradicate blindness. (https://sheridanmedia.com/news/146679/lions-club-presents-the-mane-event-gravel-grinder/)

The event includes a raffle in conjunction with the ride, with tickets sold at downtown Sheridan events. (https://sheridanmedia.com/news/146679/lions-club-presents-the-mane-event-gravel-grinder/)

## Logistics & Registration

**Distance options:** 25, 32, 54, or 78 miles (note: some years listed 25, 50, 80 mile options)

**2025 Registration fees:**
- 25 Mile: $70
- 32 Mile: $80
- 54 Mile: $90
- 78 Mile: $110

**Included:** SWAG bag, lunch meal, t-shirt, post-event meal, finishing medals, aid station nutrition. Number of aid stations: 6. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10592/the-mane-event-gravel-grinder)

**Timing:** Held annually in mid-to-late September. Recent dates: Sept 20, 2025 and Sept 19, 2026.
