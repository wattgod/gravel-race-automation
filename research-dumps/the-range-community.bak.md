## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course Features:**
- Route travels through "open grasslands, dense forest of the Porcupine hills, sweeping views of the Rocky Mountains, 2-track ranch roads, and the incredible Skyline road (one of the best kept secrets in Alberta)" (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10123/the-range)

- **Two natural creek crossings** on private road through Burton's Burke Creek Ranch (established 1890) (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10123/the-range)

- Single truss bridges and historic ranches as markers in wilderness areas (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10123/the-range)

- Exclusive access to both public and private roads, "allowing participants to traverse areas typically off-limits to the public" (https://strambecco.com/event/the-range-gravel-experience/)

- "Varied terrain, steep hills (The Wall....)" mentioned as notable features (https://www.transrockies.com/news/8inp3s2o3yjmcpvvyscid4691f0f68-fegxd-7yxsj-9lzc9-clnyd-rr23z)

**Route Distances and Elevation:**
- **The Frontier:** 211 km with approximately 3,000 meters of elevation gain (https://strambecco.com/event/the-range-gravel-experience/)
- **The Range (Classic Route):** 122 km with 1,606 meters of elevation gain (https://strambecco.com/event/the-range-gravel-experience/)
- **The Rustler:** 85 km (https://strambecco.com/event/the-range-gravel-experience/)
- **The Rodeo:** 60 km (https://strambecco.com/event/the-range-gravel-experience/)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Start/Finish Location:** All routes start and finish in Centennial Park and Campground in Claresholm, Alberta (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10123/the-range)

**Event Weekend Schedule (from historical data):**
- Friday 3:00-9:00 PM: Rider arrival and camping move-in
- Friday 6:00-8:00 PM: Rider sign-in/packet pick-up
- Saturday morning: Staggered starts for different distance categories
- Saturday 11:00 AM - 7:00 PM: Beer Garden open at "ChillVille" (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10123/the-range)

**Community Support:** Event described as "a celebration of Alberta's unique and natural ecosystems of SW Alberta where sustainable ranching practices and conservation have preserved this often-overlooked natural gem" (https://www.transrockies.com/news/8inp3s2o3yjmcpvvyscid4691f0f68-fegxd-7yxsj-9lzc9-clnyd-rr23z)

**Participant Capacity:** Limited to 500 riders (https://www.therangegravel.com/)

**Camping:** "Camping is available at Centennial Park Campground and is reserved for riders until May 31, 2026. Riders must contact the Campground directly to arrange camping." (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10123/the-range)

**What's Included:**
- Event T-shirt (guaranteed if registered before June 1, 2026)
- Custom bike plate with rider's name
- Food & beverage wristband
- $100 USD discount code for Rough Rider event
- Aid stations with hydration and nutrition
- Bike support along course
- Sweepers and full course support
- Finish line lunch (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10123/the-range)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Course Timing:** The Range is chip-timed with overall rankings for all riders, plus a "Sector Challenge" option for focused efforts on specific segments (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10123/the-range)

**Aid Stations:** 3 aid stations along the course (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/10123/the-range)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere (Additional Context)

**Series Affiliation:** The Range joined the TransRockies Race Series in December 2024 and is now part of the prestigious Gravel Earth Series, "bringing this epic race to an international platform. This affiliation allows riders to earn points toward the series' overall ranking, positioning The Range among the world's premier gravel events." (https://strambecco.com/event/the-range-gravel-experience/)

**Event History:** "Founded in 2020 by Garth Stotts—right in the middle of the pandemic—The Range quickly pedaled its way into the hearts of cyclists across the province. The route became an instant gravel cycling classic renowned as a beautiful adventure over an unrelenting demanding course." (https://www.transrockies.com/news/8inp3s2o3yjmcpvvyscid4691f0f68-fegxd-7yxsj-9lzc9-clnyd-rr23z)
