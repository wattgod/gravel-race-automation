## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course Description:** Event website describes it as a "Ride what you bring, mixed surface grinder" incorporating "a combination of gravel/fire road and 'noodle-bar friendly' single track" (https://www.cyclingva.com/events/unravel-gravel/)

**Location:** Bear Creek Lake State Park in Cumberland, VA (https://www.cyclingva.com/events/unravel-gravel/)

**Course Profile:** The 100K is a 2-lap format based on timing data showing "After 1" and "Finish time" columns (https://www.webscorer.com/results?raceid=370345&did=482698&gender=M)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Split Analysis [ELITE Level]:**
- Top elite men (Evans, Gauthier, Roy) ran first lap splits around 1:17-1:20 for the 100K, finishing around 2:36-2:37 total
- Elite woman Andrea Buttine [ELITE] split 1:26:34 / 2:55:02 total
- This suggests roughly even pacing or slight positive splits for top finishers

**Split Analysis [COMPETITIVE/RECREATIONAL Levels]:**
- Mid-pack finishers showed more variable pacing
- Liam Winn [COMPETITIVE]: 1:26:35 first lap, 3:06:18 total (1:39:43 second lap - significant fade)
- Mark North [COMPETITIVE]: 1:33:25 first lap, 3:15:34 total (1:42:09 second lap - significant fade)
- Pattern shows recreational/competitive riders experiencing 10-15+ minute fade on second lap

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Organizational Context:** RVA Racing Events describes themselves as "Bike racing built by bike racers!" and runs a full calendar of grass-roots MTB racing events in Virginia (https://rvaracingevents.com)

**Field Size:** Registration limited to 200 riders. 2024 had 75 starters in 100K, 22 in 50K (https://www.cyclingva.com/events/unravel-gravel/)

**Competitive Depth:** The event attracts regional elite talent, with teams like Miller School of Albemarle, DistrictTaco Racing, Ecoflo Chronos, and multiple Virginia cycling clubs represented (https://www.webscorer.com/results?raceid=370345&did=482698&gender=M)

**USAC Sanctioning:** Official USAC Virginia State Gravel Championship, offering points and state championship titles across multiple categories (https://www.cyclingva.com/events/unravel-gravel/)

**Category Diversity:** Race offers 15+ different categories including Elite Men/Women Open, age-grouped categories (18-34, 35-49, 50-59, 60+), Juniors, Singlespeed, Tandem, and separate Mini categories for 50K (https://www.webscorer.com/results?raceid=370345&did=482698&gender=M)
