# VUELTA ALTAS CUMBRES — COMMUNITY RESEARCH

## Event Structure & Format

**Multi-Sport Experience:** The Vuelta Altas Cumbres is part of the larger "Experiencia Altas Cumbres" multi-sport weekend event. Saturday features "Run Altas Cumbres" trail running races (5k, 10k, 21k, 42k, 60k), while Sunday is the cycling event. Some athletes compete in both days — **Magdalena "Maguie" Nieto [UNKNOWN]** ran 60km on Saturday and cycled 120km on Sunday in 2025. (https://www.carlospazvivo.com/experiencia-altas-cumbres-fin-de-semana-epico-en-traslasierra-con-destacados-carlospacenses-en-el-podio/)

**Event Size:** The 2025 edition drew over 4,500 athletes from Argentina and internationally, including participants from Chile, Uruguay, Paraguay, Bolivia, Brazil, and Ireland. (https://prensa.cba.gov.ar/informacion-general/la-experiencia-altas-cumbres-paso-a-pura-adrenalina-y-emocion-en-traslasierra/)

## Course Details (Rider Perspective)

**120K Route Profile:** 112km actual distance with 1,930m total elevation gain. Maximum altitude 2,270m, starting from 900m above sea level. The route is 92% dirt roads in varying states of repair. Approximately 35km of climbing until km 68, where the gradient turns negative. The race finishes in Cura Brochero after a beautiful, continuous 33km descent. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Events/Index/13454/vuelta-altas-cumbres-gravel)

**Three Distance Options:** 120km, 82km, and 32km, all finishing in Villa Cura Brochero. Three separate start locations with all distances converging at the finish line. (https://vueltaaltascumbres.com.ar/)

**Course Towns:** The route passes through Villa Cura Brochero, Ambul, San Lorenzo, and Panaholma. (https://prensa.cba.gov.ar/informacion-general/la-experiencia-altas-cumbres-paso-a-pura-adrenalina-y-emocion-en-traslasierra/)

**Terrain Character:** Described by organizers as taking place "in a unique, ever-changing, and at times inhospitable natural setting, offering unparalleled beauty and demanding technical challenges." (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/villa-cura-brochero/)

## Weather Experienced

**2025 Sunday Race Conditions:** Temperatures approached 40°C (104°F) with intense north wind during the cycling event, making conditions "aún más exigente" (even more demanding). (https://www.carlospazvivo.com/experiencia-altas-cumbres-fin-de-semana-epico-en-traslasierra-con-destacados-carlospacenses-en-el-podio/)

**Year-Round Climate:** Villa Cura Brochero has a "unique microclimate" with "pleasant weather all year round" and "mild and inviting throughout the year." (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/villa-cura-brochero/)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Local Organization Pride:** Organizer Sandra Sholz noted, "Desde hace 14 años, la Agencia Córdoba Deportes nos acompaña y valora esta sinergia, permitiendo que los deportistas vengan a pasar un fin de semana maravilloso en la naturaleza de Traslasierra" (For 14 years, the Córdoba Sports Agency has supported us and values this synergy, allowing athletes to come spend a marvelous weekend in the nature of Traslasierra). (https://prensa.cba.gov.ar/informacion-general/la-experiencia-altas-cumbres-paso-a-pura-adrenalina-y-emocion-en-traslasierra/)

**Expo Component:** The event features an expo throughout the weekend where major sports brands exhibit products, creating "un punto de encuentro para deportistas, equipos y aficionados" (a meeting point for athletes, teams and fans). The Córdoba Sports Agency also runs a stand distributing gifts and accessories. (https://prensa.cba.gov.ar/informacion-general/la-experiencia-altas-cumbres-paso-a-pura-adrenalina-y-emocion-en-traslasierra/)

**Finish Line Emotion:** "Más allá de los resultados, cada llegada a la meta estuvo cargada de emoción. Detrás de cada sprint final, se escondían meses de preparación, sacrificio y determinación. Las sonrisas, los abrazos y las miradas de satisfacción reflejaban el verdadero espíritu" (Beyond the results, each arrival at the finish line was charged with emotion. Behind each final sprint, months of preparation, sacrifice and determination were hidden. The smiles, hugs and looks of satisfaction reflected the true spirit). (https://prensa.cba.gov.ar/informacion-general/la-experiencia-altas-cumbres-paso-a-pura-adrenalina-y-emocion-en-traslasierra/)

**Event Tagline:** "Lo único imposible es aquello que no intentas" (The only impossible thing is that which you don't try). (https://vueltaaltascumbres.com.ar/)

## International Significance

**UCI Gravel World Series Qualifier:** The 2026 edition (March 29, 2026) will be Argentina's first UCI Gravel World Series event and a direct qualifier for the 2026 UCI Gravel World Championships. Top 25% finishers in each age group qualify for the World Championships. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/villa-cura-brochero/)

**Historical Context:** The event has run for 15 editions as of 2026, establishing itself as "una de las pruebas más desafiantes y emblemáticas del trail y el ciclismo de montaña en Argentina" (one of the most challenging and emblematic trail and mountain bike races in Argentina). (https://prensa.cba.gov.ar/informacion-general/la-experiencia-altas-cumbres-paso-a-pura-adrenalina-y-emocion-en-traslasierra/)

## Location & Tourism

**Setting:** Villa Cura Brochero is located in the Traslasierra Valley, 160km from Córdoba city, surrounded by mountains and rivers that merge into the Los Sauces River. The area features the crystal-clear waters of the Panaholma and Mina Clavero rivers, popular swimming spots like Los Cedros Spa, and views of Achala Peak. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/villa-cura-brochero/)

**Religious Heritage:** The town is known for Saint José Gabriel Brochero, the "Gaucho Priest," with sites including the Sanctuary of Our Lady of the Assumption, the Brocheriano Museum, and the House of Spiritual Exercises. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/villa-cura-brochero/)

**Tourist Infrastructure:** The Traslasierra Valley is described as having "optimal tourist infrastructure for sporting events of this nature" with one of the most important tourism regions in Córdoba province. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/villa-cura-brochero/)

## Related Events

**Rally Altas Cumbres:** A separate two-day, four-stage MTB event held August 2-3, 2025, also organized by Deportistas de Aventura Asociación Civil in the same region. Participants can compete individually or in pairs across four different stages on Saturday and Sunday. (https://rac.vueltaaltascumbres.com.ar/)
