## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course Characteristics:**
- Features "long climbs, fast descents, great forest roads, rowdy gravel, and amazing vistas" through Cherohala Skyway, Cherokee and Nantahala National Forests (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)
- Key named climbs: Basin Gap and North River Road climbs (on both 63 and 88-mile routes), Farr Gap climb (88-mile route only) (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)
- Course described as "just as challenging as it is beautiful" (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)
- Routes traverse Cherokee National Forest and Bald River area (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)

**Elevation Profile:**
- 88-mile option: 11,500+ feet of climbing (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)
- 63-mile option: 8,800+ feet of climbing (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)
- 45-mile option: 4,100 feet of climbing (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)

**Navigation:**
- Course will NOT be marked; riders are responsible for downloading and using provided GPX files (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)
- GPX files available for all three distances via RideWithGPS (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Time Cutoffs [ALL RIDER LEVELS]:**
- Must reach 24-mile split by 1:00pm or forced to take 45-mile route (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)
- Must reach 45-mile split by 2:30pm or forced to take 63-mile route (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)
- All riders must finish by sunset (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)
- 88-mile riders must have lights at rest stop 4 if arriving after 6:00pm (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)

**Race Start [ALL RIDER LEVELS]:**
- Police-escorted neutral roll-out from Tellico Grains Bakery (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)
- Start time: 8:00am (some sources say 9:00am - discrepancy in sources) (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html, https://thewaucheesi2025.itsyourrace.com/)

**Aid Station Support:**
- Four fully stocked aid stops available (https://thewaucheesi2025.itsyourrace.com/)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Event Amenities:**
- Post-race meal featuring wood-fired pizzas from Tellico Grains Bakery with craft beverages (https://thewaucheesi2025.itsyourrace.com/)
- Finish line at Tellico Grains Bakery where riders can cheer on finishers (https://thewaucheesi2025.itsyourrace.com/)
- Optional event t-shirts available for $25 (described as "high quality, super-comfy triblend") (https://thewaucheesi2025.itsyourrace.com/)

**Packet Pickup:**
- Saturday evening 6-8pm OR race morning starting at 6am at Tellico Grains Bakery (https://thewaucheesi2025.itsyourrace.com/)
- Must be picked up in person (https://thewaucheesi2025.itsyourrace.com/)

**Competition Format:**
- Prizes awarded to top three finishers in each category for 63-mile and 88-mile distances (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)
- No prizes for 45-mile distance (described as a ride option rather than competitive distance) (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)

**Event Branding:**
- Event tagline: "Wicked, Wily & Wondrous" (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)
- Overall tone: "wickedly sweet" and challenging (https://rapidexpeditions.com/waucheesi-bike-race.html)
