## Terrain Details (Rider Perspective)

**Course Profile:**
- 130km with 2500m elevation gain (full distance)
- 80km with 1500m elevation gain (shorter option)
- Course composition: 40% woods, 40% gravel roads between fields, 20% paved roads
- Start/finish at 490m altitude, majority of course between 700-800m elevation
- Route includes passage through Larzac military camp with "superb wild tracks"
(https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/millau/)

**Key Sections:**
- **Opening climb - Pouncho d'Agast:** Steep 450m elevation gain in first 8km
- Forest tracks through Grands Causses region
- Unprecedented passage through Larzac military camp
- 2025 redesigned finish: dangerous sections removed, new spectacular finish above Viaduc de Millau
(https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/millau/ and https://wishonegravelrace.com/en/accueil-english/)

**Terrain Types:**
- Rustic roads, rolling tracks, single-track paths
- Forest paths
- Limestone plateaus
- Red stone areas
- Finish under the iconic Millau Viaduct (highest cable-stayed bridge in world at 336.4m)
(https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/millau/)

## Race Strategy & Pacing

**Elite Women's Strategy [ELITE]:**
Axelle Dubau Prevot established commanding lead from the first climb, maintaining relentless pace to finish 6 minutes ahead of second place in 2025. Strategy: go hard from start on opening climb. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/PopUp/8587/france-prevails-as-prevot-and-drechou-take-uci-wish-one-millau-gran-causses-titles)

**Elite Men's Racing [ELITE]:**
2025 race saw aggressive pace from start, field gradually narrowed to 5 riders by military camp section. Race decided by sprint finish on uphill under viaduct. 2024 edition also finished with sprint from group of four on steep finish climb. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/french-riders-win-on-homeground-in-wish-one-gravel-grands-causses/ and https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/sprint-victories-for-schiff-and-dauphin-at-the-wish-one-gravel/)

**Critical Requirement [UNKNOWN]:**
"Technique à toute épreuve et condition physique solide sont de mise" (Strong technique and solid physical condition required). The course is described as difficult despite its scenic charm. (https://www.millau.fr/loisirs/les-grands-evenements/wish-one-millau-grands-causses)

## Community Feel & Atmosphere

**Event Growth:**
- First UCI Gravel event on European soil in 2022
- Grew from initial edition to nearly 900 riders by 2024
- 2025 reorganized to single day (Sunday only) "offering a more friendly and intense atmosphere between the riders"
(https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/millau/ and https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/sprint-victories-for-schiff-and-dauphin-at-the-wish-one-gravel/ and https://wishonegravelrace.com/en/accueil-english/)

**Notable Participants:**
2024 edition welcomed former F1 world champion Alain Prost and cycling legend Laurent Jalabert alongside elite racers. (https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/sprint-victories-for-schiff-and-dauphin-at-the-wish-one-gravel/)

**Location Appeal:**
Event showcases beauty of Millau and surroundings in Grands Causses Regional Natural Park (UNESCO world heritage site). Finish under iconic Millau Viaduct creates memorable backdrop. The varied terrain and stunning landscapes are consistently emphasized across sources. (https://www.granfondoguide.com/Contents/PopUp/8587/france-prevails-as-prevot-and-drechou-take-uci-wish-one-millau-gran-causses-titles)

**Race Format:**
Multiple distance options for different levels:
- UCI 130km (qualifier for World Championships)
- UCI 80km (for Women 50+/Men 60+)
- Open 80km
- Open 40km
Qualification system: Top 25% of category qualify for UCI Gravel World Championships, plus top 3 finishers regardless of field size.
(https://wishonegravelrace.com/en/accueil-english/ and https://strambecco.com/event/wish-one-millau-grands-causses/)

**Regional Tourism:**
Area offers Gorges du Tarn, medieval villages (Peyre, Conques), Grands Causses Regional Natural Park for post-race activities. Nearest airports: Rodez (75km), Montpellier (122km), Toulouse (208km). (https://www.finishers.com/en/event/wish-one-millau-grands-causses and https://ucigravelworldseries.com/en/millau/)
