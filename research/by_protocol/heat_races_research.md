## Research Focus

For heat protocol races, research:
- Average/max temperatures
- Heat index during race
- Hydration requirements
- Heat training recommendations
- Time of day (start times)
- Shade availability on course

## Research Sources

- Official race websites
- Weather data for race dates
- Race course descriptions
- Heat training resources
