## Research Workflow

1. **Start with Priority 10 races** (4 races)
2. **Then Priority 9 races** (4 races)
3. **Then zero-competition races** (265 races)
4. **Then regional series** (complete series data)
5. **Then UCI events** (33 events)
6. **Then remaining races** (fill gaps)
