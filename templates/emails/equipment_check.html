Equipment Check

6 Weeks to {race_name}

Hey {athlete_name},

Six weeks out from {race_name}. Now is the time to sort your gear — not race week.

Bike

  • Tires: Check tread wear. If you're running tubeless, refresh your sealant (it dries out after ~3 months).
  • Chain: Check for stretch. Replace if needed — a new chain is cheaper than a new cassette.
  • Brakes: Check pad wear. Bleed hydraulic brakes if spongy.
  • Drivetrain: Clean and lube. Shift through all gears. Fix any skipping now.
  • Fit: If anything hurts on long rides, address it now. Not the week before.

Race Day Kit

  • Nutrition: Practice your race nutrition on long rides. No experiments on race day.
  • Bottles/hydration: Test your bottle setup at race pace.
  • Repair kit: Spare tube, CO2, tire lever, multi-tool. Test your CO2 head.
  • Clothing: Wear what you'll race in on at least one long ride.

The goal: zero surprises on race day.
