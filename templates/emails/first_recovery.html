Recovery Week

Trust the Process

Hey {athlete_name},

This is your first recovery week. It's supposed to feel easy. That's the point.

Why Recovery Weeks Matter

Your body doesn't get stronger during workouts — it gets stronger during recovery. The hard sessions from the past weeks created the stimulus. This week is when the adaptation actually happens.

What to Do

  • Follow the plan exactly. No bonus rides.
  • Sleep 8+ hours. This is when growth hormone peaks.
  • Eat well. Your body is rebuilding — give it fuel.
  • Light mobility work. Foam rolling, stretching, easy walks.

What NOT to Do

  • Do NOT add intensity because you "feel good"
  • Do NOT extend the easy rides
  • Do NOT feel guilty about resting

You'll come back stronger next week. That's how this works.
