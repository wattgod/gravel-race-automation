You have an FTP test scheduled this week. Here's how to nail it.

Before the Test

  • Rest the day before. No hard efforts. Easy spin or full rest.
  • Eat normally. Don't try anything new. Familiar foods, adequate carbs.
  • Hydrate. Start the day well-hydrated.
  • Calibrate your power meter or smart trainer before you start.

During the Test

  • Warm up for 15 minutes with progressive effort.
  • Start conservative. The biggest mistake is going too hard in the first 5 minutes.
  • Pace evenly. Aim for a consistent effort you can barely sustain for 20 minutes.
  • The last 5 minutes should hurt. If they don't, you started too easy.

After the Test

Your new FTP = average power for the 20-minute effort x 0.95. Update your zones in your training app so all future workouts are calibrated correctly.

Good luck. You're stronger than you think.
