The #1 Rule of Race Month

Do NOT try to cram in extra fitness. Any fitness gained now won't be available on race day. What WILL help: rest, nutrition, and mental prep.

Race Month Checklist

  • Finalize equipment — no new gear on race day
  • Practice race nutrition on long rides (same brands, same quantities)
  • Review the course profile and plan your pacing strategy
  • Book travel/accommodation if needed
  • Check bike maintenance (tires, chain, brakes, tubeless sealant)

Volume Taper

You'll notice the plan is reducing volume. This is intentional. Your body needs 2-3 weeks to absorb the training load and arrive at the start line fresh.

Trust the taper. You're ready.
