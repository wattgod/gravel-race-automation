This is it. Race week for {race_name}.

Day-by-Day Plan

  • Monday-Tuesday: Easy spin (30-45 min max). Zone 1-2 only.
  • Wednesday: 30 min easy + 2x30 sec openers at race pace. Shake the legs out.
  • Thursday: Complete rest or 20 min easy walk.
  • Friday: Complete rest. Pack your gear. Prep nutrition.
  • Saturday (if race is Sunday): 20 min shakeout ride. 2 openers. That's it.

Nutrition Loading

  • Increase carbs starting 2 days before the race
  • Aim for 8-10g carbs per kg bodyweight on the day before
  • Hydrate well but don't overdo it (clear urine = good)
  • Race morning: familiar breakfast 3 hours before start

Equipment Check

  • Tires: proper pressure, check tubeless sealant
  • Chain: clean and lubed
  • Bottles/nutrition: packed and ready
  • Clothing: weather-appropriate, nothing new
  • Electronics: charged (computer, lights, phone)

You've done the work. Now go execute.
