The Science

Training creates micro-damage. Recovery is when your body repairs and comes back stronger. Skip the recovery and you just accumulate fatigue without adaptation. That's how injuries happen.

This Week

  • Follow the plan. Easy rides only. No intensity.
  • Sleep. 8+ hours. This is your most important training tool this week.
  • Eat well. Your body is rebuilding — don't restrict calories.
  • Move gently. Light stretching, foam rolling, easy walks.

You'll feel restless. That's normal. Trust the process — the hard work starts again next week.
