Do I need a power meter? +

Not required. Every workout includes RPE targets so you can train by feel. But a power meter is strongly recommended — watt targets remove guesswork entirely. Heart rate works as a middle ground. The plan adapts to whatever you measure with, but power is better.

What if I don't know my FTP? +

Most people don't. Mark it unknown. Week 1 includes an FTP test protocol. Once you have the number, every zone recalibrates.

How are workouts delivered? +

.zwo files. Load directly into Zwift, TrainingPeaks, or anything that reads the format. Each file has power targets, cadence cues, and coaching text built in. Your guide is a web page — bookmark it.

Can I get a plan for multiple races? +

Yes. Add up to 10 races with A/B/C priority. The plan periodizes around your A-race. B-races are secondary peaks. C-races are training races.

What if my race isn't in the database? +

The training still works. You won't get race-specific intel (suffering zones, terrain breakdown), but the workouts, phases, and structure are identical.

Is this coaching? +

No. This is a plan, not a relationship. You get the full plan up front and execute it yourself. No weekly check-ins. For ongoing coaching with adjustments and communication — that's a separate service.
